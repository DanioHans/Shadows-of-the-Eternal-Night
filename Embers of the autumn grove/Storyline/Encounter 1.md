### **Encounter 1: The Corrupted Advance**

**Objective:** Introduce the players to **basic D&D combat mechanics** in a **low-risk but engaging fight**.

---

### **Encounter Setup**

After Nyssa’s plea, the players follow the sounds of battle deeper into the **Autumn Glade**.

- **The air is thick with smoke and heat.** Trees burn with unnatural fire, and the once-vibrant foliage has turned **ashen and cracked**.
- **Ahead, the battle is already underway.** Three **Verdant Wardens** are locked in combat with twisted fey—**Blighted Revenants** and a lone **Blighted Branchwych**, who appears to be **directing the creatures** with fiery incantations.

Nyssa remains at a safe distance, calling out:  
_"These creatures were once protectors, but the fire has hollowed them out. Aid the Verdant Wardens—push them back before they spread further!"_

---

### **Enemies**

- **1 Blighted Branchwych (Spellcaster Support)**
- **4 Blighted Revenants (Melee Threats, Focus on Pressuring Players & Wardens)**

### **Allies**

- **3 Verdant Wardens (Tank/Damage Dealers, Reinforce the Players)**

### **Includes**
- Verdant warden knocks down branch, showing environmental hazards
- Damage reduction reaction
