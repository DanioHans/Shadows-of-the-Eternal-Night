**Type**: River village & ferry stop (~320 people).  
**Location**: South of Caer Hollow, at the Silverflow crossing. Only ferry/ford before Duskmire.  
**Importance**: All shipments bound for Duskwatch or Caer Hollow pass through here.  

---

## Layout
- **Rope Ferry Dock** (main crossing).  
- **Village Green** with well, shrine, and small market.  
- **The Swaying Reed (inn/tavern)**, common stop for merchants.  
- **Reeve’s House**: village leadership.  
- **Storehouse & Boat Sheds**: goods awaiting transport.  

---

## NPCs
- **Reeve Marla Fenwright**: pragmatic, protective of villagers. Keeps trade moving.  
- **Saja the Ferrymaster**: rope-burned hands, keeps detailed ferry logs. Will sell access for 2 gp “clerical fee.”  
- **Brother Deren**: shrine caretaker, nervous. Claims prayers “don’t land” anymore.  
- **Halvar Pike (Hunter)**: insists he saw lanterns moving *under* the river fog.  

---

## Ferry
- Fee: **5 sp per rider / 1 gp per wagon**.  
- Logs:  
  - Moonshade Moss: never logged (never left Duskwatch).  
  - Ironwood Timber: Day 9, crossed here.  
  - Salted Fish/Grain: Day 11, loaded but “held for final tally” → stuck at Duskwatch storehouse.  
  - Silver Ore: Day 10, never logged → confirms disappearance outside Duskwatch.  
  - Lantern Oil: Day 8, recorded by Salen Kett at border post.  

---

## Rumors
1. Shrine candles upriver snuff out all at once.  
2. Duskwatch fishers stare through you, never blink.  
3. Lantern oil never reached Caer Hollow; Salen Kett vanished.  
4. Reeds whisper names after sundown.  
5. “Someone rich” (High Ring?) keeps asking about rune-marked animals.  

---

## Hooks
- PCs can help repair the ferry rope (Survival/Tools) → earns goodwill & free crossing.  
- Rescue a child found sleepwalking toward the river (“following mother’s voice”).  
- Bandits in the area may be fleeing villagers; negotiation or fight.
