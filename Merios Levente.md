## 🧬 Species & Lineage

- **Base Race**: Tiefling (Charisma +2, Darkvision, Fire Resistance)
- **Custom Features**:
  - **Charm (1/day)**  
    DC 15 Wisdom save or one humanoid is charmed for 1 hour. Ends on damage. One target at a time.
  - **Draining Kiss (1/day)**  
    Melee spell attack vs. charmed/willing target. DC 15 Con save or 4d10 psychic damage + HP max reduction until long rest. Half damage on success.

> These reflect her succubus ancestry and are treated as innate, limited-use abilities. She may gain additional uses or dark evolutions depending on corruption path.

## ⚔️ Weapon: *Sword of Screams* (Magic Item)

- **+1 longsword**, no additional damage.
- **Frighten Pulse**:  
  Once per long rest, when drawn, creatures within 10 ft. make a **DC 13 Wis save** or are frightened until end of next turn.
- **Corruption Save**:  
  Each long rest, make a **DC 13 Wisdom saving throw**. On a fail, Merios gains:
  - 1 hidden **Corruption Point**  
  - OR suffers subtle mental effects (DM decides).

> The sword may "whisper" to her, encourage cruel solutions, or resist radiant influence. This is a slow-burn corruption arc.

## 🧠 Corruption Hooks

Merios is **susceptible to corruption** via:

- Repeated use of succubus abilities (if used recklessly or for gain).
- Sword of Screams (already active).
- Entering Shadowfell regions or convergence zones.
- Prolonged denial of divine connection.

Effects may begin subtly: dreams, voice shifts, mood swings. Later stages might include:

- Visible changes (horns, aura, voice).
- Charm-like aura she can’t control.
- Temptations to switch allegiance or embrace demonic strength.
