### Detailed Description of Elara, the Village Elder

**Name:** Elara Silverleaf

**Appearance:**

- **Age:** Late 60s
- **Height:** 5'5"
- **Build:** Slender but sturdy, with a presence that exudes both frailty and hidden strength.
- **Hair:** Long, silver hair usually tied back in a loose braid. Strands of her hair have become wispy and delicate over the years, framing her face softly.
- **Eyes:** Deep blue, sharp and perceptive, with a hint of sorrow reflecting the burdens she carries.
- **Skin:** Fair but weathered, with fine lines that speak to a life of worry and care.
- **Attire:** Elara typically wears a long, dark green cloak made of heavy wool, lined with fur to ward off the chill. Underneath, she wears simple, earth-toned dresses with intricate embroidery around the cuffs and neckline, suggesting a mix of practicality and a lingering touch of elegance from her younger days. She also wears a silver pendant shaped like a leaf, a symbol of her connection to nature and the village's heritage.

**Personality:**

- **Wise and Knowledgeable:** Elara possesses a deep understanding of Shadowbrook’s history, the forest, and the dark forces at play. Her wisdom is sought by the villagers in times of trouble.
- **Compassionate:** She is kind-hearted and deeply cares for the well-being of her fellow villagers. She treats everyone with respect and empathy, often putting their needs above her own.
- **Resilient:** Despite her age and the mounting troubles, Elara remains resolute and determined. She has faced many hardships but continues to lead with strength and hope.
- **Perceptive:** Elara has a keen eye for detail and can often sense when something is amiss. Her sharp mind allows her to piece together clues and see through deceptions.
- **Gravitas:** Her presence commands respect, and even the most skeptical of villagers listen when she speaks. Her voice carries a soothing, melodic tone that can calm fears and instill confidence.

**Background:**

- **Heritage:** Elara comes from a long line of village elders who have served Shadowbrook for generations. Her family has always been seen as the guiding light for the community.
- **Personal History:** Elara grew up in Shadowbrook, learning the ways of the forest and the responsibilities of leadership from her parents. She became the village elder after her father passed away and has led the village through several crises.
- **Connection to Magic:** While not a practitioner of magic herself, Elara has a deep understanding of natural and arcane lore. She has worked with mages and healers in the past, accumulating a vast repository of knowledge.
- **Family:** Elara's husband, Aric, passed away several years ago, and her two children have grown and moved to other villages. She remains devoted to the people of Shadowbrook, seeing them as her extended family.

**Role in the Story:**

- **Guide and Informant:** Elara is the primary quest-giver, providing the adventurers with crucial information about the disappearances, the undead sightings, and the history of the village.
- **Support:** She offers support in the form of maps, historical documents, and personal insights. She might also provide minor healing or blessings to aid the adventurers.
- **Moral Compass:** Elara serves as the moral center of the story, reminding the adventurers of the human cost of the necromancer's actions and urging them to act with compassion and justice.

**Key Interactions:**

- **Initial Meeting:** Elara approaches the adventurers as they arrive in Shadowbrook, welcoming them and explaining the village's plight.
- **Providing Clues:** She shares maps and old records, points out key locations for investigation, and deciphers any strange symbols or runes they find.
- **Emotional Moments:** Elara might show moments of vulnerability, sharing stories of lost villagers, her late husband, or her worries for the future. These moments can deepen the players' emotional investment in the story.
- **Aftermath:** Upon the adventurers' return, Elara leads the village in a celebration of their victory. She provides further guidance for the continuing campaign, hinting at greater threats looming on the horizon.
