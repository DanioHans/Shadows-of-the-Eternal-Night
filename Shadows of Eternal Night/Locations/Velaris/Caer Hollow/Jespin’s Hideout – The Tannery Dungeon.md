
Jespin Wrent’s hideout lies beneath an old, abandoned tannery in Smokestack Row—"where leather rots." The building is barely standing, reeking of ancient chemicals, and long forgotten by the public. Beneath it: his stash, his secrets, and maybe more.

---

## 📍 Entry and Layout

**Surface Structure**:  
A dilapidated single-floor tannery, with broken roof beams and mold-coated vats. A rusted trapdoor is hidden beneath an old drying rack.

**Trapdoor Trap (new)**:  
- **Trigger**: Picking the lock or breaking the trapdoor open without investigation  
- **Trap**: Alchemical fume trap — a pressurized vial releases a potent noxious gas  
- **Effect**: CON save DC 13 → On fail: 1d6 poison damage and **Disoriented** (see below) for 10 minutes  
- **Disarm**: Investigation DC 13 to notice it; Thieves’ Tools DC 14 to disable  
- **Narrative Hint**: "You hear a faint hiss and a chemical smell creeps up before the trapdoor even creaks open."

---

## 🤢 The Stench Effect (Updated)

The dungeon air is putrid, the scent of rotting leather, decay, and chemical runoff hangs thick.

- **Effect**: On first descent, CON save DC 12  
    - **Fail**: Gain the **Disoriented** condition for 1 hour  
    - **Disoriented**: -1 to concentration checks, Wisdom (Insight/Investigation), and Intelligence (Arcana/Nature)  
    - **Immune**: Scrap (Warforged), Queek (Skaven)

**Possible Mitigation Tools** (if players prepared well or searched):  
- *Damp herbal cloths* found in the upper floor (Survival/Investigation DC 13)  
- *Perfumed rags* sold earlier at the market (if purchased)  
- *Samson’s Stoneskin Potion* might protect briefly as a stretch ruling

## 🗺️ Layout Overview

1. **Main Room (Descent Point)**  
   - Dim alchemical lighting  
   - Open crates of smuggled goods  
   - Alchemical refuse on the floor  
   - Hidden tripwire (DC 13 Perception) triggers **Guard Beast release**

2. **Caged Storage Room**  
   - Locked wooden cage (DC 14) with remains of strange creatures  
   - Among them, **a Basilisk skull missing both eyes**

3. **Sleeping Niche**  
   - Hammock, logbook with coded entries (DC 15 to decipher → smuggling routes & payments)  
   - *Arcane residue* on a broken summoning circle (DC 15 Arcana)

4. **Side Alcove**  
   - Forgotten documents partially buried in dust:  
     - Mentions of strange purchases in Duskwatch  
     - Shipment logs with *unusual glyphs* matching Shadowfell magic (very obscure)
     - Notes on “the veiled merchant” (unseen NPC lead for future)

## 🐍 Combat Encounter – Smuggled Horrors

If the **tripwire** is triggered or the party is too loud:

- **Encounter**:  
  2x *Needlefang Crawlers* (CR 1/4) + 1x *Giant Centipede* (CR 1/4) + 1x *Basilisk Hatchling* (custom creature, CR 1/2)

- **Basilisk Hatchling (Homebrew)**  
  - HP: 30, AC: 13  
  - Bite: 1d8 + 2  
  - Gaze: Once per short rest, target makes CON save DC 12 or is Restrained for 1 round (not petrified)

- **Triggering**:  
  - Loud noises  
  - Opening certain crates  
  - Tripwire

## 🔍 Secret Lore Clues

- **Coded Ledger**: Implicates certain “noble” buyers in Caer Hollow funding strange animal imports (DC 17 to decode)  
- **Shadowfell Runes**: Players who know deep magic or have read planar texts can recognize a link (Arcana DC 16)  
- **Old Tattered Journal Page**: “Duskwatch has gone quiet. Even birds avoid the skies.”  
- **Dead Rat with Scarred Sigil**: The same markings as found in the cults (could tie into later Greyspite Cult plots)

## ⛓️ Exit and Wrap-Up

- **Jespin is here**, either trying to sneak out or bargaining for mercy  
- If caught:
    - Tries to talk his way out
    - Offers names, maps, or secrets in exchange for leniency
- **Capturing him alive** is preferred by Reno & the Crownwatch

---

## 🎲 DM Tips

- Let the clues build tension; even if they find him early, the environment reveals how deep things go.
- Use the **stench** and the **trapdoor trap** to slow them down without frustration.
- Optional: Jespin sets off a small blast to collapse the tunnel behind him if he escapes, leading to a **chase scene**.
