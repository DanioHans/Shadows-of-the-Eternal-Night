**Race:** Human  
**Class:** Paladin of Selûne  
**Role:** Former hunter of Merios, potential ally or antagonist  

## Overview
Gawain Brightlance is a renowned Selûnite paladin known for his unwavering devotion, martial prowess, and deep tactical wisdom. Once hailed as a hero for halting a fiendish invasion, his most legendary feat was defeating and then redeeming the succubus general Merios — a task entrusted to him directly by Selûne herself.

A veteran of countless planar incursions, Gawain is respected among divine circles but lives far from the glory of court life. His past is defined by duty, but his future remains unwritten.

## Key History
- Led a coalition to stop the Host of Lust from establishing a foothold at Thundertree.
- Defeated Merios twice: once in war, and once in a secluded duel that resulted in a divine vision from Selûne ordering her rehabilitation.
- Personally oversaw Merios’ redemption, delivering her into the care of Damon and Silas Celyn.
- Since then, Gawain has lived a more reclusive life — occasionally visiting Merios and corresponding with the Selûnite temple hierarchy.

## 🌘 Possible Future Paths

### Option A — **Ally in the Shadowfell**
- Gawain becomes a critical NPC in Act II or III, assisting the party within the Shadowfell or near its borders.
- Acts as a spiritual compass and powerful martial presence.
- May guide the party to lost Selûnite temples or help them resist creeping corruption.
- Still believes in Merios' redemption and sees the party as key to preventing a broader divine schism.

### Option B — **Turned to Nagash**
- Corrupted by exposure to the Shadowfell or by Selûne's silence in dark times.
- Believes Selûne has abandoned him or now supports Nagash’s plan of “eternal balance through undeath.”
- Gawain may have been visited by a false vision or turned by divine betrayal.
- Could become a recurring foe — a tragic and dangerous enemy with insight into party weaknesses.

## Personality
- Calm, patient, and noble — but not immune to disillusionment.
- Holds Merios’ redemption as one of his greatest tests and victories.
- Believes deeply in purpose, honor, and cosmic balance.
- May clash with party members who defy divine authority or mock faith.

## Roleplay Notes
- Frequently speaks in measured, thoughtful tones.
- Uses metaphors of moonlight, reflection, and tides.
- Tends to watch others in silence before acting or speaking.

## Possible Connections
- **Merios**: His most complicated connection; part ward, part penance.
- **The Royal Arcanum**: Respected but wary of arcane meddling in divine affairs.
- **Crownwatch**: Might be known in higher military or divine command circles.
- **Nagash (secretly)**: A looming influence if he is to fall.
