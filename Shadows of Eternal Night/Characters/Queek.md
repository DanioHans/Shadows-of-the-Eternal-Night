# 🧷 Character: Queek

## Overview
- **Race**: Skaven (reskinned Goblin, Volo’s)
- **Class**: Fighter (Champion) 4
- **Background**: Custom – Exiled Warlord
- **Player**: 

## Personality
- Brutal but honorable in his own twisted way  
- Fierce sense of ambition and desire for recognition  
- Constantly torn between Skaven instincts and newfound heroic ideals  
- Speaks in third person, often boasts of his deeds and future greatness

## Backstory
Queek Headtaker was once one of Clan Mors’ most feared Warlords, commanding legions beneath the streets of Skavenblight. During a climactic battle against the Ubersreik 5 and forces of Chaos, the arcane Skittergate collapsed, hurling Queek and a few surviving loyalists into a new world.

Now stranded in this unfamiliar realm, Queek has chosen a radical new path: to become a **hero** — not just among Skaven, but to all races who once saw his kind as nothing more than vermin. With an eye toward redemption and glory, Queek seeks to redefine what it means to be Skaven. 

Despite these lofty goals, he still yearns to return home — to reclaim his lost power, rebuild his shattered swarm, and perhaps one day rule Skavenblight anew… but as a hero, not just a tyrant.

## Goals
- Prove Skaven can be heroic and noble (in his own way)
- Earn recognition and respect from the “surface dwellers”
- Reclaim glory and rebuild his status among Skaven
- One day return to Skavenblight as a living legend

## Hooks
- Skaven magic or whispers of a broken gate may compel him
- Tension between feral instincts and heroic ambitions
- NPCs may react with fear, curiosity, or hostility to his appearance
- Unresolved ties to the chaos of his homeland

## Notes
- Queek’s skaven traits use Goblin stats as a base (from Volo’s Guide)
- He is treated with suspicion in most cities and by most guards
- Queek will likely be tested early on by the party’s moral compass
