**Race:** Human  
**Class:** Sorcerer  
**Affiliation:** Celyn Farmstead  
**Role:** Monsterologist, Co-guardian of Merios  

## Overview
Damon is one half of the Celyn brothers — renowned sorcerers and monster researchers who live and work on a quiet farmstead outside of Caer Hollow. Practical, curious, and unusually empathetic for someone in his field, Damon helped rehabilitate the succubus Merios alongside his brother Silas and paladin Gawain Brightlance.

Damon specializes in **fiend anatomy and infernal behavior**, and often takes a softer, more curious approach than his brother. He is the one who first noticed Merios’ preference for sweet food (notably mangos) and uses this insight to help reinforce positive behavior.

## Key Traits
- Patient and warm, though blunt when stressed.
- Deeply inquisitive and always taking notes.
- Maintains a cautious optimism about rehabilitating fiends.

## Relationship with Merios
- Initially skeptical, but grew to believe in her potential.
- Acts as an emotional bridge between Silas’ clinical observations and Gawain’s divine duty.
- Frequently accompanies her to Caer Hollow during supervised visits.

## Possible Use
- A point of contact if Merios becomes emotionally unstable or needs guidance.
- Potential quest-giver or magical expert regarding infernal magic or planar travel.
