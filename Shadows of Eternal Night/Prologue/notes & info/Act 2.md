#### Preparation and Departure

**Setting the Scene:**

- After gathering clues from their investigations, the adventurers prepare to venture into the [[Darkwood Forest]] to uncover the source of the undead threat.

**Provisioning:**

- [[Elara]] provides the adventurers with supplies for their journey, including:
    - Rations
    - Healing potions (2 per character)
    - A detailed map of the forest area leading to the ancient ruins
    - A small enchanted stone that glows faintly when undead are nearby (for detecting ambushes at night)

---

#### 2. The Journey Begins

**Forest Exploration:**

- Describe the eerie atmosphere of the [[Darkwood Forest]] as the adventurers navigate through dense underbrush and twisted paths. The forest is silent, with only the occasional rustle of leaves and distant animal calls breaking the silence.

---

#### 3. The River Crossing

**Scene Description:**

- The adventurers come across a fast-moving river with a fragile, ancient rope bridge swaying precariously over it.

**Challenge:**

- **Crossing the Bridge:** The bridge is old and worn. Characters must make a series of skill checks to cross safely:
    - **Dexterity (Acrobatics) Check:** DC 12 to maintain balance.
    - **Strength (Athletics) Check:** DC 10 to help stabilize the bridge for others if they choose to assist.

**Complications:**

- **Failed Checks:** If a character fails a check, they slip and must make a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw to catch themselves. On a failure, they fall into the river and must swim to shore (DC 10 Strength (Athletics) check).
- **Broken Planks:** After two characters cross, one of the planks breaks, making it more difficult (DC 14 checks) for the remaining characters.

---

#### 4. Setting Up Camp

**Scene Description:**

- As night falls, the adventurers find a small clearing suitable for setting up camp. The dense trees and thick underbrush provide some cover, but the forest feels alive with hidden eyes watching.

**Camp Activities:**

- Describe the characters setting up tents, starting a fire, and preparing their evening meal. Encourage role-playing and interactions among the characters.

**Watch Schedule:**

- The adventurers decide on a watch schedule for the night.

---

#### 5. The Ambush

**Ambush Trigger:**

- **Detection:** The enchanted stone given by [[Elara]] glows faintly, warning the characters of approaching danger.
- **Ambush:** A group of 2 [[Skeleton|Skeleton Swordsman]] and 1 [[Skeleton|Skeleton Bowman]] attacks during the second watch of the night.

- **Clues and Warnings:**
    
    - **Enchanted Stone:** Glows faintly when undead are nearby.
    - **Perception Checks:** DC 12 and DC 15 to hear or see signs of undead.
    - **Survival Checks:** DC 12 and DC 15 to set up defensive measures.
    - **Nature Checks:** DC 12 to recognize signs of undead activity.
- **Avoiding the Ambush:**
    
    - Detecting the undead allows the party to prepare and potentially avoid the ambush.
    - Success in checks leads to preemptive actions and gaining the upper hand.
- **Role-Playing Opportunities:**
    
    - Encourage interaction during watch shifts.
    - Provide hints or clues if they are paying attention.
- **Consequences:**
    
    - Reward successful detection with a clue to the puzzle.
    - Proceed with combat if they fail to detect the ambush.

**Combat Encounter:**

- **Objective:** Defend the camp and defeat the ambushing skeletons.
- **Tactics:** The skeletons try to take advantage of the element of surprise, aiming to disrupt the camp and catch the adventurers off guard.

**Resolution:**

- After the battle, the adventurers can search the area to find clues (old bones, remnants of necromantic magic) suggesting that more undead might be lurking in the forest.

---

#### 6. The Ruins

**Scene Description:**

- The next day, the adventurers follow their map and the clues they’ve gathered to the ancient ruins deep within the forest. The ruins are overgrown with vines and moss, with crumbling stone structures hinting at a long-forgotten civilization.

**Exploration:**

- As they explore the ruins, the adventurers encounter signs of past battles and dark rituals, with ancient carvings and symbols adorning the walls.

**Encounter: The Corpse Flower**

- **Monster:** A gnarly [[Corpse Flower (Nerfed)]]
- **Scene:** The adventurers stumble upon the Corpse Flower feeding on the remains of fallen adventurers or villagers, surrounded by a few animated zombies.

**Combat Encounter:**

- **Objective:** Defeat the Corpse Flower and its minions.
- **Tactics:** The Corpse Flower uses its tentacles to lash out at the adventurers while animating zombies to aid it in battle.
- **Enemies:**
    - 1 Corpse Flower
    - 2 Zombies (animated by the Corpse Flower)

**Resolution:**

- After defeating the Corpse Flower, the adventurers find a hidden entrance beneath the ruins leading to a dungeon. This entrance marks the transition to Act 3, where they will confront the necromancer.
