## **Phase 1: The Burning Guardian** _(Start of the Fight)_

Vaelthir is still partially resisting the corruption, but Emberblight weakens his control. The battlefield contains **corrupted roots**, binding him to the Emberblight’s influence.

### **Additional Mechanics in Phase 1**

#### **1. Corrupted Roots (Weaken Vaelthir Through the Environment)**

- **Corrupt roots** are spread across the battlefield, acting as conduits for Emberblight.
- Each root can be **targeted and destroyed** (**AC 12, HP 10**).

#### **2. Stagger Bar Mechanic (Soft Stagger)**

- **Each time a corrupted root is destroyed**, Vaelthir reacts, momentarily losing focus.
- If players destroy **4 roots**, he enters **Phase 2 early**, preventing him from reaching full power.
- Destroying **all roots** **before Phase 2** causes an **early stagger**, making his final phase easier.

---

## **Phase 2: The Inferno Awakens** _(Triggered at 50% HP OR if enough roots are destroyed)_

Vaelthir fully succumbs to Emberblight, his form wreathed in **uncontrollable flame**.

### **Additional Mechanics in Phase 2**

#### **1. Corrupting Surge (Upon Phase 2 Activation)**

- Vaelthir **absorbs remaining roots**, strengthening Emberblight.
- All creatures within **20 feet** take **1d6 fire damage per root left** and must make a **DC 15 Dexterity/Strength saving throw** or be knocked prone.

#### **2. Root Purification (Weaken Vaelthir’s Final Form)**

- Players **can still remove corruption** by **targeting or interacting with weakened roots**.
- **Roots now regenerate**, but can be permanently removed by:
    - **Destroying them directly** (AC 12, HP 10).
    - **Burning them with radiant energy or Fey magic.**
    - **Using Verdant Warden energy to cleanse them.**
- If players **remove 2 more roots**, Vaelthir’s **Phase 2 fire aura weakens** (2 instead of 4 fire damage).
- If **all corrupted roots are destroyed**, Vaelthir is **staggered early**, setting up the final confrontation.
- Respawn 1 root per round, during Lair Action

---

## **Final Stagger: The Guardian’s Last Stand** _(Triggered by reducing Vaelthir to 0 HP OR removing all corruption)_

Vaelthir collapses, his **burning core exposed**. This is the final opportunity to **end the fight or save him**.

### **Additional Mechanics in the Stagger Phase**

#### **1. Stagger Timer (Players Have One Round to Act)**

- Players have **1 full round** to **either cleanse or destroy Vaelthir**.
- **If they do not act in time, he rises again, continuing Phase 2.**

#### **2. Cleansing Ritual (Save Vaelthir)**

- **To cleanse Vaelthir, players must channel enough restorative energy into his core.**
- Any combination of the following actions can **contribute to the cleansing**:
    - Casting **healing magic or radiant damage** at his core.
    - Using **water, druidic, or Fey-based magic** to counteract Emberblight.
    - Calling on the **Verdant Wardens** to aid in the ritual.
- If players **succeed**, Vaelthir is **freed from Emberblight**, restoring balance to the glade.

#### **3. Destruction Condition (End Him Permanently)**

- **To destroy Vaelthir, players must deal a total of 50 damage** in the **Stagger Phase**.
- **His HP does not matter anymore**—if they fail to deal 50 damage, he **rises again**, continuing Phase 2.
- **If they succeed**, he is permanently destroyed, ending the corruption but leaving the glade unprotected.

#### **4. The Final Choice**

- If players **cleanse Vaelthir**, the **Autumn Glade is restored**, but some risk of Emberblight remains.
- If players **destroy him**, the **corruption is wiped out**, but the **glade loses its guardian**.
