### **Verdant Warden**

_Medium Fey (Guardian), Neutral_  
These once-silent protectors of the **Autumn Glade** stand as the last line of defense against the **Emberblight’s corruption**. Their bark-like skin is cracked and scorched, but their resolve remains unshaken.

---

**AC**: 16 (Natural Armor)  
**HP**: 55 (7d8 + 21)  
**Speed**: 30 ft.

---

### **Stats**

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|18|12|16|10|14|10|

**Saving Throws**: Str +6, Wis +4  
**Skills**: Athletics +6, Perception +4  
**Damage Resistances**: Fire, Bludgeoning  
**Condition Immunities**: Charmed, Frightened  
**Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 14  
**Languages**: Sylvan, understands Common

---

### **Abilities**

#### **Guardian’s Resolve (1/Short Rest)**

When an ally within **10 feet** takes damage, the Warden can use its reaction to **halve the damage taken**.

#### **Healing Tree Teleportation**

As a **bonus action**, the Warden can teleport up to **30 feet** to a **Healing Tree Zone** within line of sight.

#### **Magic Weapons**

The Warden’s weapon attacks are considered **magical**.

---

### **Actions**

#### **Greatsword (Melee Weapon Attack)**

- **Attack:** +6 to hit
- **Reach:** 5 ft.
- **Hit:** **2d6 + 4 slashing damage** (avg. 11).

#### **Thorn Volley (Recharge 5–6)**

- The Warden conjures a flurry of razor-sharp thorns.
- **Range:** 30 feet (cone).
- **Effect:** All creatures in the area must make a **DC 13 Dexterity saving throw**.
    - **On Failure:** **2d6 piercing damage**.
    - **On Success:** Half damage.
