
**Location**: [[The High Ring]], [[Caer Hollow]]  
**Purpose**: Guest quarter and diplomatic barracks for esteemed visitors and adventuring agents of the throne.

---

## Overview

Crownwatch Bastion is a fortified guest keep positioned in the High Ring of [[Caer Hollow]], adjacent to the royal palace but distinctly separate. It represents the crown’s trust in independent agents without granting full integration into noble or arcane circles. Though formally neutral, its walls whisper with quiet observation.

The Bastion is not luxurious — it is dignified. Reserved for adventurers, diplomats, or other "trusted outsiders," its function is both practical and political.

During Act I, the party is offered temporary residence here, a reward for their actions during the [[Session 1 - Market Day Disruption]].

---

## Structure and Facilities

- **Main Hall** – Public receiving area, decorated with Tharion’s banners and a central firepit. Two guards posted at all times.
- **Private Quarters** – Each PC has a personal room. Walls are embedded with low-tier alarm wards and soundproofing glyphs.
- **Messenger's Tower** – Connects to the palace's mundane scroll network. Magical messaging restricted to palace authority.
- **Watch Garden** – A tranquil, high-walled courtyard with a still reflecting pool. Rumors persist that the water shows hidden figures after midnight.
- **Wardroom** – Armory (locked unless emergency), basic rations, and a shared shrine to the **Dawnmother** and the **Hollow Flame**.

---

## Personnel and Security

- **[[Captain Teron Veyne]]** – Commander of the Crownwatch, maintains direct authority over the Bastion. Stern, authoritative, and politically aligned with the palace.
- **Night Wardens** – Two stationed agents blend with the guard. Their true authority is not widely acknowledged.
- **Palace Whisperer** – One low-ranking staff member (identity unknown) reports directly to [[Verrath]] or another palace figure.
- Curfew: **Guests may not leave after moonrise** without written consent. This rule is enforced quietly but strictly.

---

## Secrets and Anomalies

- A **sealed hallway** extends beneath the Bastion, possibly leading to a forgotten vault or holding cell. Its lock is magical and ancient.
- The **Watch Garden’s pool** distorts reality after midnight — glimpses of people, memories, or alternate selves may appear.
- The **guest ledger** lists individuals recorded as deceased or missing in past decades — dates align with previous planar anomalies.

---

## Function for Players

- Offers:
  - A **safe resting place**
  - Access to official Crownwatch and basic palace resources
  - A central location for regrouping, receiving leads, and storing important items
- Encourages:
  - Subtle political involvement
  - Growing ties with the palace
  - Questions about the **Night Wardens**, the **Hollow Flame**, and the sealed areas beneath Caer Hollow
