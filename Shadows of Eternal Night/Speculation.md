# Shadowfell → Void Transition (Campaign Notes)

## Core Idea

- **Nagash-stand-in** (renamed custom lich-king) is defeated around lvl 9–10.
    
- His death **fractures the Shadowfell**, releasing localized Void corruption.
    
- The **artifact companion** (Xal’atath-style) manifests, revealing itself as a manipulator.
    
- The Shadowfell remains intact overall → Void spreads slowly → room for side arcs before full escalation.
    

---

## Artifact / Void Agent

- **Early form:** Whispering relic (weapon, gem, trinket). Gives subtle hints + minor aid.
    
- **Reveal:** Upon Nagash’s fall, shatters and manifests (seductive figure, hollow armor, fragmented guide, etc).
    
- **Role:**
    
    - Manipulative ally → steers PCs to clear rivals, hunt cults, stabilize Shadowfell.
        
    - Seeds Act III + Act IV by hinting at a greater Hunger.
        
    - Eventually betrays PCs / ascends to become final or penultimate threat.
        

---

## Shadowfell After Nagash’s Death

- **Localized Catastrophe:** Nagash’s fortress becomes **ground zero of Void corruption**.
    
- **Plane Status:** Wider Shadowfell remains stable but scarred. Corruption grows over time.
    
- **Escape Session:**
    
    - Fortress collapses inward (fractals, geometry breaks, undead void-twisted).
        
    - PCs flee through collapsing terrain + hazards (void storms, gravity flips, avatar chase).
        
    - End: exit through rift → meet the manifested artifact.
        

---

## Side Arcs (Post-Nagash, Pre-Void)

- **Wraith-King’s Court:** Rival undead monarch tries to seize power.
    
- **The Bleak March:** Revenants locked in endless war — now rewritten by Void.
    
- **Shadow Markets:** Shadar-kai traders unknowingly sell void-tainted goods.
    
- **Harrowed Fey:** Feywild cracks bleed in; Fey warn of _“the Nameless Hunger.”_
    

---

## Void Footholds in the Shadowfell

- **Wells of Silence:** Kill = no body, no spirit.
    
- **Fractured Geometry:** Bent ruins, repeating hallways.
    
- **Cult Nests:** Shadar-kai + undead influenced by Void whispers.
    

---

## Escalation Plan

1. **Act II End:** Fortress corrupted; Void localized.
    
2. **Act III Mid:** Multiple Void pockets linked; Shadowfell destabilizing.
    
3. **Act IV:** Planes converge (Shadowfell, Feywild, Material). Void threat in full.
    

---

## Thematic Throughline

- **Nagash/Shadow King = Fear of Death.**
    
- **The Void = Fear of Nothingness.**
    
- Defeating Nagash was not victory — it was opening the first lock.
