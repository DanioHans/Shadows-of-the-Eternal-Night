**Level**: 2nd | **School**: Necromancy  
**Casting Time**: 1 Action  
**Range**: 30 feet  
**Duration**: Concentration, up to 1 minute  
**Components**: V, S

**Effect**: Target one creature within range.

- The creature’s **movement speed is halved** for the duration.
- At the start of each of its turns, the target must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failure, it **cannot take reactions** during that turn.

**Flavor**: The target feels their joints lock and their bones tighten as if aged a century. Their limbs tremble with every motion, and the faint shimmer of exposed bone flickers over their skin.
