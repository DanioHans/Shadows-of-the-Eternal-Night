
**Location**: [[The Slums]], Southern Caer Hollow  
**Function**: Industrial husk turned informal residence  

---

## 🏚️ Overview

Smokestack Row is a **blighted, smoke-choked stretch** of rusted chimneys, collapsed kilns, and crumbling textile mills. Once a proud district of Caer Hollow’s industrial age, it now lies broken — a shell of soot and whispers.

Years ago, the Crown banned combustion-based industry within city limits, citing arcane pollution. The Row was never restored, and what remains is now **a haven for squatters, illicit trade, and the desperate**.

---

## 🌫️ Atmosphere

- Air is acrid with the lingering scent of coal and mildew  
- Buildings lean unevenly, patched with tarps and scavenged wood  
- Crows nest in broken smokestacks  
- Steam pipes hiss intermittently despite having no known source  
- Children run messages between unmarked doors

---

## 🧭 Points of Interest

- **Wrenth’s Hideout** *(see: [[Jaspin Wrenth]])*  
  A boarded-up dye house with a rusted back entrance, accessible only via a narrow alley and a steep pipe-ladder. Protected by simple alchemical tripwires.

- **The Iron Maw**  
  A gutted forge converted into a fight den and illegal auction hall. Most know *not* to ask questions here.

- **Smokestack Tenements**  
  Collapsed worker dorms partially rebuilt by squatters. The upper floors sway unnaturally in the wind.

- **The Sulfur Baths**  
  Formerly heated by furnaces, now a fog-dense ruin rumored to house something... watching.

---

## 👥 Locals

- Smugglers, beggars, back-alley alchemists  
- Former workers who refuse to leave despite the danger  
- Children acting as informants or scouts for petty gangs  
- A few quiet souls with mysterious pasts — including one known as *“Nana Clag,”* who trades rumors for dried fish

---

## 🕵️ Clues About Wrenth

- Locals are evasive unless **bribed or charmed**  
- Some recall seeing a crate matching the basilisk’s cage being wheeled into *“the old dye house”*  
- Faint trail of blue-dyed hay and glass shard dust can be spotted on a high Investigation or Survival check
