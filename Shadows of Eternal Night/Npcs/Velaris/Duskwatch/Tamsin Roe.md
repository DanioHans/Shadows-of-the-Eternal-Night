Storehouse Keeper

_Human, broad-shouldered, practical, exhausted_

**Role:** Mundane anchor, supply reality check  
**Tone:** Blunt, grateful, focused on survival

### Background

Tamsin managed shipments during the dream _without knowing it_.  
He wakes to:

- Perfectly stacked crates
    
- No memory of unloading them
    
- A ledger filled out in his handwriting
    

This unsettles him deeply.

---

### Use in Play

- Confirms timeline confusion
    
- Grounds supernatural events in logistics
    
- Reinforces the horror of unconscious functionality
    

> “If we can work without knowing… what else can we do?”
