
## Overview
Lira Ven is a former field arcanist turned independent researcher, known in academic circles for her unconventional theories and combative personality. Once affiliated with the **Royal Arcanum of Velaris**, she left under strained circumstances and now resides in **Caer Hollow**, continuing her work in private. Her expertise lies in planar anomalies, leyline flux, and magical corruption — particularly those originating near the **Verdant Wastes**.

## Appearance
- Mid-30s half-elf with windblown black hair, streaked prematurely with grey
- Wears threadbare mage robes reinforced with alchemical patches and hidden scroll tubes
- Often surrounded by notes, arcane measuring tools, and two sleep-deprived ravens

## Personality
- Brilliant but abrasive — suffers no fools and mocks the slow-witted
- Dismissive of bureaucracy and formal titles
- Blunt, sarcastic, and impatient — but passionate when discussing magical anomalies

## Role in the Campaign
- One of the few in Caer Hollow who **understands the danger** posed by fluctuations from the Verdant Wastes
- May become an **early ally** or a reluctant informant
- Has **suspicions about Verrath** and believes something unnatural is being encouraged in the Arcanum's research curriculum
- Keeps an eye on local magical surges and creature behavior patterns

## Secrets & Hooks
- Was **expelled** from the Royal Arcanum after accusing high-ranking mages of burying research on "Forbidden Leylines"
- Believes **Forgotten Magic** is leaking through the Verdant Wastes — and the Basilisk incident only confirmed her fears
- Keeps **a hidden planar calibration device** tuned to fluctuations beneath Caer Hollow (could be damaged or go haywire during later events)

## Possible Future Use
- Offers cryptic or overly technical insight into magical events
- May need the players to **collect data** or stabilize something she's tracking
- Can be a source of **hidden truths** if players earn her trust — or steal from her
- Potential recruit or target depending on where the party stands in the Verrath plot

## Connections
- **Samson** may know of her — or seek her out after the Basilisk incident
- **Archivist Elira Felwin** considers her dangerous and disgraceful
- Possibly known to Gawain, depending on how far her accusations reached

## Voice & Demeanor
- Quick-spoken and sharp, often mid-thought
- Frequently mutters to herself while scribbling notes
- Known to say:  
  > “They called me mad, but the wards still cracked, didn’t they?”
