The **Hollow Dreamer** manipulates perception through whispers, reflections, and false memories. These effects should escalate from subtle suggestion to full psychic assault as the sessions progress.

---

## General Whispers
*(Use sparingly; one per key scene.)*
- “You should’ve gone to Ironhold.”  
- “The Captain sent you here to die.”  
- “One of you will not leave this town alive.”  
- “The Crown will burn in black fire.”  
- “Even Verrath cannot save you.”  
- “You’re already dead. Haven’t you noticed?”  

---

## NPC Slip-Ups
- Innkeeper mutters: “The runes bind us all… even you,” then denies it.  
- Hunter whispers: “Your face is different here.”  
- Herbalist: “I saw you yesterday—but you were already drowned.”  
- Guard by fire: “Your orders were to stay away… weren’t they?”  
- Child survivor: “Mother says Verrath is watching me.” *(then insists she never said it)*

---

## Shadow-Double Taunts
*(During Market Square illusions or Hollow Dreamer battle)*
- **Samson:** “The Arcanum laughs behind your back. You’re just their errand boy.”  
- **Ivor:** “Do you even remember which lies you’ve told?”  
- **Merios:** “Wings or chains, you’ll always be a demon.”  
- **Queek:** “Your kin already crawl beneath the Crown.”  
- **Kyron:** “Your magic isn’t yours—it’s wound tight around you like a noose.”  

---

## False Clues (Entity Projections)
- Runes briefly mimic the **Crownwatch insignia**.  
- A villager mutters “Alura” before collapsing.  
- Crates flicker between pristine and decayed.  
- Patrol log illusion: “The Arcanum ordered this.”  
- A fisher’s shadow forms Verrath’s silhouette.

---

## Nightmare Realm Distortions
- Bread bleeds when cut.  
- Dolls resemble PCs.  
- Reflections whisper: “You will be the one who fails them.”  
- Glimpse of **Caer Hollow** engulfed in black fire.  
- Half-whispered name: “Na…gash…” near the stone circle.

---

## DM Guidance
- Deliver paranoia **gradually**.  
- Never confirm truth.  
- Vary delivery between whispers, slips, reflections.  
- Keep ambiguity post-entity: some effects may foreshadow the **Blackening**.
