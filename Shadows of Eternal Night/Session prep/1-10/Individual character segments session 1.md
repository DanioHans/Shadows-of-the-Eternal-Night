# Session 1 – Character Hooks and Timeline

**Purpose**: Guide individual character scenes leading into the Market Square incident, while maintaining fluid pacing and shared timing.

---

## ⏳ Timeline and Structure

Session begins around **mid-morning** in Caer Hollow. The Market Day festival is already in full swing. Characters are in different locations, but events are staggered to allow alternating scene play without leaving players idle.

Use a **cinematic structure**:
- Swap between scenes as tension or decision points rise.
- Align characters toward the Market Square by the **early afternoon** when the basilisk breaks loose.

---

## 🧠 Samson – Errand from the Arcanum

**Starting Location**: Royal Arcanum, Scholar's Descent  
**Involved NPC**: *Master Feloren Drax* (Dean of Arcane Theory)

**Narrative Thread**:
> Samson is summoned to a high-arched, glyph-lit office atop the Arcane Theory Tower. Dean Drax, weathered and clipped in tone, greets him without rising. He hands Samson a sealed note with instructions to retrieve a scroll on planar damping stored at an older merchant's stall in the Market Square.  
> "This scroll predates current classifications," Drax says. "And it *shouldn’t* exist outside the Archive. We were informed it resurfaced."  
> Drax doesn’t elaborate, but there's tension in his posture.  
> "Take no risks, and don’t open the scroll. Just bring it to me. Quietly."

**If Declined**:
> Drax narrows his eyes. “Then I will find another who can act with discretion. Dismissed.”  
> Samson is left to reflect — but the scroll may be discussed by other students, tempting him to go anyway.

**Pacing Tip**: Start with Samson, to give Kyron a reason to intersect with him.

---

## 🔮 Kyron – Encouraged to Explore

**Starting Location**: Public Library, Eastveil Commons  
**Involved NPC**: *Lira Ven* (Curious acolyte)

**Narrative Thread**:
> Kyron is browsing planar anomalies, quietly rereading an article on cross-reality overlays when Lira leans over his shoulder. “That scroll's a little out of date, you know.”  
> She sits across from him with a smile, gently teasing. “You’ve been here how long, and still haven’t seen half the city?”  
> She nudges him: “It’s Market Day. Go. Smell fresh bread. Argue with a fruit vendor. If you’re lucky, someone might shout *‘sorcery!’* and run away.”  
> If Samson walks past the library on his errand, she calls out to him — "Take this recluse with you!" — encouraging Kyron to follow.

**If Declined**:  
> Lira shrugs. “Suit yourself, mystery boy.”  
> Kyron can still arrive later after hearing the panic in the streets once the incident begins.

**Pacing Tip**: Let him attach to Samson early if pacing allows.

---

## 🕵️‍♂️ Ivor – A Whispered Offer

**Starting Location**: An alley behind an herbalist’s stall  
**Involved NPC**: *Lin*, his shady contact

**Narrative Thread**:
> Ivor waits near the herbalist’s stall — not far from the Square but shielded from the crowd. A cloaked figure approaches: Lin, younger than she seems, always glancing over her shoulder.  
> “You want that name?” she whispers. “I want something first.”  
> She hands him a note: a location and time — shortly after noon. “Follow the man there. See what he buys. Don’t be seen.”
> Then she vanishes into the crowd, and Ivor is left on edge.  
> As he tails the man through the shifting market traffic, a sudden scream rips the air — the basilisk cage has broken.

**If He Resists the Task**:  
> Lin backs off, but coldly: “Then the name stays buried.”  
> He may still be drawn into the chaos by sheer proximity.

**Pacing Tip**: This thread **can trigger the combat** — Ivor’s scene leads into the disruption.

---

## 🧪 Scrap – The Wonder of Market Day

**Starting Location**: Already in the Market Square  
**Involved NPCs**: None initially

**Narrative Thread**:
> The smells. The colors. The noise.  
> Scrap is overwhelmed by sensory input — voices shouting, spices wafting from skewered meat, the glimmer of coins in sunlight.  
> Someone bumps into him, then apologizes. Another offers a carved charm with “resistance to ill luck.”  
> A dog barks. A child offers him a candy apple.  
> And just when he leans in to smell an exotic cactus, a *crack* echoes from behind a stall. A cage has snapped open.

**Pacing Tip**: Keep Scrap active — let him follow his curiosity until the incident begins.

---

## 😇 Merios – Familiar Route, Familiar Town

**Starting Location**: Accompanying vendors from Silas & Damon’s farm  
**Involved NPCs**: *Silas* (with her), *Damon* stays at the farm

**Narrative Thread**:
> Silas adjusts the cart reins as they cross into Eastveil Commons. “Trip six,” he says. “Try not to scare *too* many people this time.”  
> Merios walks confidently, disguised lightly but allowing her wings to show. Some vendors recognize her now — she buys from them, sometimes chats.  
> Silas nods toward a stall. “Go on. You’ve earned your freedom.”  
> Merios wanders. She touches woven cloaks. She samples dried fruit.  
> Then something cold brushes her mind — the moment before panic breaks.

**Pacing Tip**: Her moment can sit quietly in the background until you rotate to her in early combat.

---

## 🐀 Queek – A Sudden Arrival

**Starting Location**: Just outside the market’s north alley  
**Narrative Thread**:
> For Queek, one moment was war. The next — silence.  
> Then blinding daylight. A cart nearly flattens him. A flock of pigeons scatters.  
> He crouches, eyes wild, tail twitching. The stench of people. Of surface-world.  
> Then — screams. Not his. Human screams.  
> And something *wrong* — a beast, not unlike the caverns of home, but glowing with arcane pulses.  
> Instinct surges. The warlord charges, blade drawn.

**Pacing Tip**: Introduce Queek once the chaos is fully underway. Drop him in mid-combat — a disruptive wildcard.

---

## 🪓 Integration and Switching Scenes

**Scene Rhythm Tip**:  
- Open with Samson > move to Kyron > Ivor > then Merios/Scrap.  
- Start Ivor’s follow task at **12:15pm** equivalent time.  
- Have Samson & Kyron reach the market by **12:30pm**.  
- Merios & Scrap already on scene.  
- Basilisk breakout at **12:35–12:45pm**.
- Queek drops into chaos at **12:50pm**.

This pacing allows quick rotation:
- Give each player ~5 minutes screen time before rotating.
- If needed, insert group-wide narration or crowd behavior to pace transitions.
