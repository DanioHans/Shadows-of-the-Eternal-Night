#### **Setup: Arrival at Vaelthir’s Domain**

- **Description:**  
    As the players step through the final Fey gateway, the serene beauty of the clearing fades into the **darkened remnants of the grove’s heart.** The air grows heavy with ash, and the faint, crackling sound of burning roots fills their ears.
    - **Barrier to the Arena:**  
        A massive wall of **twisted, burning roots** blocks the path ahead, writhing unnaturally. The faint glow of **red and black embers** pulses in time with an unseen force deeper within.
    - **Nyssa’s Warning:**  
        Nyssa flits anxiously, her glow dim:  
        _“Beyond these roots lies Vaelthir, bound and broken by the Emberblight. This corruption feeds on despair and destruction. Ready yourselves, for once we pass through, there will be no turning back.”_

---

#### **Player Preparation**

- **Opportunity for Final Prep:**
    - The players have time to use abilities, drink potions, or strategize before entering the arena.
    - **Checks or Questions:**  
        Players can make last-minute skill checks or ask Nyssa questions about Vaelthir’s corruption.
        - _“What do we focus on?”_ Nyssa replies:  
            _“Strike at his roots, sever the corruption’s hold. And beware his rage—it blinds him, but it will seek to consume you.”_
    - **Optional Small Boon:**  
        If the group engages meaningfully with Nyssa or each other, she may offer a small boost:
        - _“Here, take this light—it may guide you in your darkest hour.”_ (Grants one player **advantage** on an attack roll or saving throw).

---

#### **Breaking the Barrier**

- **How the Barrier Opens:**  
    As the players finish preparing, the roots suddenly **pull apart**, leaving a jagged, smoldering opening into the arena.
    - The **burning glow intensifies**, and the air fills with the scent of charred wood and magical decay.
    - From within, the **low rumble of Vaelthir’s voice** echoes:
        - _“Intruders. You dare to defile this grove further? You will burn with the rest.”_

---

#### **The Arena Introduction**

- **Description of the Arena:**  
    The players step into a **scarred battlefield**, where the corruption’s full force is evident:
    
    - **Twisted roots and smoldering vines** stretch across the area, glowing faintly with Emberblight.
    - In the center, **Vaelthir, the Ashen Protector**, looms like a burning monolith, his once-vibrant form reduced to ash and glowing embers.
    - The **Verdant Wardens** arrive at the edges of the grove, stepping forth to assist as the fight begins.
- **Vaelthir’s Final Words Before the Fight:**  
    He raises his massive, burning blade, his voice filled with anger and sorrow:  
    _“You will not save this grove—you will join it in ash!”_
    

---

#### **Conclusion**

The tension rises as the corrupted guardian prepares to attack. With his fiery aura radiating across the arena, Nyssa shouts her final warning:  
_“This is it! Stand strong!”_
