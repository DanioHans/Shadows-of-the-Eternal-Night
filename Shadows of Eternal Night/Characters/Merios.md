## 🧬 Species & Lineage

- **Base Race**: Tiefling (Charisma +2, Darkvision, Fire Resistance)
- **Custom Features**:
  - **Charm (1/day)**  
    DC 15 Wisdom save or one humanoid is charmed for 1 hour. Ends on damage. One target at a time.
  - **Draining Kiss (1/day)**  
    Melee spell attack vs. charmed/willing target. DC 15 Con save or 4d10 psychic damage + HP max reduction until long rest. Half damage on success.

> These reflect her succubus ancestry and are treated as innate, limited-use abilities. She may gain additional uses or dark evolutions depending on corruption path.

## 🧠 Corruption Hooks

Merios is **susceptible to corruption** via:

- Repeated use of succubus abilities (if used recklessly or for gain).
- Sword of Screams (already active).
- Entering Shadowfell regions or convergence zones.
- Prolonged denial of divine connection.

Effects may begin subtly: dreams, voice shifts, mood swings. Later stages might include:

- Visible changes (horns, aura, voice).
- Charm-like aura she can’t control.
- Temptations to switch allegiance or embrace demonic strength.

## Backstory and motivation dump

One of hells' generals sent to the material plane to seek an abandoned or weakly defended fortress as a staging ground for a larger invasion force. The Host of Lust, known for subtle actions behind the backs of others, was specifically chosen for this task. However, the breach into the material plane was predicted and prepared for by Human, Dwarven and Elven defenders led by a selunite paladin named Gawain Brightlance. This information came from a summoned, captured and tortured demon deemed as a side objective to retrieve (who turns out to be her sister Meruos).

The incursion targeted the fallen stronghold of Thundertree and saw it overtaken by the Host of Lust, until the fall of dusk when Gawain started his attack. The host was driven into the nearby forest and all but exterminated by the better armed and trained mortal force. Merios, the Hostess at the time, got knocked out by the paladin in an honorary duel and dragged away into the forest by her fleeing subordinates to later be awoken without memory. Fleeing from the remaining survivors, she became more feral by the day until only a hunter was left, prowling the woods for 5 to 7 years until Gawain was sent to finish what was started all those years ago.

A drawn out fight between the uncut power of a feral, fallen succubus and the dedicated skill of an oathsworn veteran paladin is all but spectacular. It was 5 hours of the most devastating micro-war, until sudden unconsciousness hit the succubus. Exhaustion struck the devil hard and once more Gawain stood victorious. Cries of victory were held, a seat was taken and darkness was bestowed on Gawain aswell. For this night both Merios an Gawain received a vision by the moon maiden herself. She ordered cooperation between the two and requested Gawain to train Merios in the ways of worship to the moon maiden. His quest to kill altered to a quest of guidance and training a succubus princess in the ways of selunite worship.
