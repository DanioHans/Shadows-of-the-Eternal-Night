# The High Ring

**Location**: Central elevated tier of [[Caer Hollow]]  
**District Type**: Noble, political, and royal quarter  
**Known For**: The palace, [[Crownwatch Bastion]], noble estates, and judicial halls

---

## Overview

The **High Ring** is the crown of Caer Hollow — both geographically and politically. Built atop a wide rise at the city’s center, its **white stone walls** gleam with well-funded elegance. While rarely open to commoners, its walkways are pristine, its towers adorned with royal banners, and its air thick with unspoken power.

Only the **most trusted servants, officials, and guests** enter freely. The streets are quiet, but every window might house a watcher. Conversations here are soft and rehearsed — even the birdsong feels curated.

---

## District Character

- **Orderly and composed** — patrols are frequent, streets are spotless.
- **Social hierarchy** is immediately felt; even clothing seems more formal.
- Visitors feel out of place unless invited or expected.
- Magical interference is carefully warded — divination is suppressed in key buildings.

---

## Key Locations

- **The Royal Palace of Tharion** – Residence of [[King Halvar Thorne]]. Only partially visible from the streets.  
- **[[Crownwatch Bastion]]** – Fortified guest barracks and holding quarters for adventuring agents and diplomatic guests.  
- **Council Spire** – Judicial and legislative tower where city policy is debated and recorded.  
- **Moonstep Terrace** – A narrow overlook used by royalty during festivals or speeches.  
- **Velstone Archives** – The oldest remaining archive in the city, with guarded royal records.  
- **Gilded Stables** – Housing for official Crownwatch mounts, including wyverns in upper tiers.

---

## Inhabitants

- **Noble Houses** – A dozen minor noble families maintain estates here. Most are politically tied to Velaris.
- **Crownwatch Officers** – Elite soldiers assigned directly to the king’s interests.  
- **Couriers and Clerks** – Rushing between the palace, spire, and Bastion.
- **Magical Servants** – Arcane constructs and invisible aids help maintain the district.

---

## Security

- **Crownwatch presence** is constant — most guards wear enchanted armor.
- **Spell usage** is strictly regulated and monitored by palace wards.
- Secret agents and **palace whisperers** report directly to high officials.
- Escape routes exist but are hidden — known only to certain royal bloodlines or the [[Night Wardens]].

---

## Secrets and Rumors

- The **Velstone Archives** house a sealed lower level marked with ancient planar glyphs.
- Some claim **King Halvar** has not been seen directly in over a year.
- Whispers suggest a secret **council beneath the Council Spire**, made of magically preserved minds.
- One of the palace statues is rumored to be a disguised **planar guardian**.

---

## Use in Campaign

- The party is escorted here post–[[Session 1 — Basilisk Breakout]], to be debriefed at the [[Crownwatch Bastion]].
- Later sessions may involve **royal intrigue**, attempts to **gain or lose favor**, or delving into sealed locations like the **Velstone Archives**.
- Important factions including **Verrath**, **the Night Wardens**, or foreign diplomats may have presence here.
