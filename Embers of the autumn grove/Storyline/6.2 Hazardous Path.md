### **1. Hazardous Path Setup**

- **Scene Description:**  
    The players move deeper into the corrupted forest, forced to traverse a perilous path. Emberblight’s influence grows stronger, warping the terrain.
    
    - The ground is **littered with burning embers**, and corrupted roots seem to **move unnaturally**.
    - **Heat intensifies**, and the air is filled with faint, mocking whispers.
- **Nyssa’s Warning:**  
    _“Without the gate, we must tread carefully. This land is treacherous, and not all who dwell here can be trusted.”_
    

---

### **2. Hazards and Skill Challenges**

Players encounter **environmental challenges** that require teamwork and creative thinking.

#### **Skill Checks:**

1. **Dexterity (DC 12):** Avoid stepping on Emberblight roots that lash out.
    
    - Failure: A player takes **1d6 fire damage** and is knocked prone.
2. **Perception (DC 13):** Spot hidden fire traps in the terrain.
    
    - Failure: A trap triggers, dealing **1d8 fire damage** to the triggering player.
3. **Survival (DC 12):** Find the safest route through the Emberblight-infested terrain.
    
    - Failure: The party becomes disoriented, losing time and taking **1d6 fire damage** each.

---

### **3. Encounter with the Wisps**

- As the group navigates the hazardous path, **small glowing wisps** appear in the distance.
- **Nyssa’s Reaction:**  
    She hesitates, saying:  
    _“Not all Fey are benevolent. These wisps may guide us—or mislead us. Tread lightly.”_

#### **Player Choices:**

- **Ignore the Wisps:**
    - Players avoid the encounter and continue to the camping scene with no further issues.
- **Follow the Wisps:**
    - The wisps lead them into a **dark cave**, illuminated faintly by their glow.
    - **Inside the Cave:**
        - A Fey spirit manifests, playful but mischievous.
        - The spirit offers an alluring reward (e.g., "power," "knowledge," or "blessings").
        - If players engage or accept the spirit’s offer:
            - One player receives a **minor debuff** (e.g., disadvantage on their next saving throw or -1 to a single stat for the next encounter).
        - The spirit vanishes, laughing:  
            _“Not every gift in the Feywild is free. A small price, don’t you think?”_

---

### **4. Transition to the Campfire Scene**

- After the hazardous path, the terrain begins to clear, with the corrupted forest giving way to a small clearing.
- Nyssa encourages the group to rest, reflecting on the **dangers of the Feywild** and the **choices they’ve made**.
    - If the players followed the wisps, she subtly warns:  
        _“Lessons in the Feywild are rarely kind, but they are always valuable.”_
