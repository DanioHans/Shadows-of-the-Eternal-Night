# 📌 PC Integration: Ivor

> A young human rogue driven by the mystery of his own erased past — a calm and dangerous presence in the shadows of Tharion.

---

## 🎭 Backstory Summary

- Ivor **volunteered for a magical experiment** in exchange for gold.
- The experiment failed catastrophically, causing a **magical shockwave** that erased his entire memory — except the sensation of pain.
- The wizard responsible saved him with a last-ditch spell and revealed that Ivor's lost memories might be found in one of the **three kingdoms**, including **Caer Hollow**.
- After a year of unsuccessful searching, Ivor turned to a **life of crime** out of necessity.
- He is now in **Caer Hollow**, following **fragmentary leads** about his past.

---

## 🎭 Personality / RP Notes

- **Calm**, composed — never raises his voice or lets emotions control him.
- **Loyal** to friends; indifferent or ruthless to others.
- Carries a **deep distrust of wizards** due to the trauma of the experiment.
- Publicly comes across as a **quiet, professional criminal**.
- Internally haunted by the **question of his own identity**.

---

## 🗺️ Reason to be in Caer Hollow

- Explicit in backstory: following **leads about his missing memories**.
- Possible NPC hooks:
  - The wizard responsible has **contacts or records** here.
  - An old **arcane archive** in the city might hold more clues.
  - Ivor has **criminal contacts** willing to help him for a price — or manipulate him.

---

## 🧠 Mechanics & Balance

- Rogue 4 — Assassin.
- Combat role: High burst damage, ambush specialist.
- Skill focus: **Stealth**, **Deception**, **Sleight of Hand**, **Persuasion**, **Perception**.
- Excellent for infiltration or RP-heavy scenes.

---

## 🤝 NPC Reactions

- Known to some in the **underworld**; reputation growing.
- May attract the attention of **mages** due to his unusual magical damage aura (if you choose to develop this plotline).
- **Crownwatch** likely unaware of his deeper past — would consider him a minor criminal at best (for now).

---

## 🎭 Roleplay Hooks

- Wizard-related trauma can create tension with **party spellcasters** — great for subtle RP moments.
- As corruption spreads, the "blank slate" nature of Ivor could make him particularly vulnerable — or uniquely resilient.
- His **need to uncover his identity** gives a strong *personal arc* you can tie to larger campaign events.
- Possible hidden truth: was Ivor *special* even before the experiment? (Optional DM reveal.)
