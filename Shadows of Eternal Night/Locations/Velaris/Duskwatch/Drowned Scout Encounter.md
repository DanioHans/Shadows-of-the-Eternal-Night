## Context
This encounter occurs aboard the ferry en route to Duskwatch during **Session 6**.  
A body drifts near the vessel, its **Crownwatch cloak** still intact. Retrieving it triggers a brief but unsettling skirmish, bridging the dream voyage and the investigation arc.

---

## Setup
- **Time of day:** Late morning, calm seas.  
- **Trigger:** Passive Perception ≥ 12 notices movement in the water.  
- **Tension build:** Captain mutters about “sea burials that don’t stay buried.”  
- **Goal:** Establish supernatural tone, provide scout evidence, light combat.

---

## Combatants
### Drowned Scout
Use the standard *Scout* statblock (Monster Manual p. 349) with the following tweaks:
- **Type:** Undead (instead of Humanoid).  
- **Speed:** 20 ft., swim 30 ft.  
- **Damage resistances:** necrotic; nonmagical slashing/piercing.  
- **Attack modification:** Shortbow and shortsword attacks deal an additional 1d4 necrotic damage.  
- **Death Burst:** When reduced to 0 HP, releases a 10-ft. cloud of brine; creatures in the area make DC 13 Con save or take 1d8 necrotic damage.

### Shadow Eels × 2
*Tiny monstrosities (aquatic), neutral evil.*

**Traits**
- **Amphibious.** Breathes air and water.  
- **Shadow Blend.** While in dim light or darkness underwater, gain advantage on Stealth checks.  
- **Blood in the Water.** If a creature within 20 ft. has taken damage this round, eel attacks have advantage.

**Actions**
- **Bite.** +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing + 3 (1d6) necrotic.

---

## Environment
- **Slippery Deck:** Any creature that moves more than 10 ft. must DC 12 Dex save or fall prone.  
- **Crew Involvement:** Two sailors assist using Help actions; if a PC falls overboard, they throw lines on initiative 20.  
- **Lighting:** Daylight muted by haze.

---

## Resolution
- Upon defeat, the eels dissolve into oily film; the Drowned Scout’s body slackens.  
- Recoverable evidence:
  - **Raven-marked brooch** (proof for Crownwatch).  
  - **Water-logged journal** — final entry: *“They dream even with open eyes. The fountain hums. Something beneath watches.”*  
  - 10 gp pouch (crew pay).  
- The captain demands the corpse be cast back to sea. The compass then spins violently before fixing due south → **Duskwatch.**
