Chapel Keeper

_Middle-aged, hollow-eyed, robes unkempt_

**Role:** Spiritual fracture, metaphysical warning  
**Tone:** Uncertain, searching, subdued

### During the Dream

- Repeated prayers endlessly
    
- Heard echoes of his own voice responding
    
- Felt watched by something that _wasn’t hostile — at first_
    

Now:

- His faith is shaken, not broken
    
- He believes something **blocked divine perception**
    

> “I don’t think we were abandoned.  
> I think something stood in the way.”

---

### What He Offers

- Perspective, not answers
    
- Validation that this was _not_ natural
    
- The sense that greater forces are moving unseen
    

He does not claim prophecy.  
He does not claim knowledge.

He simply knows this:

> “The silence felt intentional.”
