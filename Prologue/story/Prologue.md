### Prologue One-Shot: "Shadows of Nagash"

**Level:** 3  
**Setting:** A small village on the edge of a dark forest.

#### Overview:

The village of [[Shadowbrook]] has been plagued by mysterious disappearances and sightings of undead creatures. The villagers are terrified, and rumors of dark magic have spread. The adventurers arrive to investigate and uncover the first threads of Nagash's influence.

### [[Act 1]]: Arrival and Investigation

**Setting:** Village of [[Shadowbrook]]

**1. Introduction:**

- **Scene:** The players arrive at [[Shadowbrook]], a village shrouded in fog and fear. The villagers are wary but hopeful that the adventurers can help.
- **Hook:** The village elder, [[Elara]], approaches the party, explaining that several villagers have gone missing, and strange creatures have been seen at night.

**2. Investigation:**

- **Key Locations:**
    - **The Tavern:** Villagers share rumors and personal accounts of the disappearances.
    - **The Elder's House:** Elara provides more detailed information and maps of the area.
    - **The Graveyard:** Signs of disturbed graves and tracks leading into the forest.
    - **The Missing Villager's Home:** Clues suggesting dark magic (e.g., strange symbols, necromantic residue).

**3. Encounter:**

- **Event:** As night falls, the players witness an attack by undead creatures (skeletons and zombies) in the village.
- **Combat:** Defend the villagers and defeat the undead. One of the undead carries a dark talisman inscribed with runes hinting at Nagash's influence.

### [[Act 2]]: Into the Forest

**Setting:** Darkwood Forest

**1. Exploration:**

- **Scene:** The players follow the tracks and clues into Darkwood Forest, a place of eerie silence and dark shadows.
- **Environmental Challenges:** Navigating through thick underbrush, avoiding traps, and dealing with the oppressive atmosphere (potential skill checks).

**2. Investigation:**

- **Key Location:**
    - **Altar Clearing:** The players come across a clearing with an altar used for dark rituals. Nearby, they discover an old hermit, an ex-necromancer who has renounced his ways. He can provide valuable information about the necromancer in the forest and hints about Nagash.

**3. Roleplay Encounter:**

- **Hermit Encounter:** The hermit is wary of strangers but opens up about his past and the dark magic he once practiced. He shares information about the necromancer who has been disturbing the forest and directs the players towards the ancient ruins.
- **Clue:** The hermit mentions a scroll detailing a grander plan involving Nagash and a map pointing to an ancient ruin deeper in the forest.

### [[Act 3]]: The Ancient Ruins

**Setting:** Ruins of an old temple

**1. Discovery:**

- **Scene:** The players follow the map to ancient ruins hidden deep in the forest. The temple is partially collapsed and overgrown, with an ominous aura.
- **Investigation:** The players explore the ruins, finding signs of past battles and dark rituals. They encounter undead guardians protecting the inner sanctum.

**2. Encounter:**

- **Puzzle/Trap:** Before reaching the inner sanctum, the players must solve a puzzle or navigate a series of traps. This can involve deciphering runes, avoiding pitfalls, or manipulating ancient mechanisms.

**3. Final Battle:**

- **Boss Battle:** In the inner sanctum, the players face the main antagonist of the one-shot: a powerful necromancer who serves Nagash. He wields dark magic and commands several undead minions.
- **Combat:** This fight should be challenging but balanced for level 3 characters, with opportunities for strategic play and role-playing.
- **Revelation:** Upon defeating the necromancer, they find a journal or artifact that reveals more about Nagash's plans to merge the material plane with the realm of the undead.

### Conclusion

**1. Aftermath:**

- **Scene:** The players return to Shadowbrook, where the villagers celebrate their victory. The village elder thanks them and hints at a greater darkness looming over the land.
- **Hook for Campaign:** The artifact or journal contains clues that lead to the next adventure, setting the stage for the main campaign against Nagash.

**2. Rewards:**

- **XP and Loot:** Distribute experience points and treasure, including some unique items tied to the lore of Nagash.
- **Potential Allies:** The ex-necromancer hermit could offer to join the party as an NPC or provide ongoing support.

### Key NPCs

- **Elara, the Village Elder:** Wise and knowledgeable, provides initial quest and background information.
- **The Hermit:** A former necromancer with valuable insights, possibly a future ally.
- **The Necromancer:** The main antagonist of the one-shot, guarding the ruins and hinting at the larger threat of Nagash.
