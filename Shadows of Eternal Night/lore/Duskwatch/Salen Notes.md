*(Recovered through partial dream-imprint in the Duskwatch anomaly)*

> These entries appear in differing levels of clarity, as if written during moments of lucidity amid confusion or panic.  
> Some are scrawled. Some are neat. Some repeat themselves.

---

## 🜁 Initial Patrol Notes  
*(Likely from before the anomaly worsened)*

- “Villagers quiet. Too quiet. Not avoiding us — just… distant.”  
- “Eyes unfocused. Slow to respond. Some don’t respond at all.”  
- “Movement patterns strange. Several walk the same route twice.”  
- “Children sitting by a dried-up well. Staring into it like there’s water.”

---

## 🜁 Things Seen Near the Fountain

- “Saw a man carrying lumber. When I blinked, he was back at the start of the path.”  
- “Saw reflections in the fountain that didn’t match the people standing there.”  
- “Heard someone call my name from behind. No footprints. No one there.”

Margin note, shaky:
> “My own shadow lingered after I moved.”

---

## 🜁 The Chapel  
*(Scout had some familiarity with basic theology but no arcane training)*

- “Candles bent like melted wax but still burning.”  
- “The priest was praying. Same words every time I passed the door.”  
- “Tried to speak to him. He looked at me, but it felt like he looked through me.”

---

## 🜁 The Storehouse / Missing Shipments

- “Crates stacked as if ready to leave, but no wagon tracks.”  
- “Fish barrels warm to the touch though it’s cold.”  
- “Someone tried to hide the Crownwatch seal under mud. Why?”

---

## 🜁 Watching the Locals at Night  
*(Entries become more frantic)*

- “People walking at night. Silent. Bare feet in mud. Don’t react to lantern light.”  
- “One walked into the water and didn’t break the surface.”  
- “Another walked into a wall and vanished.”  
- “A woman stood outside her own house staring in through the window.”

Underlined several times:
> “None of them blink.”

---

## 🜁 The Things That Weren’t People

- “Saw shapes in doorways that dissolved when I got close.”  
- “Saw five figures walking together. I think they were us — our patrol.”  
- “No tracks. No breath. No weapons.”  
- “One looked right at me. Its face was wrong — too smooth, like wax.”

---

## 🜁 The Well

- “Voices echo up from the bottom. Sounded like children at first.”  
- “Dropped a stone. Never heard it hit.”  
- “Felt air pulling downward.”  
- “The stone circle nearby has carvings. Not runes. Claw marks?”

Later:
> “Water isn’t wet here. How?”

---

## 🜁 The Thing Beneath  
*(This is the only entry that suggests he saw the parasite’s influence — but still only as a witness, not an analyst.)*

- “Something under the village. Moving. Breathing.”  
- “Ground shifts sometimes, like it’s stretching.”  
- “Air vibrates when it passes beneath the square.”  
- “If I stand still, I can hear it dreaming.”

Final line:
> “It’s listening. Listening for us.  
> **Don’t sleep.**”

---

## 🜁 Final Attempt at Escape  
*(Likely moments before his death)*

- “Running east. Saw someone ahead waving me forward.”  
- “Looked like Tobias. Or maybe me.”  
- “Can’t tell anymore. All shadows look the same.”  
- “Getting dragged. Can’t… breathe…”

Last fragment, smeared:

> “The well hides everything it swallows.”
