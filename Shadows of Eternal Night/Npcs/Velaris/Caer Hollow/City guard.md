
**Jurisdiction**: [[Caer Hollow]]  
**Commanding Officer**: [[Sergeant Halna]]  
**Affiliated With**: Local governance, **not** directly under the Crownwatch or palace command

---

## Overview

The City Guard serves as the **primary law enforcement** for the general populace of Caer Hollow. Unlike the elite Crownwatch, the City Guard handles **day-to-day policing**, including theft prevention, civil disturbances, market patrols, and neighborhood safety.

They are a **permanent presence** in most districts, often underfunded and overstretched, but still viewed as an essential institution for maintaining order in the city’s sprawling streets.

---

## Duties

- **Patrols** in markets, residential districts, and tavern alleys
- **Response** to reports of theft, assault, public disturbances
- **Crowd control** during festivals and market days
- **Support** during magical or planar incidents, though often undertrained for such events
- **Coordination** (occasionally strained) with the [[Crownwatch Bastion]] in emergencies

---

## Structure

- **Sergeant Halna** – Stern but fair, leads the guard with a hands-on approach
- **Post Captains** – Oversee small local barracks in each district
- **Foot Patrols** – Two to four guards per patrol, usually lightly armored

---

## Equipment

- Light chainmail or gambeson
- Clubs or short swords (non-lethal enforcement prioritized)
- Whistles or horn-charms to summon backup
- Occasional access to basic alchemical restraints or anti-magic manacles (rare)

---

## Attitude and Behavior

- **Practical and weary** – Most guards are locals who’ve grown up with the people they police.
- **Reluctant in magical combat** – Will **hesitate** or retreat during dangerous arcane events.
- **Corruption risk** – Not unknown; bribes or favoritism may happen, particularly in fringe districts or poorer barracks.

---

## Locations

- **Main Barracks** – Located in the Westveil Quarter near the [[Rubblegate]]
- **Watch Posts** – Scattered through the [[Eastveil Commons]], Slopeside, and merchant roads
- Frequently present at **[[Market Square]]** events

---

## Notable Notes

- During high-risk events, the **Crownwatch** may overrule City Guard orders
- Some senior guards quietly resent the prestige of the palace forces
- Rumors suggest **a few guards were frozen in place** during the basilisk attack, shaken by fear or something more...
