## Overview

Captain Teron Veyne is the commanding officer of the **Crownwatch Bastion** in Caer Hollow, overseeing all Crownwatch operations within the city and surrounding region. His authority is widely respected, and his reputation as a strategic and no-nonsense leader ensures loyalty and efficiency within his ranks.

Veyne rarely tolerates incompetence or unnecessary bureaucracy but is known to reward initiative and integrity. While he is not overtly warm, his commitment to protecting the kingdom and its people is unwavering.

---

## Role in Campaign

- Veyne is the **primary contact** for the party following their heroic intervention during the [[Session 1 - Market Day Incident]].
- He considers the party "unofficial operatives" for the Crownwatch, keeping them on his radar for future incidents.
- May become an **ally**, **mentor**, or **obstacle**, depending on how players act.

---

## Authority Structure

- **Crownwatch:** Led directly by Veyne — elite investigative and tactical division.
- **City Guard:** Day-to-day city policing and defense, under the command of [[Sergeant Halna]].
- Veyne holds jurisdiction over both when matters escalate to kingdom-level threats.

---

## Potential Threads

- May suspect links between the market incident and the [[Royal Arcanum of Velaris]].
- Interested in Verdant Wastes activity and seeks capable agents to investigate quietly.
- May be challenged politically if the Crownwatch's authority is questioned or overstepped.

---

## Notes

- The party should feel a balance of **respect and caution** when dealing with Veyne.
- A good candidate for delivering urgent quests or providing support with resources or information.
