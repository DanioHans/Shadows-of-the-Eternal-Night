# Kingdoms and Major Regions of Tharion

## 1. Velaris (Human-led, Arcane-Tilted)

- **Race Mix**: Primarily human, some elves and gnomes.
- **Flavour**: Scholarly and proud, but stagnant. Reliant on magic as tradition, now shattered after Hollowfall's fall.
- **Capital**: Hollowfall (formerly Caer Hollow)
- **Key Sites**: Greylake, Temple of Dawn, Weeping Fields, Cradle Tower.

---

## 2. Redvale (Human Kingdom, Martial and Proud)

- **Race Mix**: Mostly human, dwarven minority in the army.
- **Flavour**: Harsh winters, strong steel, and suspicion of magic. Frontier spirit.
- **Capital**: Redhall
- **Key Sites**: Ashfort Watch, Bannerhold Fields, Hallowcrag Keep.

---

## 3. Greymoor (Dwarven Hold, Crumbling Glory)

- **Race Mix**: Dwarves, gnomes, and deep halflings.
- **Flavour**: Old halls, fractured politics, mines whispering with shadow.
- **Capital**: Stonegate
- **Key Sites**: Emberdeep Forge, Hollowed Hall, Blackmouth Chasm.

---

## 4. Thornmere (Elven Domain, Nature-Touched but Fading)

- **Race Mix**: Wood elves, fey-blooded half-elves.
- **Flavour**: Dying forests, ancient songs, desperation cloaked in beauty.
- **Capital**: Elenshade
- **Key Sites**: Wyrmroot Tree, Moonbrook Crossing, Shardgrove Ruins.

---

## 5. Lyndor’s Reach (Human Republic, Political Powerhouse)

- **Race Mix**: Humans, halflings, dwarves, and a few tieflings.
- **Flavour**: Merchants, bureaucrats, and quiet corruption.
- **Capital**: Highmere
- **Key Sites**: Gallows Harbor, Hall of Balance, Vault of Dusk.

---

## 6. Duskwatch (Mixed Border Region, Cursed Frontier)

- **Race Mix**: Humans, half-orcs, tieflings, other "outcasts."
- **Flavour**: Independent holds struggling to survive near corruption zones.
- **Capital**: No true capital — clusters of fortified settlements.
- **Key Sites**: Lastlight Keep, Mireway Bridge, Veilscar Hollow.

---

## 7. Kaelstone (Isolated Coastal Kingdom)

- **Race Mix**: Humans, coastal dwarves, sea elves.
- **Flavour**: Misty coasts, forgotten truths, old magics.
- **Capital**: Caelwyn
- **Key Sites**: Crescent Bay, Stormrest Monastery, Moonwell Depths.

---

## 8. The Verdant Wastes (Corrupted Wilds)

- **Race Mix**: Unknown. Twisted remnants of once-living creatures.
- **Flavour**: Formerly lush lands, now wild and unpredictable due to ancient magical disaster.
- **Nature**: Not a kingdom—an ungoverned ruinous expanse.
- **Key Sites**:
  - **Thornspire**: A massive semi-sentient dead tree at the heart of the Wastes.
  - **Ruins of Caerneth**: City devoured by magic and entropy.
  - **Evershade Mire**: A swamp where reality twists under the growing veil.

---

# Notes on the World

- **Shadow corruption** is spreading, primarily affecting Velaris for now.
- **Race distribution** is practical — most regions are human-majority, but major minority enclaves exist naturally.
- **The Verdant Wastes** are a chaotic zone, great for side quests, powerful relics, or shadow anomalies.
