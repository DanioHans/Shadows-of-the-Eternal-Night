**Level**: 2nd | **School**: Enchantment 
**Casting Time**: 1 Action  
**Range**: 60 feet  
**Duration**: 1 round  
**Components**: V, S

**Effect**: Choose one creature that can see you. The target must make a Charisma saving throw.

- On a failure: The creature has **disadvantage on its next attack roll or ability check** before the end of its next turn.
- Additionally, **undead allies within 10 feet** of the caster gain **5 temporary hit points**.

**Flavor**: The caster’s eyes briefly reflect the target’s dying moment — not their death, but the fear of it. The image lingers like an afterburn.
