**Blighted Revenant**  
_Medium Undead (Fey-Touched), Neutral Evil_  
**AC**: 13 (Natural Armor)  
**HP**: 24 (4d8 + 6)  
**Speed**: 30 ft.

---

### **Stats**

|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
|14|12|12|6|10|8|

**Saving Throws**: Wis +2  
**Skills**: Perception +2, Stealth +3  
**Damage Resistances**: Fire, Necrotic  
**Condition Immunities**: Charmed, Frightened  
**Senses**: Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 12  
**Languages**: Understands Sylvan but cannot speak

---

### **Abilities**

#### **Burning Husk (Passive)**

When the Blighted Revenant takes **slashing or piercing damage**, it releases a burst of embers. The attacker must make a **DC 12 Dexterity saving throw**, taking **1d4 fire damage** on a failure.

#### **Withered Mind (Passive)**

The revenant's mind is dulled by its corruption. It has **advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened**.

---

### **Actions**

#### **Blighted Strike (Melee Weapon Attack)**

- **Attack:** +4 to hit
- **Reach:** 5 ft.
- **Hit:** 1d6 + 2 slashing damage, plus **1d4 necrotic damage** (avg. 7 total).

#### **Flaming Grasp (Recharge 5–6)**

- The revenant's claws ignite with burning energy.
- **Attack:** +4 to hit
- **Range:** 5 ft.
- **Hit:** **2d6 fire damage** (avg. 7). The target must make a **DC 12 Strength saving throw** or be **grappled** (escape DC 12).
- A grappled creature takes **1d4 fire damage** at the start of each of its turns.
