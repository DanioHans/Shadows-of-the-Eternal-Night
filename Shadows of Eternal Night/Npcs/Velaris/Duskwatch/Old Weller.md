 Fisher / River Warden

_Elderly, sharp-eyed, speaks slowly_

**Role:** Quiet omen  
**Tone:** Calm, unsettling in hindsight

### During the Dream

- Dreamed of standing knee-deep in water
    
- Something brushed past his legs repeatedly
    
- Not hostile — curious
    

Now awake:

- Says the river feels “emptier”
    
- Claims something **left**, not died
    

He is not a scholar.  
He doesn’t know what it was.

But he’s certain:

> “It’s not finished.”
