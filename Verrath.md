# Verrath, High Arcanist of the Crown

**Title**: High Arcanist Verrath  
**Affiliation**: Royal Arcanum of Velaris, Crownwatch  
**Location**: Primarily within the [[Cradle Tower]] or council chambers in the [[High Ring]]  
**Tone**: Controlled, cold, watchful. Never intrusive — but always aware.

---

## Overview

Verrath is the King’s most trusted arcane advisor — the final authority on matters of magical security and the unstable fabric between planes. He speaks rarely, acts with precision, and holds authority over both the Royal Arcanum and the magical operations of the Crownwatch.

Rumors claim he has not visibly aged in decades. His robes are simple but unburning, his eyes pale as ash, and his voice rarely rises above a whisper.  
Some believe he can hear all magical speech within the High Ring. Others believe he was once human — and now is something else.

---

## Personality and Demeanor

- **Silent Authority**: Does not engage in small talk. Rarely answers questions directly.
- **Measured Movements**: Every action feels rehearsed and minimal.
- **Protective of Knowledge**: Treats magical secrets as both tools and threats.
- **Political Ghost**: Present in almost every decision, but rarely mentioned in public declarations.

---

## Role in Caer Hollow

- Oversees all arcane matters related to the Royal Court and Crownwatch operations.
- Enforces **restricted knowledge protocols** in the [[Royal Arcanum of Velaris]].
- Frequently opposes open research into the [[Verdant Wastes]], [[Void Magic - Overview]], and the **Shadow corruption**.
- May have quietly sanctioned the expansion of the Arcanum's forbidden sections.

---

## Secrets and Suspicions

- **Ties to Forgotten Magic**: His aversion to its study may stem from **intimate knowledge** or former use.
- **Temporal Instability**: Some scholars report seeing him in multiple locations — impossibly close in time.
- **Keeper of the Book of Eternity**: Said to have locked it away after a failed reading warped the mind of a promising graduate.
- **Selunite Tensions**: Certain temples report that Verrath’s presence causes divine magic to falter, though no formal accusation has ever been made.

---

## Relationship with the Players

- **Cautiously Curious**: Verrath may take interest if a player exhibits unusual magical behavior or divine conflict.
- **Potential Interrogator**: If any incident occurs involving arcane disruption, Verrath might personally oversee questioning.
- **Unclear Motives**: He may assist or hinder the players depending on their alignment with the throne — and their willingness to obey information boundaries.

---

## Possible Hooks

- Players may encounter Verrath if they:
  - Trigger magical detection at the Arcanum.
  - Attempt to study or tamper with sealed knowledge.
  - Ask too many questions about leyline surges, the Wastes, or shadow entities.
- His approval or disdain may influence the players’ **privileges at the Crownwatch Bastion** or Arcanum access.
- He may eventually push for **control** over players with strong magical potential — as allies or as risks.
