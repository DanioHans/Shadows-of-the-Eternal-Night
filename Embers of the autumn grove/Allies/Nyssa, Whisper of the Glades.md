### **Abilities**

#### **Healing Tree Creation (Recharge 5–6)**

As an **action**, Nyssa plants a **Healing Tree Zone** within **30 feet**.

- **Effect**: The tree provides **2 HP healing per round** to all allies **within 5 feet** at the start of their turn.
- **Duration**: Until destroyed or the battle ends.
- **Weakness**: Fire-based attacks (**Vaelthir’s Falling Branches, Fire Spires**) automatically destroy these trees.

#### **Whispering Guidance (Passive)**

At the **start of each round**, Nyssa provides tactical guidance to the players, pointing out weak points, hazards, or ways to **interact with the environment**.

#### **Feylight Form (Passive)**

Nyssa hovers and flickers between **this realm and the Feywild**, giving her **advantage on Dexterity saving throws** against area effects.

---

### **Actions**

#### **Healing Wave (Recharge 5–6)**

Nyssa releases a burst of **restorative Fey energy**.

- **All creatures within 20 feet heal for 8 HP**.

#### **Feystep (1/Short Rest, or as a 1st-level spell)**

Nyssa **teleports up to 30 feet** to an unoccupied space she can see.

- **Limitations**: Cannot pass through solid objects or magical barriers.
