### **The Riddle Ring: Summary**

#### **Setup**

- **The Wisps' Challenge:**  
    In the center of the clearing, the wisps form a glowing ring and hum a melodic phrase:  
    _“Test your wits and answer true; for those who do, a boon is due.”_
    
- **Mechanics:**  
    The wisps ask **3 riddles**, which the group must solve collectively. Players can discuss and propose answers, emphasizing teamwork.
    
    - To succeed, the group must answer **2 out of 3 riddles** correctly.

---

#### **Riddles**

1. _“I am not alive, but I can grow. I do not have lungs, but I need air. What am I?”_  
    (Answer: Fire)
    
2. _“The more you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?”_  
    (Answer: Footsteps)
    
3. _“I fly without wings, I cry without eyes. Wherever I go, darkness flies. What am I?”_  
    (Answer: A cloud)
    

---

#### **Outcome**

- **Success (3/3 Correct):**  
    The wisps hum joyfully and gift the party:
    
    - **Boon of Unity:** Once during the final battle, one player can activate the boon, giving all allies **advantage on their next attack roll or saving throw.**
    - _"Take this gift, brave ones, a spark of our light. It may brighten your darkest hour... or snuff out the flame entirely. Choose its use wisely."_
    - When activated, the orb releases a burst of Fey light, surprising the group and revealing its beneficial nature.
- **Failure (Less than 3 Correct):**  
    The wisps giggle and scatter, saying:  
    _“Close, but not quite. Perhaps your wits will serve you better elsewhere!”_
    
    - A **minor Fey prank** occurs, such as a harmless illusion or one player glowing faintly for an hour.

---

This keeps the encounter fun and interactive without overly punishing failure. Let me know if you need further refinements!
