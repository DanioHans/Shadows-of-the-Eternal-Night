## Race: Half-Elf

## Class: Ranger (Hunter)

## Background: Folk Hero

---

### Appearance

- **Age:** Mid-30s
- **Hair:** Blonde, long
- **Eyes:** Hazel
- **Clothing:** Camouflaged leather armor, green and brown tones

---

### Backstory

Kael has lived in the forests around [[Shadowbrook]] all their life, protecting the village from threats and aiding in hunts. They are well-respected among the villagers.

---

### Skills

- **Stealth**
- **Nature**
- **Perception**

---

### Abilities and Features

- **Strength:** 12 (+1)
- **Dexterity:** 16 (+3)
- **Constitution:** 13 (+1)
- **Intelligence:** 10 (0)
- **Wisdom:** 14 (+2)
- **Charisma:** 12 (+1)

#### Ranger Features

- **Favored Enemy:** Beasts. You have advantage on Wisdom (Survival) checks to track your favored enemies, as well as on Intelligence checks to recall information about them.
- **Natural Explorer:** Forest. You have a favored terrain type where you are particularly adept.
- **Fighting Style:** Archery. You gain a +2 bonus to attack rolls you make with ranged weapons.
- **Spellcasting:** Ability to cast ranger spells using Wisdom.

#### Hunter Features

- **Colossus Slayer:** Your tenacity can wear down the most potent foes. When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, the creature takes an extra 1d8 damage if it’s below its hit point maximum.

#### Racial Traits

- **Darkvision:** See in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light.
- **Fey Ancestry:** You have advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can’t put you to sleep.
- **Skill Versatility:** You gain proficiency in two skills of your choice (Stealth, Perception).
