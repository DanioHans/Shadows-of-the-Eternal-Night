### [[Prologue]] (One-Shot)

**Title:** "Shadows of Nagash" **Level:** 3 **Setting:** A small village on the edge of a dark forest. **Synopsis:**

- The players start in a village plagued by mysterious disappearances.
- They investigate the forest and discover a necromancer performing dark rituals.
- The necromancer is a minor follower of Nagash, testing the boundaries of life and death.
- The players defeat the necromancer, but not before he hints at a greater power, Nagash, orchestrating events from the shadows.

### Act 1: "The Gathering Darkness"

**Levels:** 5-8 **Setting:** Various small towns and wilderness. **Synopsis:**

- The players are drawn together by reports of undead rising in increasing numbers.
- They discover that several small cults dedicated to Nagash are operating in the region, each led by powerful necromancers or undead creatures.
- Key Battles: Fighting a vampire lord terrorizing a town, dismantling a cult summoning powerful undead, and defending a town under siege by a zombie horde.
- Climax: The players find a map indicating several sites of dark significance, suggesting a larger plot.
- **Incorporating Null:** The players encounter a dragon cult that worships Null, seeking to exploit the chaos Nagash is causing for their own ends. They hint at a potential alliance between Nagash and Null.

### Act 2: "The Shadowed Path"

**Levels:** 9-12 **Setting:** Custom demiplane of shadows or Shadowfell. **Synopsis:**

- The players follow clues leading them to a portal that takes them to the Shadowfell or a custom demiplane where Nagash’s influence is strong.
- They encounter a twisted version of the material plane, filled with shadowy undead and dark magic.
- Key Battles: Confronting a shadow dragon aligned with Null, defeating a powerful banshee queen, and rescuing a captured celestial who reveals more about Nagash’s plans.
- Climax: Discovering a grand ritual site where Nagash’s minions are attempting to merge the material plane with the Shadowfell.

### Act 3: "The Rise of Null"

**Levels:** 13-16 **Setting:** Shadowfell and cursed lands. **Synopsis:**

- The players must thwart Nagash’s plans by disrupting key rituals and destroying his remaining lieutenants.
- They learn that Null, the dragon god, is seeking to capitalize on the merging of the realms to spread his own influence and chaos.
- Key Battles: Defeating a powerful necromancer dragon cult leader, thwarting a death knight’s attempt to corrupt a major city, and facing a necromancer who has turned an entire region into a deathtrap.
- Climax: The players face Null’s avatar, a powerful draconic entity trying to merge the realms further for Null’s gain, revealing the conflict between Nagash and Null’s followers.

### Final Act: "The Fall of Nagash"

**Levels:** 17-20 **Setting:** Nagash’s Fortress and the Shadow Realm. **Synopsis:**

- The players must infiltrate Nagash’s heavily guarded fortress, battling through his most powerful minions.
- They face several mini-bosses, including an ancient lich, a vampire lord, and a death knight general.
- **Character Development:** Players, especially paladins and clerics, must adapt to the Shadowfell’s lack of light. They might seek alliances with shadowy beings, change their gods, or accept shadowy/undead powers to continue their quest.
- Key Battles: An epic confrontation with Nagash’s personal bodyguard, a demonic entity bound to his will, and navigating a labyrinth of traps and undead horrors.
- Climax: The final battle against Nagash himself, where the players must use all their skills and power to defeat the Supreme Lord of the Undead.
- Epilogue: The world begins to heal from Nagash’s influence, with the players hailed as heroes. The conflict with Null leaves lingering threats, setting the stage for potential future adventures.

### Potential Henchmen/Bosses:

1. **Necromancer Cult Leader:** A powerful spellcaster leading a cult dedicated to Nagash.
2. **Vampire Lord:** A tyrant ruling over a town with an iron fist, creating thralls to serve Nagash.
3. **Lich:** An ancient undead wizard with vast knowledge and power, serving as one of Nagash’s chief lieutenants.
4. **Death Knight:** A fallen paladin who now serves Nagash, commanding legions of undead.
5. **Banshee Queen:** A powerful undead spirit haunting a cursed castle.
6. **Undead Dragon:** A colossal undead beast raised by Nagash’s magic.
7. **Shadow Dragon:** A dark dragon serving Null, found in the Shadowfell.

### Incorporating Player Adaptations:

- **Paladins and Clerics:** Offer quests or NPCs that provide alternative sources of divine power, like shadowy deities or ancient spirits of the Shadowfell. These alternatives should be powerful but come with their own challenges and moral dilemmas.
- **Light and Shadow:** Use the Shadowfell’s oppressive atmosphere to enhance storytelling and character development. Light-based spells and abilities may have reduced effectiveness, encouraging creative solutions and role-playing opportunities.

This structure provides a clear progression, escalating challenges, and a series of memorable battles leading up to the ultimate confrontation with Nagash, while incorporating the dragon god Null and offering rich character development opportunities in the Shadowfell.
