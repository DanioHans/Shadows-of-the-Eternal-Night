# Market Square

**Location**: [[Eastveil Commons]], [[Caer Hollow]]  
**Type**: Central market district  
**Events**: Monthly market festival, regular produce market

---

## Overview

The Market Square is a **dense, bustling maze of stalls and alleyways**, centered around a broad plaza with a **stone fountain** at its heart. Nestled in the heart of Eastveil Commons, it serves as the economic pulse of Caer Hollow — lively, chaotic, and diverse in every sense.

During major market days, the district becomes **a labyrinth of voices, smells, and sights** as merchants, travelers, and townsfolk gather. While cheer dominates the air, the crowd invites **petty crime**, and **guard presence** is visible but sometimes reluctant to act — especially in the face of magical threats like the recent basilisk incident.

---

## Structure & Layout

- **Central Fountain** – The focal point of the district, often used as a meeting point or stage for performers.
- **Stall Networks** – Dozens of tightly packed merchant stalls line the paths and alleys; few permanent structures exist here.
- **Alleyways** – Twisting and narrow, the alleys serve as shortcuts, hideouts, and escape routes. Watch your purse.
- **Perimeter Paths** – Connect to other Eastveil areas, with easy access to side streets and residential zones.

---

## Market Types

- **Monthly Festival Market** – Occurs once per month, drawing merchants from across Velaris. Exotics, street performers, and rare finds are common.
- **Standard Produce Market** – Operates several days a week with local farmers selling fruit, vegetables, cheeses, and crafts.

---

## Vibe and People

- **Atmosphere**: Bustling, cheerful, chaotic — a true melting pot of Caer Hollow society.
- **Attendees**: Locals, nobles' servants, adventurers, farmers, traders, and unusual folk from across Velaris.
- **Pickpockets & Petty Crime**: Common in dense crowds. Guards patrol, but enforcement is lax unless serious harm occurs.

---

## Guard Presence

- **City Guard** patrols in pairs during market days.
- Present, but hesitant to intervene during magical threats.
- May use crowd control tactics or call for backup when overwhelmed.

---

## Regional Goods (Examples)

- **Velarin Honey-Brushed Flatbread** – Sweet and smoky, sold warm from carts.
- **Ridgebark Carvings** – Small animal totems made from the bark of cliffside trees in central Velaris.
- **Sunroot Cheese** – Pale yellow, firm cheese made from mountain-fed cattle.
- **Ashfire Spices** – Blended powders from volcanic southern Velaris, often used in street food.
- **Midsilk Cloaks** – Bright, flowing cloaks favored by Eastveil performers.
