Mayor of Duskwatch

_Human, late 50s, gaunt, tired eyes, steady voice that occasionally falters_

**Role:** Civic leader, moral hinge, political seed  
**Tone:** Earnest, ashamed, quietly resolute

### Background

Elric Thorne is a **distant cousin** of **Halvar Thorne**, King of Velaris.  
Not close enough to wield power — close enough to be **noticed**, forgotten, and resented in equal measure.

He did not gain his position through royal appointment:

- He was _elected locally_ decades ago
    
- The Thorne name simply made the process smoother
    

Elric has spent most of his life **trying to prove he is not his lineage**.

---

### During the Dream

- He remembers _standing in his study for days_, rereading the same page
    
- He dreamed of paperwork never finished, decisions never made
    
- He feels responsible — _even if logic tells him he couldn’t act_
    

He woke with:

- Crushing guilt
    
- A headache that has not faded
    
- The certainty that “this will not be the last village”
    

---

### What He Knows

- An **academy researcher** (Calder Veyl) approached him months ago
    
- Framed the research as _purely observational_
    
- Mentioned the well as “anomalous but stable”
    
- Promised no danger, no rituals, no summoning
    

Elric:

- Allowed access
    
- Signed no formal report
    
- Did not inform Caer Hollow authorities
    

> “I thought discretion was kindness.”

---

### What He Fears (But Won’t Say Unless Pressed)

- That the Academy will deny involvement
    
- That the Crown will quietly bury this
    
- That Duskwatch will become a _footnote_
    

If the party mentions **reporting to higher authority**, he hesitates — not to stop them, but because:

- He doesn’t know **who** can be trusted
    
- He is unsure whether the King would even listen
    

---

### Subtle King Foreshadowing

If the party asks about **Halvar Thorne**, Elric says:

> “The King is… distant. He always has been.  
> Attentive to borders. To threats.  
> Less so to… quiet suffering.”

This is _not an accusation_ — it’s a **seed**.

If pressed harder:

- Elric admits letters have gone unanswered
    
- Or replies arrived weeks late, oddly worded
    

Enough to raise:

> _Is the King unaware — or selectively blind?_

---

### Likely Player Outcomes

- Request for an audience (later)
    
- Suspicion of royal inaction
    
- Sympathy for Elric as a man caught between duty and blood
