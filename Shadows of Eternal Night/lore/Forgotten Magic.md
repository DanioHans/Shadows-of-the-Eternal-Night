An ancient, nameless discipline believed to predate formal arcane theory. It is not taught, recorded, or even acknowledged in modern magical circles — and most scholars deny it ever existed.

Fragments of it have begun to reappear through anomalies in the Weave, corrupted artifacts, and the rise of necromantic cult activity tied to [[Nagash]].

---

## Nature of the Discipline

- **Not a school of magic**. Its spells and rituals do not conform to the Weave’s accepted principles.
- **Cannot be learned** through study alone. It requires access to unstable magical fonts, forgotten artifacts, or cursed tomes such as the [[Book of Eternity]].
- Associated with **rituals**, **planar manipulation**, **veil-thinning**, and **mass soul alteration**.
- Capable of altering reality on a **regional or planar scale** — often permanently.

---

## Why It Was Forgotten

- The cost of practicing it was catastrophic: lives, memories, even divine connections were consumed.
- Most historical evidence was either erased or hidden, often by divine orders or arcane purges.
- Those who practiced it often **ceased to be mortal in mind or form**, becoming shadows of their former selves — or worse, **anchors of corruption**.

---

## Traits and Mechanics

- **Ritual-Based**: Effects may require hours, days, or weeks to fully complete.
- **Catalyst-Driven**: Needs powerful magical fonts (e.g. [[The Black Pyramid]], veil fractures, corrupted leyline nodes).
- **Follows No Spell Slot System**: Cannot be counterspelled or learned via scrolls or spellbooks.
- **Costly**: Typically involves sacrifices of life, identity, time, or belief — sometimes unwillingly.
- **Unstable**: Results are often irreversible and draw the attention of planar entities.

---

## Known Rituals (Partial)

- [[The Blackening]] – A city-consuming event that shatters the boundary between the [[Shadowfell]] and Material Plane.
- [[Ritual of Dusk Crossing]] – A planar shift ritual to access the [[Shadowfell]].
- [[The Hollow Covenant]] – A binding pact that converts divine casters into veil-aligned forms.
- [[ The Hourglass Reversal]] – A soul-restoration ritual with heavy cost and risk.
- [[Rebirth of Flesh]] – Rumored method of attaining immortal form through sacrifice (unconfirmed).

## Interaction with the Weave

- **Bends or bypasses** traditional spellcasting systems.
- **Holy and arcane magic weaken** in its presence.
- **Divine suppression** is common near Forgotten Magic rituals or sites.

---

## Modern Understanding

- Only the most obscure scholars, undead entities, or long-lost cults acknowledge its existence.
- Some in the Shadowfell claim it is **not forgotten, only waiting**.
- The resistance refers to it as:
  > “The hollow art... not because it’s empty, but because it hollows out *everything else.*”
