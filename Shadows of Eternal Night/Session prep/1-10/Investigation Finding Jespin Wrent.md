
## Goal
The party must discover Jespin Wrent’s hideout, located in the decaying basement of an abandoned tannery. Clues are scattered throughout Smokestack Row, provided by NPCs, environmental cues, and risk-laden interactions. Players should piece these clues together through conversation, exploration, and deduction. This investigation should take approximately 1 hour of session time.

---

## What the Party Already Knows

- Jespin frequents the Severed Mug.
- He wears gloves and often carries a foldable cage on his back.
- He is in debt to someone.
- The barkeep at the Mug mentioned “where leather rots” as a cryptic location.

---

## Investigation Zones & Tiered Clues

`dice: [[Tables^clues]]`


### Tier 1 – General Area Hints (Low Difficulty, Common)

1. **Street Gossip (DC 12 Persuasion / Bribe 1–2 sp)**  
   *“I’ve seen someone matching that description near the west end. Creepy bastard always wears gloves.”*

2. **Mug Loiterer (DC 12 Insight or Perception)**  
   Recognizes Jespin from the Mug, but claims he hasn’t seen him in a few days—“Vanished like a rat down a drain.”

3. **Shop Owner (DC 10 Conversation)**  
   A cobbler mentions a strange chemical odor from one of the alleys. “Smells like rotting glue and piss.” Doesn’t connect it to a tannery.

---

### Tier 2 – Mid-Level Clues (Moderate, DC 13–15)

4. **Sewer Map Found** (DC 15 Investigation or searching alley drain grates)  
   Map shows sealed access routes, but one is marked with chalk near an old tannery.

5. **Suspicious Bag (DC 13 Survival)**  
   Found in a corner. Contains damp rags, animal hairs, and a bit of dried skin with faint scales. Smells of rot. Suggests creature handling.

6. **Argument in Alley (DC 14 Insight to interpret)**  
   Two shady figures argue about a “debt collector” searching too close to the “old tannery.” May trigger a chase or fight if party presses too hard.

---

### Tier 3 – High-Value Clues (Hard, DC 16+)

7. **Graffiti Tag** (DC 16 Investigation)  
   Hidden message in Thieves’ Cant referencing “skin trade” and a “quiet rotten cellar.”

8. **Wounded Informant** (DC 17 Persuasion / Bribe 10gp or heal wounds)  
   A Greyspite member recently beaten. Knows Jespin rents a place below the old tannery to hide “the lizard and more.”

9. **Gutter Access (DC 18 Perception / Stealth)**  
   Party spots Jespin moving below through a floor grate if they’re sneaking quietly nearby. Risk of alerting him early.

---

## False Leads / Red Herrings

- **Sewer Tunnel**: Rumors suggest Jespin vanished into the sewers—but it leads to a dead end. A good place to leave the sewer map.
- **Warehouse**: A decaying warehouse has boot prints, but they're unrelated—used by squatters.
- **Pickpocket**: A failed perception check results in one party member being pickpocketed by a child—inside the pouch is a fake “map” marked with random points.

---

## Failsafes

- **Pickpocket Interception**: If a player succeeds a high Perception check (DC 16), they catch a pickpocket who admits someone paid him to mislead people. Can be intimidated or bribed to reveal Jespin's true direction.
- **Noise and Smell**: As players roam, mention a faint tannery stench increasing as they approach. Add animal noises or occasional movement heard beneath floorboards.
- **NPC "Drunk Prophet"**: An old drunk in the street mumbles a rhyme:  
  *"Skin to rot, debt not forgot, down where the leather sleeps, he keeps."*

---

## Suggested Locations to Introduce Clues

- **Drainage Grate Near Severed Mug**: Sewer map.
- **Abandoned Butcher's Shop**: Graffiti and bag of rags.
- **Crowded Back Alley**: Arguing thugs, pickpocket event.
- **Faded Sign Near a Large Brick Building**: Old tannery sign (nearly unreadable).

---

## When All Else Fails

If players are stuck or misled for too long (30+ min real time), allow one of these:

- **Tannery Odor Intensifies**: A strong breeze brings it past their noses.
- **Animal Cries from Below**: A strange noise from underground.
- **A Familiar NPC Appears**: Someone they met earlier (e.g., the cobbler or shopkeeper) says, “You’re still here? Try that old leatherworks down the way—I’d stay clear though.”
