# Crownwatch Bastion

**Location**: [[The High Ring]], [[Caer Hollow]]  
**Purpose**: Guest quarter and diplomatic barracks for esteemed visitors and adventuring agents of the throne.

---

## Overview

Crownwatch Bastion serves as the king’s formal gesture of trust — a fortified guest keep adjacent to the palace proper. While positioned in the High Ring, it remains distinct from noble manors or arcane institutions. Guarded but not lavish, it reflects honor without indulgence.

The Bastion houses agents, diplomats, and adventurers deemed valuable to the crown. Players assigned here during Act I are given temporary rights of residence, access to limited facilities, and an unspoken **"watch from within"** by certain palace eyes.

---

## Structure and Facilities

- **Main Hall** – Public gathering area. Modestly decorated with Tharion banners. Two guards posted at all times.
- **Private Quarters** – Players each receive a private chamber. Spells of mild warding (alarm, message amplification) are woven subtly into the walls.
- **Messenger's Tower** – Connects to palace messaging system. Scroll-based communication only — magical messaging restricted to upper palace channels.
- **Watch Garden** – A walled interior garden with mirror-polished stone. At night, reflections in the pool seem slightly off.
- **Wardroom** – Basic supplies, weapons locker (locked unless emergency), small shrine to the Dawnmother and Hollow Flame.

---

## Security

- Two permanent **Night Warden agents** stationed within the Bastion.
- Guests are **not permitted to leave after moonrise** without written permission (a rule enforced more strictly than explained).
- One **palace whisperer** is believed to operate inside — a low-ranking clerk or servant reporting directly to [[Verrath]].

---

## Player Function

- Acts as the players’ **base of operations** during Caer Hollow’s pre-Blackening chapters.
- Offers **privacy** for planning, **limited access** to palace-supplied resources, and subtle pressure to remain aligned with the throne.
- May be affected during the [[Blackening of Caer Hollow]] — sections collapse or become inaccessible.

---

## Secrets and Anomalies

- One **sealed hallway** leads deeper into the High Ring — allegedly to an old royal vault now unused.
- The watch garden's reflections **show figures that aren't there** if stared into too long after midnight.
- A guest ledger records **visitors from decades ago**, some of whom were thought to have vanished during the last planar disruption.
