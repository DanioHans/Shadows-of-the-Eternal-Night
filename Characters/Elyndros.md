## **Elyndros Origin**
Elyndros is a celestial being crafted in Mount Celestia. His task was to alleviate the final days of mortals before they would pass into whatever afterlife was waiting for them. But Elyndros flew too close to the sun, he started to show compassion, doing more than necessary and eventually intervening with fate, something that is strictly forbidden according to his doctrine. After a mutual agreement he decided it was best to cast him to the mortal plane, sealing his memories and his powers. 

## **A new life**
I don't remember the heavens people claim I'm from. I only remember waking up in a crater, surrounded by kind people. "Dawn" they called me, as I couldn't remember my own name. I was given sanctuary by the village and learned their language and ways.

When I found an ill village boy, something odd happened. When touching his forehead to check if he was feverish, I felt a warmth flowing through me and my scars began to glow with radiant light and the boy recovered. As it turns out, my touch can heal people. I am unsure where this power came from, but I will use it to the best of my abilities.

Soon after, I started praying to higher beings, trying to figure out where this power could have come from. After many prayers, Lathander answered, a deity driven by renewal, trying to bring light to darker times. I dedicated myself to him, not out of obedience but out of compassion. Lathander enhanced my healing capabilities and I became what he calls a dawnbringer, as I understand it, clerics devoted to him. The title didn't exactly fit my given name, and I don't want to relinquish that name either.

Now, my goal is to bring light to the darkness and vanquish evil that tries to stand in my ways. I'm not actively searching for answers to my past, but my curiosity can't let it go. For now, I am rekindled and I will do my duty of healing those in need.
