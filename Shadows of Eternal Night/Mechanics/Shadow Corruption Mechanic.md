# Shadow Corruption Mechanic

Shadow Corruption reflects the slow, creeping influence of the Shadowfell and Nagash's power over mortal minds and souls.

It is an invisible, escalating danger that grows through exposure to corrupted places, artifacts, and forbidden magic.

---

## Sources of Corruption

- Staying in convergence zones or corrupted locations (e.g., Hollowfall, Verdant Wastes).
- Carrying or studying forbidden artifacts (e.g., [[Book of Eternity]]).
- Performing rituals from the Nine Books of Nagash.
- Failing saving throws against Shadowfell hazards or dark entities.
- Accepting shadow-based bargains or blessings.

---

## Corruption Thresholds

| **Corruption Points** | **Effects**                          |
|------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| 0–24                   | No mechanical effects. Narrative symptoms may occur. |
| 25–49                  | Minor narrative effects intensify. Some spells become harder to cast. |
| 50–74                  | Moderate mechanical penalties. Divine powers falter. Visions worsen. |
| 75+                    | Severe mechanical changes. Risk of permanent transformation. |

---

## General Effects by Stage

### Minor Corruption (25 Points)
- Dreams become consistently dark or twisted.
- Animals react with fear or aggression.
- Mild aura of unease detectable by Insight (DC 15).

### Moderate Corruption (50 Points)
- Divine healing spells may heal for minimum possible amount (1/day random roll).
- Saving throws against radiant spells suffer -1 penalty.
- Characters appear slightly "hollow" — sunken eyes, cold touch.

### Severe Corruption (75+ Points)
- Radiant damage vulnerability (optional if revealed).
- Healing spells can fail entirely (small % chance).
- Small shadowy wisps follow character in dim light.
- NPCs with strong religious backgrounds may refuse to aid.

---

## Embracing Corruption

- Partial Embrace: Gain resistance to necrotic damage but lose eligibility for divine blessings.
- Full Embrace:  
  - Passive aura of despair (-1 to Wisdom saves for creatures within 10 feet).  
  - Minor shadow powers (DM approval).  
  - May become a permanent agent of the Shadowfell.

> True embrace makes characters targets for Nagash’s forces and permanently alters their narrative destiny.

## Sample Personalized Effects

### Paladin (Good-aligned)
- Hallucinations of oaths breaking.
- Doubts whispered into dreams: "Why suffer for a world that will die regardless?"

### Cleric (Life or Light Domain)
- Prayer spells feel unanswered or delayed.
- Holy symbols feel colder, lose their luster.

### Wizard (Neutral or Selfish)
- Obsessive need for forbidden knowledge increases.
- Temporary confusion between reality and visions during spellcasting.

### Ranger or Druid (Nature-tied)
- Nearby plants wither at their touch if emotionally unstable.
- Animal companions show fear or aggression.

### Rogue or Warlock (Self-reliant)
- Trust in allies weakens; persistent belief that betrayal is imminent.
- Faces occasionally seen shifting into death-masks during conversation.
