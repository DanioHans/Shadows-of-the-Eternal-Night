# Thog

## Race: Half-Orc
## Class: Barbarian (Path of the Berserker)
## Background: Outlander

---

### Appearance
- **Skin:** Grey-green
- **Hair:** Black, braided
- **Clothing:** Fur armor with tribal markings

---

### Backstory
Thog grew up in the wild, learning to survive and thrive in harsh environments. They now work as a mercenary, providing muscle and protection for those who can afford it.

---

### Skills
- **Athletics**
- **Survival**
- **Intimidation**

---

### Abilities and Features
- **Strength:** 16 (+3)
- **Dexterity:** 12 (+1)
- **Constitution:** 16 (+3)
- **Intelligence:** 8 (-1)
- **Wisdom:** 10 (0)
- **Charisma:** 10 (0)

#### Barbarian Features
- **Rage:** Enter a rage as a bonus action (2/day).
  - Advantage on Strength checks and saving throws.
  - +2 melee damage with Strength weapons.
  - Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
- **Unarmored Defense:** AC = 10 + Dexterity modifier + Constitution modifier (AC 14).

#### Path of the Berserker Features
- **Frenzy:** Enter a frenzied rage. While in frenzied rage, you can make a single melee weapon attack as a bonus action on each of your turns after this one. When the rage ends, you suffer one level of exhaustion.

#### Racial Traits
- **Darkvision:** See in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light.
- **Savage Attacks:** When you score a critical hit with a melee weapon attack, you can roll one of the weapon’s damage dice one additional time and add it to the extra damage of the critical hit.
- **Relentless Endurance:** When you are reduced to 0 hit points but not killed outright, you can drop to 1 hit point instead. You can’t use this feature again until you finish a long rest.

---

### Equipment
- **Greataxe:** 1d12 slashing damage.
- **Javelins (4):** 1d6 piercing damage.
- **Explorer’s Pack:** Includes a backpack, bedroll, mess kit, tinderbox, torches (10), rations (10 days), waterskin, and 50 feet of hempen rope.

---

### Personality Traits
- **Trait 1:** I’m driven by a wanderlust that led me away from home.
- **Trait 2:** I place no stock in wealthy or well-mannered folk. Money and manners won’t save you from a hungry owlbear.

### Ideals
- **Ideals:** Life is about experience and challenges; to grow strong, one must face adversity.

### Bonds
- **Bonds:** My tribe is the most important thing in my life, even when they are far from me.

### Flaws
- **Flaws:** Violence is my answer to almost any challenge.
