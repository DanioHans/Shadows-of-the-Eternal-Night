
**Role**: Judicial investigator of Caer Hollow

## 🧑 Profile

Cael Rynel is a **methodical and articulate** magistrate, trained in both **legal precedent** and **arcanum ethics**. While not unkind, he is meticulous to a fault and dislikes deviations from established protocol. He values decorum and recordkeeping more than intuition or “gut feeling.”

### Appearance
- Early 40s, pale skin, neatly kept dark hair with a silver streak
- Wears narrow spectacles (no glass—just enchanted rims)
- Trimmed grey judicial robes with silver cuffs
- Carries a runic scribing rod instead of a weapon

---

## 🗣️ Personality

- **Neutral Lawful**: Seeks truth via process, not passion
- Mildly condescending when dealing with emotion-driven individuals
- Believes in reform, not punishment — unless obstruction occurs
- Not afraid to pause proceedings to clarify *"for the record"*

> “Let us focus on facts. Feelings, while valid, do not alter the consequences.”

---

## 🎯 Function in Session

- Leads the **interrogation** with clipped professionalism
- Mostly addresses the group, but may challenge inconsistencies
- Will *not* threaten, accuse, or escalate — unless provoked

---

## 📝 Notes

- Dislikes Verrath’s silent authority but will not openly confront him
- Has previously ruled against noble interests, earning quiet respect
- If asked for personal thoughts: *“The law is a mirror. We must not flinch when it shows us something we dislike.”*
