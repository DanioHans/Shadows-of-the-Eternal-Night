## Overview
The party begins **Day 7**, aboard the ferry bound for **Duskwatch**. The thick mist that lulled them into sleep has thinned, yet an uncanny stillness remains. The dream voyage is never mentioned again by the world—but its echo lingers in subtle ways. Within hours they’ll reach the coast, face one last danger at sea, and set foot in the cursed border village.

---

## Opening Scene
The crew stirs uneasily as the morning light flickers across the waves.  
- The **captain** complains of lost time: his hourglass drained too quickly.  
- A sausage can be found in the lower decks briefly.  
- Faint **runes shimmer** in the ship’s railing before vanishing.  
Let the party role-play freely here; allow quiet reflection or humor to offset the tension.

A DC 14 Arcana or Investigation check reveals a single rune fragment etched into the hull, identical to the one burned into Jaspin’s rat. It fades when touched.

---

## Sea Encounter – [[Drowned Scout Encounter]]
Midday brings an unnatural calm. Then a sailor spots a body floating beside the vessel—a **Crownwatch cloak** visible beneath the surface.

If hauled aboard, the corpse convulses, black water pouring from its mouth and forming writhing tendrils. Combat begins.

- **Enemies:** 1 × Drowned Scout + 2 × Shadow Eels  
- **Environment:** slick deck, DC 12 Dex save each round after movement or fall prone  
- **Reward:** raven-marked brooch (+evidence for Crownwatch report), 10 gp, water-logged journal  

The journal’s last entry reads:  
> “They dream even with open eyes. The fountain hums. Something beneath watches.”

After the fight, the captain orders the body thrown back. The sea swallows it whole. Compass spins wildly, then locks due south.

## Arrival at Duskwatch
By late afternoon, the coast emerges through the haze—**marshy shallows and leaning docks**.  
The ferryman refuses to disembark, promising to return in **two days**.  

### First Impressions
- Silence: not even gulls cry.  
- Reflections on the water move **half a second late**.  
- A distant **bell** tolls once and stops.  
- Crates on the jetty bear the **Crownwatch seal**, dusted with **moonshade moss** residue.

The town sign reads:  
> *DUSK WATCH – Population 312*  

Lanterns flicker along empty streets. Shadows move behind windows, looping the same gestures endlessly.

## Exploration
Players may freely explore several key locations. Each reveals either a **Crownwatch lead** or a **Nightmare clue**.

- **[[Duskwatch - Points of Interest#Gull’s Rest Inn||Gull's Rest Inn]]** – tables set, meals untouched; spectral barkeep murmurs *“last call.”*  
- **[[Duskwatch - Points of Interest#Chapel of the Dawnfather|Chapel of the Dawnfather]]** – kneeling priest trapped in prayer loop; rune-carved bone hidden in altar.  
- **[[Duskwatch - Points of Interest#Fountain Square|Fountain Square]]** – water reflects sky instead of ground; ripples distort faces.  
- **[[Duskwatch - Points of Interest#Old Storehouse|Old Storehouse]]** – stacked crates from missing shipments; shadows crawl toward back wall rune.  
- **[[Duskwatch - Points of Interest#Ruined Watchtower|Ruined Watchtower]]** – scout corpse clutching a cracked compass spinning aimlessly.

Each discovery should grant one or more **[[Duskwatch – Investigation Leads]]**.  
Encourage open-ended problem solving: deciphering runes, preserving evidence, consoling villagers who seem *half present.*

## Ambient Effects
To reinforce paranoia and dream-bleed:
- Random whispers using lines from [[Duskwatch – Paranoia Effects]].  
- Slight delay in echoes and reflections.  
- Occasional déjà vu loops (a crow flying past twice, identical waves).  

Every few in-game hours, reality grows thinner: describe washed-out color and static hum in the air.

---

## Nightfall Trigger
Once two or more clues are uncovered—or when night naturally arrives—the veil trembles.

> The bell tolls again, drawn-out and hollow.  
> For a heartbeat, every reflection in water and glass moves opposite the party.  
> The priest’s chant falters. The wind stops.

End on this **cliffhanger image**:  
The fountain ripples upward toward the moon as the world holds its breath.
