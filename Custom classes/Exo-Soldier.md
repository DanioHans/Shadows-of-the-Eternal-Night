# **Exo-Soldier Class**

### **A Master of Technology and Combat**

The Exo-Soldier is a warrior who merges combat expertise with advanced mechanical ingenuity. With each step in battle, they become stronger, faster, and more capable, thanks to their evolving exo-suit. Starting as a soldier skilled in firearms, they eventually craft a suit of powered armor that turns them into an unstoppable force on the battlefield. From augmenting physical abilities to utilizing advanced battlefield tactics, the Exo-Soldier represents the pinnacle of mechanical warfare.

---

### **Class Features**

**Hit Points**

- **Hit Dice**: 1d8 per Exo-Soldier level
- **Hit Points at 1st Level**: 8 + your Constitution modifier
- **Hit Points at Higher Levels**: 1d8 (or 6) + your Constitution modifier per Exo-Soldier level after 1st

**Proficiencies**

- **Armor**: Light armor, medium armor
- **Weapons**: Simple weapons, martial weapons, firearms
- **Tools**: Tinker’s tools
- **Saving Throws**: Intelligence, Constitution
- **Skills**: Choose two from Athletics, Perception, Intimidation, Investigation, Arcana


---

## **Level 1: Combat Expertise**

Starting at 1st level, you are a highly trained soldier, proficient in advanced weaponry. Whether you prefer long-range precision or close-quarters combat, your training prepares you for anything.

- **Firearm Proficiency**: You gain proficiency with all firearms and martial weapons.
- **Tactical Reload**: You can reload a firearm as a bonus action, ensuring you’re always ready for the next shot.

---

## **Level 2: Subclass Selection**

At 2nd level, you choose your subclass, reflecting your specialized training in combat techniques. Whether you master rifles, explosives, or another weapon type, your subclass will provide you with distinct combat features that complement your Exo-suit.

---

## **Level 3: Momentum Surge**

At 3rd level, you unlock the ability to push your body and mind to their limits. Drawing on deep reserves of energy, you can build up momentum on the battlefield.

- **Momentum Surge**: As a bonus action, you enter a surge state for 1 minute. While surging:
    - Your movement speed increases by 10 feet.
    - You gain a stacking bonus to damage, increasing by +1 for each consecutive round you hit a target (up to +3).
    - Missing an attack resets the bonus.

This feature mirrors the adrenaline-fueled bursts of energy that soldiers experience in the heat of battle.

---

## **Level 4: Ability Score Improvement**

When you reach 4th level, and again at 8th, 12th, 16th, and 19th level, you can increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or you can increase two ability scores of your choice by 1. As usual, you can’t increase an ability score above 20 using this feature.

---

## **Level 5: Prototype Exo-Suit**

At 5th level, your journey toward becoming an unstoppable warrior begins. You build your first **Prototype Exo-Suit**, a basic exoskeleton designed to assist in combat and physical tasks. You are proficient in this and all following Exo-suits.

- **Augment slots**: 1
- **Strength Augmentation**: Gain a +2 bonus to Strength checks and saving throws.
- **Exo-Suit Support**: The suit assists in carrying heavy loads, allowing you to lift, push, or carry twice as much weight.
- **Exo-Suit Defense**: The suit acts like Hide armor, with an AC of 12

This prototype represents the first step toward mastering mechanical warfare, granting you a blend of mobility and defense.

---


## **Level 7: Arcane Integration**

By 7th level, your exo-suit becomes capable of channeling magical energy to enhance your weapons, allowing you to overcome resistances in battle.

- **Magic Weapon**: All weapons and firearms you wield count as magical for the purpose of overcoming resistance and immunity to non-magical damage.
- **Enhanced Ammo**: You can craft special ammunition that deals an additional 1d6 elemental damage (fire, cold, or lightning) per shot. You can create a number of rounds equal to your Intelligence modifier per long rest.

This represents the exo-suit's integration with advanced technology, allowing you to fight foes resistant to ordinary attacks.

---


## **Level 10: Half-Suit**

At 10th level, your exo-suit evolves into a **Half-Suit**, granting increased protection and mechanical assistance.

- **Augment slots**: 2
- **Strength Boost**: Gain +2 to Strength checks and saving throws.
- **Reinforced Armor**: The suit acts as Half-Plate armor, with an AC of 15
- **Enhanced Mobility**: Your speed increases by 10ft. Your jump distance doubles, and you take no damage from falls of 30 feet or less while in the suit.
-  **Power Strikes**: Your unarmed strikes deal 1d8 bludgeoning damage

---

## **Level 11: Modular Upgrades**

At 11th level, you can upgrade your exo-suit with modular systems once per long rest, allowing you to customize your capabilities for different missions.

Choose one of the following upgrades:

- **Overcharged Agility**: You gain advantage on Dexterity saving throws and +10 feet to your movement.
- **Reinforced Plating**: Gain +1 to your AC.
- **Overloaded Momentum Surge**: .

---

## **Level 14: Battle Command Protocol**

At 14th level, you become a leader on the battlefield, inspiring your allies to fight harder.

- **Battle Command Protocol**: Use an action to rally your allies, granting them temporary hit points equal to half your Exo-Soldier level and allowing them to move up to their speed without provoking opportunity attacks, limit: proficiency bonus.

---

## **Level 15: Full Exo-Suit**

At level 15, you unlock the **Full Exo-Suit**, a fully developed powered exoskeleton that allows you to dominate the battlefield.

- **Augment slots**: 3
- **Strength Mastery**: Gain +4 to Strength checks and saves.
- **Powered Armor**: This suit acts as Plate armor, with an AC of 18
- **Energy Shield**: Gain your level in Exo-soldier as temporary hit points every short rest, lasts until a short rest.
- **Power Strikes**: Your unarmed strikes deal 1d10 bludgeoning damage.

---


### **Level 18: Enhanced Systems**

At 18th level, you further enhance your exo-suit's systems with advanced technology, granting you powerful benefits that go beyond the modular upgrades gained at level 11. These upgrades are permanent improvements to your suit and reflect your mastery of exo-suit systems. You can choose one of the following upgrades (swapping upgrades? time consuming to swap?):

- **Adaptive Systems**: You gain resistance to one type of damage of your choice (elemental or physical).
- **Kinetic Conversion**: Whenever you are hit by a melee attack, you can use your reaction to deal 1d8 force damage to the attacker.
- **System Overload**: Your suit further enhances your **Momentum Surge**. Your gain resistance to all damage types except psychic and your damage bonus caps at +5 instead of +3

---

## **Level 20: Capstone Suit**

At 20th level, you unlock the ultimate form of your exo-suit, becoming an unstoppable force of war:

- **Augment slots**: 4
- **Self-Rejuvenation**: samurai capstone
- **Overclocked Strength**: You gain +6 to Strength checks and saving throws.
- **Heavy-Plated Armor**: Your base AC becomes 20 while wearing this suit.\
