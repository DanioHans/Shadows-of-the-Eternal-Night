### **Venture into the Forest: Tracking Vaelthir**

#### **1. Transition Scene: Leaving the Grove**

- **Nyssa’s Guidance:** As the players prepare to leave, Nyssa steps forward and says:  
    _“Vaelthir’s corruption spreads like a firestorm. We cannot linger. His path should be clear to see—he leaves only ash and ruin in his wake.”_
- The group enters the forest, following a trail of **burnt foliage and smoldering roots.**

---

#### **2. Skill Challenge: Tracking Vaelthir**

The forest is dense, and the corruption has disrupted its natural paths. Players must rely on **tracking and problem-solving** to stay on his trail.

- **Goal:** Cover 3 successes before 2 failures.
    
- **Skills/Options:**
    
    - **Survival (DC 13):** Follow the trail of burned roots and ash.
    - **Perception (DC 12):** Spot distant scorch marks or signs of recent destruction.
    - **Arcana (DC 12):** Sense lingering magical energy from the Emberblight.
    - **Investigation (DC 13):** Analyze broken branches or claw marks to confirm direction.
    - **Creative Roleplay:** Players can propose other ideas (e.g., using a spell like _Detect Magic_ to locate the corruption).
- **Consequences:**
    
    - **Success (3/3):** The party keeps Vaelthir’s trail and arrives at the Fey Gate without delay.
    - **Failure (2/3):** The group becomes **briefly disoriented**, and the Emberblight seems to close in.
        - Add tension by describing the forest growing darker and warmer, with faint whispers of corrupted voices.

---

#### **3. Environmental Descriptions Along the Way**

To immerse players, describe the **gradual corruption** of the forest:

- Early on: **Crisp autumn air and scattered leaves**, with signs of faint scorch marks on the trees.
- Midway: **Burned vegetation**, with roots glowing faintly red and ash filling the air.
- Near the Fey Gate: **Trees fully engulfed in smoldering embers**, the sky darkened by unnatural firelight.

---

#### **4. Arrival at the Fey Gate**

The trail leads to a **crumbling stone archway**, its once-brilliant Fey runes flickering dimly. Nyssa says:  
_“This was once a path of the Feywild—a gate between realms. Its magic has faded, but if we can restore it, it may lead us closer to Vaelthir.”_

- This introduces the **optional puzzle** tied to the Fey Gate and the Dancing Wisps.
