# Travel Encounters – Duskwatch Route

The journey takes ~4 days southwest from Caer Hollow.  
- **Day 1–2**: Shared travel → Caer Hollow → Briarford.  
- **Day 3–4**: Party chooses route:  
  - **Duskmire Road (via Briarford Ferry)**  
  - **Riverrun Passage (via Anchor Port private boat)**  

---

## Day 1 (Caer Hollow → south road)
- **Merchant Caravan**: fleeing Duskwatch, wagons half-empty. Nervous, mutter about “faces in the fog.” May sell goods at ½ price.  
- **Starving Wolves**: gaunt, unnaturally silent. Trail the party but don’t attack unless provoked.  

---

## Day 2 (Approach Briarford)
- **Ruined Shrine**: roadside shrine to Pelor (or local deity). Offerings rotted, shadows too long. Religion DC 12 → planar bleed clue.  
- **Scout’s Token**: broken pendant of [[Missing Scouts – Duskwatch#Lysa Dorn]] with bloodstains. Trail fades into woodland.  

### Evening Stop: [[Briarford]]
- PCs cross the **Silverflow by ferry** (cheap, common) unless they push westward to Anchor Port.  
- Decide here: Duskmire Road vs Riverrun Passage.  

---

## Route 1: Duskmire Road (South from Briarford Ferry)

[[Bandit Encounters – Road to Duskwatch]]

### Day 3
- **Briarford Ferry Crossing**: routine crossing, fee = **5 cp per rider / 2 sp per wagon**.  
- **Sinking Road**: causeway collapsed into mire. Carriage stuck.  
  - Athletics DC 14 (multi-helper) or Survival DC 12 with ropes/pulleys.  
  - Wagon ruts vanish deeper into swamp (link to [[Missing Shipments – Duskwatch#Lantern Oil Barrels]]).  
- **Swarm Encounter**: biting insects cloud the air. Con save DC 12 → fail = **Exhaustion 1** until rest. Torches/smoke repel.  

### Day 4
- **Lantern Post Ruins**: toppled stone marker. Oil barrels shattered, footprints vanish into swamp. [[Missing Scouts – Duskwatch#Salen Kett]] last known assignment.  
- **Combat**: pack of **Shadow-Touched Mirewolves (3–4)** + **1 Swamp Hag or Wraith**. Rune scars visible.  
- Clue: broken weapon of Salen Kett found in wolves’ den.  

---

## Route 2: Riverrun Passage (West to Anchor Port → downriver boat to Duskwatch)

[[Bandit Encounters – Road to Duskwatch]]

### Day 3
- **Abandoned Fisher’s Hut**: mid-stop demanded by ferrymen. Looted hut, rusting nets, eerie quiet. One crate of [[Missing Shipments – Duskwatch#Salted Fish & Grain]].  
- **Combat**: 2–3 **Drowned Corpses (undead)** rise from the water at night. Rune scars glow faintly when struck. Ferrymen may panic.  
### Day 4
**Anchor Port**: small dock settlement. Smugglers/fishermen still risk the southern waters.  
  - Private boat deal = **5 gp per rider / 15 gp per wagon & horses**.  
  - NPCs warn the river grows treacherous near Duskwatch.  
- **Thick Mist on River**: while mid-journey, fog rolls in. Illusory figures drift along the banks. Wis DC 13 → recognize illusions. Failures may panic or fall overboard.  



---

## Route Comparison

**Duskmire Road (Briarford Ferry):**  
- Cheap, common ferry crossing.  
- Overland through swamp = exhausting & dangerous.  
- Strong planar bleed → insect swarms, mirewolves, hag/wraith.  
- More direct clues for scouts & shipments.  

**Riverrun Passage (Anchor Port private boat):**  
- Expensive private passage.  
- Travel is eerie & tense, ferrymen superstitious.  
- Strong encounters on water (drowned dead, thick mists).  
- Clues: abandoned crates + fisher’s hut.
