# Act I: [[The Blackening of Hollowfall]]

**Level Range**: 4–6  
**Tone**: Rising dread, catastrophe, survival, desperate retaliation.

---

## Kingdom: [[Velaris]]

A mid-sized realm known for its mastery of arcane arts and ancestral traditions. Velaris stood as a symbol of magical order and balance, though in recent generations it has grown complacent and proud.

---

## City: [[Caer Hollow]] (pre-Blackening [[Hollowfall]])

Once called Caer Hollow, the capital was a beacon of scholarship, architecture, and responsible magic. After the fall, it becomes known as [[Hollowfall]] — a bitter reminder of the devastation.

---

## Minor BBEG: [[Verrath|High Arcanist Verrath]]

Advisor to the king, Verrath discovered the [[Book of Eternity]] and sought to protect Velaris by transcending mortality.  
His ambition triggered the collapse of Caer Hollow. Verrath survives the initial Blackening, retreating into the [[Cradle Tower]], where he becomes the final challenge of Act I.

---

## Structure Overview

### Phase 0: Gathering Shadows (Session 0–Early Session 1)

- Players start individually within [[Caer Hollow]] pursuing personal goals.
- Each experiences strange disturbances linked to minor cult or undead incidents.
- Forced to band together during a chaotic early combat or disaster event.

---

### Phase 1: Rising Tension (Level 4)

- Investigate undead disturbances and cult activities in border towns (e.g., [[Greylake]], [[The Weeping Fields]]).
- First signs of divine interference and shadow corruption.
- Gather rumors of unrest back in [[Caer Hollow]].
- Build bonds as a party through early victories.

---

### Phase 2: Cracks in Caer Hollow (Early Level 5)

- Return to [[Caer Hollow]] and investigate its crumbling facade.
- Discover subtle signs of forbidden magic experimentation.
- Track influence webs leading back toward the [[Cradle Tower]] and [[Verrath]].
- Attempt to warn authorities, only to meet denial, fear, or silence.

---

### Phase 3: [[The Blackening of Caer Hollow]] (Mid Level 5)

- Hollowfall’s fall occurs overnight during a major public event.
- The city descends into undead chaos; magical barriers fail catastrophically.
- Players witness [[Verrath]] wielding the [[Book of Eternity]] during the collapse.
- Forced survival and desperate escape from [[Hollowfall]].

(Level Up to 6 after surviving the Blackening and helping key survivors reach safety.)

---

### Phase 4: Retaliation and the [[Cradle Tower]] (Level 6)

- Regroup at the [[Sanctum of Light’s End]] refugee camp.
- Plan a retaliatory strike into [[Hollowfall]]'s corrupted ruins.
- Infiltrate the devastated city, now crawling with undead horrors.
- Assault the [[Cradle Tower]] and confront [[Verrath]].
- Defeat Verrath and recover the [[Book of Eternity]] — a dangerous key to future survival.

---

## End of Act I: Victory — but Uncertainty

- The players hold the [[Book of Eternity]], a cryptic and cursed prize.
- Act II will begin with the deciphering of the Book and preparations to enter the [[Shadowfell]].

---

## Key Locations in Act I

- [[Greylake]] – Rural town showing early signs of undead influence.
- [[Temple of Dawn]] – Monastery where divine suppression becomes evident.
- [[The Weeping Fields]] – Ancient battlefield disturbed by early planar fractures.
- [[Caer Hollow]] / [[Hollowfall]] – The glorious capital, and its downfall.
- [[Sanctum of Light’s End]] – Refugee camp and resistance staging ground.
- [[Cradle Tower]] – Forbidden observatory; final dungeon of Act I.

---

## Major Narrative Beats

- Initial gathering of unlikely allies during civil unrest.
- Escalating undead threats and rising divine silence.
- Exposure to political corruption and Verrath’s shadowy rise.
- The catastrophic Blackening of Caer Hollow.
- Survival against impossible odds.
- Retaliation against Verrath.
- Recovery of the Book of Eternity — and the first glimpse into the dark truths beyond the mortal world.
