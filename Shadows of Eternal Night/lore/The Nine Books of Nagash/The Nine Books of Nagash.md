
The Nine Books of Nagash are ancient and malevolent tomes authored by [[Nagash]] himself, each representing a vital aspect of his dominion over death, corruption, and the [[Shadowfell]].  

Fragments and full volumes of these Books have been scattered across [[Tharion]], hidden, protected, or lost to history.  
Recovering and understanding these Books is vital to comprehending — and ultimately defeating — Nagash.

---

## The Nine Books (Overview)

- [[Book of Eternity]]  
  Themes: Severance of souls from the life-death cycle.  
  Location: [[Hollowfall]] (recovered from [[Cradle Tower]]).

- [[Liber Mortis]]  
  Themes: Foundation of necromantic magic. Reanimation of the dead.  
  Location: Presumed in the possession of [[Nagash]] himself.

- [[Book of Binding]]  
  Themes: Binding spirits to unwilling hosts, objects, and weapons.  
  Location: Hidden within the cursed ruins of [[Caerneth]] (Verdant Wastes).

- [[Book of Withered Grace]]  
  Themes: Decay, plague, and the slow rotting of all living things.  
  Location: Infiltrated deep within the diseased forests of [[Thornmere]].

- [[Tome of Final Night]]  
  Themes: The extinguishment of divine light and the corruption of sacred ground.  
  Location: Lost beneath the desecrated halls of [[Temple of Dawn]].

- [[Codex of Broken Crowns]]  
  Themes: Corrupting kings, saints, and divine rulers into servitude or ruin.  
  Location: Entombed within the Sunken Vaults beneath [[Highmere]] (Lyndor's Reach).

- [[Oathshard Grimoire]]  
  Themes: The breaking of oaths, pacts, and sacred bonds.  
  Location: Fractured pieces scattered between the [[Duskwatch]] frontier ruins.

- [[Scroll of Endless Hunger]]  
  Themes: The devouring of souls, planar energies, and life forces.  
  Location: Guarded by a corrupted warden deep beneath the shattered city of [[Mirecairn]].

- [[Silent Thrones Manuscript]]  
  Themes: The erosion of civilizations, slow decay of kingdoms into ruin.  
  Location: Buried beneath the haunted ruins of [[Stonegate]] (Greymoor).
