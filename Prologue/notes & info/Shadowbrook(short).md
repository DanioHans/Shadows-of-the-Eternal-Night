**General Atmosphere:**

- Perpetual fog, eerie and heavy air.
- Dim lighting with flickering lanterns and weak torchlight.

**Village Layout:**

- **Central Square:** Weathered stone fountain, surrounded by key buildings.
- **Houses:** Small, wooden, thatched roofs, many neglected.
- **Paths and Roads:** Narrow cobblestone paths, overgrown with weeds; main road is a muddy dirt track.

**Key Locations:**

1. **The Tavern: "The Weary Traveler"**
    
    - **Exterior:** Sturdy, faded wooden sign.
    - **Interior:** Dimly lit, cozy fireplace, wooden tables.
    - **Owner:** Garret, friendly but cautious.
2. **The Elder's House**
    
    - **Exterior:** Slightly larger, well-maintained, small garden.
    - **Interior:** Cluttered with books, maps, trinkets, large table with documents.
    - **Elder:** Elara, wise, knowledgeable, kind-hearted.
3. **The Graveyard**
    
    - **Location:** Outskirts, low stone wall.
    - **Atmosphere:** Overgrown, leaning tombstones, disturbed graves, thin mist.
    - **Feature:** Ancient oak tree casting shadows.
4. **The Missing Villager's Home**
    
    - **Exterior:** Small, dilapidated, door ajar.
    - **Interior:** Disarray, overturned furniture, strange symbols on walls.

**Villagers:**

- Common folk – farmers, laborers, tradespeople.
- Simple, practical clothing, often patched and worn.
- Fearful, suspicious, but desperate for help.

**Local Wildlife:**

- **Daytime:** Forest is quiet, animals keep their distance.
- **Nighttime:** Strange sounds – rustling, howls, unearthly moans.

**Summary:**

- Shadowbrook is a village in fear, plagued by disappearances and undead sightings. Villagers hope adventurers can end the darkness.
