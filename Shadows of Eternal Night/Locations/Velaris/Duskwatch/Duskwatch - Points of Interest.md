# Duskwatch – Points of Interest

Each location below serves as a potential investigation site within Duskwatch.  
Sections include sensory descriptions, optional read-aloud text, and DM cues for atmosphere, clues, and paranoia.

---

## Gull’s Rest Inn
**Description:**  
The largest intact building in the central row, its windows fogged from within. The door creaks open easily, and the interior smells faintly of ale, cold grease, and candlewax that never quite burns out. Dust motes float lazily in the stagnant air.

**Read-Aloud:**  
> The scent of stale ale hangs thick in the air. Tables stand half-set with cold meals; chairs pulled out as if their occupants simply stepped away mid-bite. Behind the counter, a barkeep polishes a glass in slow, looping motions. Every minute or so, he mutters in a hoarse voice, *“Last call… last call…”*  

**Notable Details:**  
- The barkeep’s eyes are unfocused, repeating the same motions indefinitely.  
- A half-filled mug leaks onto the counter, but the puddle never grows.  
- PCs can see faint heat shimmer around the barkeep’s outline, as though he isn’t fully *here*.  

**Checks:**  
- *Arcana / Religion DC 13:* detects lingering planar energy.  
- *Insight DC 15:* barkeep has no self-awareness.  

**Clue:**  
Villagers act on shared routines—first sign of dream entrapment.  

**Paranoia Prompt:**  
When a PC looks into a mirror or glass surface behind the bar, their reflection is *smiling back* even if they aren’t.

---

## Chapel of the Dawnfather
**Description:**  
The chapel sits near the village’s east edge, its stained glass warped and darkened. Candles flicker though there’s no wind, melting upward and sideways. The smell of incense mingles with damp rot.

**Read-Aloud:**  
> You step inside to the soft crackle of unseen candles. The air hums faintly, warm and cold at once. At the altar, a priest kneels—robes stiff with dried wax—bowing and unbowing in slow, deliberate rhythm. His voice echoes with two tones: one human, one hollow.  

**Notable Details:**  
- The priest repeats, *“Light save us from the dream,”* endlessly.  
- Shadows of pews stretch in impossible directions.  
- The altar’s base panel conceals a small compartment.  

**Checks:**  
- *Perception DC 14:* notice false panel under altar.  
- *Arcana DC 12:* detect necrotic resonance from beneath the chapel.  
- *Religion DC 15:* connection to foreign runic symbology (not Pelor).  

**Find:**  
A fragment of rune-carved bone—warm to the touch, faintly humming when near other fragments.  

**Clue:**  
Connects to the rat rune and Dreamer’s anchors.  

**Paranoia Prompt:**  
A candle flame briefly forms the outline of a skull before resuming its shape.

---

## Fountain Square
**Description:**  
The village center: cobblestone square with a dried-up fountain at its heart. The air feels denser here, the color muted like an overcast painting. The water, stagnant and black, reflects the *sky above* instead of what surrounds it.

**Read-Aloud:**  
> The fountain gurgles faintly, though the water doesn’t ripple. Looking down, you see no reflection of yourselves—only the sky, pale and distant, as though staring through glass into another world.  

**Notable Details:**  
- Coins in the basin are tarnished black.  
- The fountain’s outer edge bears half-erased runes similar to the rat’s mark.  
- Villagers occasionally wander through, dropping objects that fall soundlessly into the water.  

**Checks:**  
- *Religion DC 15:* sense divine anchor linking planes.  
- *Arcana DC 15:* identify as planar convergence site.  

**Event:**  
As the sun sets, the PCs’ reflections begin mimicking *different movements*, whispering indistinctly.  

**Clue:**  
Establishes the fountain as a key anchor to the Nightmare Realm.  

**Paranoia Prompt:**  
Reflections occasionally look directly at one PC and mouth *“wake up.”*

---

## Old Storehouse
**Description:**  
An aging warehouse near the docks, the air thick with rot and salt. Wooden crates are stacked neatly, untouched—each marked with the Crownwatch seal and shipment labels. Cobwebs stretch from ceiling to ceiling beam.

**Read-Aloud:**  
> The door creaks as it opens to reveal rows upon rows of sealed crates. Lantern light catches the wax seals of the Crownwatch, unbroken. The smell of damp wood and salt hangs heavy, and the silence is so thick it feels like a weight.  

**Notable Details:**  
- Shadows seem to lean toward one back wall.  
- Runic symbols faintly burned into planks near the floor.  
- If a crate is opened at night, contents appear rotten—an illusion that fades when checked again.  

**Checks:**  
- *Investigation DC 12:* find moonshade moss residue inside several boxes.  
- *Arcana DC 13:* recognize faint planar signature identical to the chapel fragment.  

**Hazard:**  
Touching the rune-marked wall inflicts 1 psychic damage/round; repeated contact triggers whisper: *“The Crown binds… the Crown blinds…”*  

**Clue:**  
Confirms missing shipments, connects to rune network.  

**Paranoia Prompt:**  
A single box shifts an inch when no one is near it.

---

## Ruined Watchtower
**Description:**  
The tower looms over the western ridge, half-collapsed, its stone base sunken into mud. Inside, the air smells of wet moss and iron. A collapsed stair curls upward into nothing.

**Read-Aloud:**  
> The watchtower stands as a broken spine against the marsh sky. Inside, a rusted lantern sways in the still air. A man in Crownwatch leathers lies slumped near the wall, skeletal hand still clutching a cracked compass that spins in place.  

**Notable Details:**  
- The compass hums softly when held.  
- A faint trail of boot prints circles the tower, stopping abruptly.  
- The body’s eyes are missing; sockets packed with black sand.  

**Checks:**  
- *Medicine DC 10:* dead for roughly 10 days, no sign of struggle.  
- *Arcana DC 14:* compass responds to runic energy, possibly a detector.  

**Clue:**  
Identifies **Salen Kett** and ties scouts to the nightmare influence.  

**Paranoia Prompt:**  
When the compass is held near a PC’s chest, it spins faster.

---

## Hunter’s Hut (Outskirts)
**Description:**  
Small cabin on the edge of the marsh. The roof sags, and faint candlelight flickers inside. A pile of tools sits by the door—recently used, but rusting.

**Read-Aloud:**  
> The cabin creaks as you approach. Inside, the air is thick with mildew and old smoke. A journal lies open on a table beside a bowl of grayish stew, its surface still rippling though there’s no breath of air.  

**Notable Details:**  
- The stew smells fresh but is cold as ice.  
- Journal pages mention “the dream-hollows” and villagers walking into fog.  
- Muddy tracks circle the house, fading near the waterline.  

**Checks:**  
- *Survival DC 13:* realize footprints loop back on themselves.  
- *Investigation DC 12:* find knife etched with the same rune.  

**Illusion:**  
At night, faint figures walk out of the woods toward the hut—then vanish before reaching it.  

**Clue:**  
Confirms that villagers are physically wandering into the dream realm.  

**Paranoia Prompt:**  
While reading the journal, handwriting slowly shifts to match the PC’s own.

---

## Child Survivor’s Home
**Description:**  
A two-room cottage filled with flickering candlelight. The faint smell of stew and burnt bread lingers. Two adults sit at a table, endlessly repeating the same gestures of eating and talking, their faces blank.

**Read-Aloud:**  
> The cottage door swings open to the quiet clink of cutlery. Two villagers sit at the table, lifting spoons to their mouths, chewing, swallowing—again and again. The food never changes. The child stands nearby, head tilted, humming softly to a wooden toy.  

**Notable Details:**  
- The child’s eyes flick between awake and closed in rhythm.  
- The toy hums faintly when touched, like a distant heartbeat.  

**Checks:**  
- *Insight DC 13:* realize child is faintly aware, senses something watching.  
- *Arcana DC 14:* toy carries residual planar energy.  

**Nightmare Interaction:**  
If toy is brought into the dream realm, it whispers: *“The Blackening comes.”*  

**Clue:**  
Evidence of families locked in perpetual time loops.  

**Paranoia Prompt:**  
The child briefly calls a PC by their **real name** (if any has been revealed in backstory).

---

## Rune Trail (Southern Marsh)
**Description:**  
Beyond the town, the marshland spreads in shallow pools and broken reeds. Stone markers rise intermittently, each carved with faint runes that hum under moonlight.

**Read-Aloud:**  
> The marsh spreads like a mirror of the night sky—pools of black water reflecting the crescent moon. At their center, old standing stones rise like broken teeth, marked with runes that pulse faintly as you approach.  

**Notable Details:**  
- Air temperature drops sharply near each stone.  
- Touching the runes causes faint dizziness or whispers.  
- The final set of stones forms a circle leading to a low-lying island.  

**Checks:**  
- *Arcana DC 16:* identify hybrid planar magic, not native to Velaris.  
- *Religion DC 15:* detect fragment of Shadowfell origin intertwined with divine residue.  

**Effect:**  
Resonates with chapel bone fragment. Touching both simultaneously produces a flash of inverted light—possible entry point to Nightmare Realm.  

**Clue:**  
Defines the entity’s anchor network; leads to final confrontation.  

**Paranoia Prompt:**  
Each time the wind shifts, the stones appear closer together.




# Point of Interest – Mayor’s House (Material Realm)

## Overview
The Mayor’s House was once the proud heart of Duskwatch: well-kept herb garden, polished windows, tidy stonework.  
Now it looks *lived in but empty*, like someone stepped out mid-task and never came back.

This location provides:
- Information about the last days before the curse  
- A new piece of the puzzle (journal, orders, or maps)  
- A sense of authority collapsing  
- A lead toward the Dreamer’s influence  

---

## Exterior Description
- The front gate hangs half-open, rocking gently despite no wind.  
- Fresh bootprints lead to the door, but **none leave**.  
- Herb beds are overgrown but recently watered — mechanically watered?  
- A lantern on the porch is still lit, oil nearly empty.  
- The front door is unlocked. The brass handle is warm.

**Optional check:**  
- *Survival DC 12:* Bootprints match scout-issued footwear.  
- *Investigation DC 14:* Another set of tracks — barefoot, small, dragging gait.

---

## Interior – General Feel
- Everything inside is **meticulously tidy** but positioned wrong:  
  - A chair facing the corner  
  - A half-eaten meal plated but untouched  
  - A broom resting against a wall with fresh dust pile beneath  
- The house smells faintly of **lavender and damp fog**.

### Atmosphere Cues
- Clocks inside the house show **different times**.  
- Pages of open books rustle though windows are closed.  
- Every portrait has the faces scratched out *except* the mayor’s.

---

## Area-by-Area Details

### **1. Entry Hall**
- Hooks hold a heavy coat, worn recently.  
- Visitor log book on a small table with the last entry:  
  > “Salen Kett — urgent report.”  
  (Dated the day before Duskwatch went silent.)

- A cracked hand mirror lies face-down (if players flip it → they see only themselves, no villagers).

### **2. Dining Room**
- Table set for two.  
- Candles burned down to nubs — identical burn height.  
- A wooden ladle rests beside a pot of cold stew; the stew was stirred recently.

**Clue**:  
- *Medicine DC 12:* There’s moonshade moss residue under one of the bowls → same shipment that went missing.

### **3. Study (Main Clue Room)**
This is the room your players will get the most out of.

#### What they see:
- Desk with open journal.  
- Map of Duskwatch pinned to wall with small copper pins (marking runic sites if they think to check).  
- Locked drawer (easy pick: DC 10 thieves’ tools or Dex).  
- Fireplace cold, but faint scent of burnt paper.

#### Possible Clues in Journal (choose what fits session pacing):
- **Entry 1:**  
  “The villagers complain of strange dreams. Many have stopped speaking. I will seek advice from the chapel.”
- **Entry 2:**  
  “Scout Kett returned… shaken, rambling about echoes and a well. He begged me to close the roads.”
- **Entry 3 (last):**  
  “They stand outside my windows at night. Not the villagers.  
  *They look like us.*  
  I hear them whispering…”  

(Scratched off violently after this line.)

#### Inside the locked drawer:
- A sealed **Crownwatch letter** informing the mayor that shipments are delayed and that scouts are being dispatched.  
  → Confirms the Crownwatch *did* follow proper procedures.  
- A **broken compass**, needle twitching even when held still.  
- A **half-burnt page** of runic shapes matching the ones from the rat.

---

### **4. Bedroom**
- Bed neatly made; indentation suggests someone slept only on the edge.  
- Footlocker with personal items (non-valuable):  
  - a mayor’s signet  
  - dried lavender  
  - six letters from family in Caer Hollow  
- Window curtains half-torn as if grabbed.

**Clue:**
- *Perception DC 14:* Under the bed is a child's drawing of the fountain with a scribbled black circle in the center.

---

### **5. Cellar (Optional Tension Area)**
- Wine racks, storage barrels.  
- Dirt floor shows **drag marks** leading to a blank stone wall.  
- The stones are perfectly still, but slightly warm to the touch.

This is NOT a portal — just atmospheric foreshadowing.

Optionally, a **sleepwalker villager** is down here, kneeling, rocking, whispering:
> “Don’t go down… don’t go down…”

If startled, he flails and attacks like any other sleepwalker.

---

## What the Mayor’s House Contributes to the Investigation

- **Mayor was aware** something was deeply wrong.  
- **Knew about the well** or at least knew a scout reported it.  
- **Mapped disturbances** (connects runes, chapel, storehouse, fountain).  
- **Found runic fragments**, confirming outside interference.  
- Confirms that villagers’ decline happened rapidly — *within days*.

---

## Optional Handout (Easy to Use)
Give the players this page from the journal:

> **Entry – Last Day**  
>  
> They are outside again.  
> But I do not hear footsteps.  
> Just whispers.  
> Like they are speaking through glass.  
>  
> I pray the scouts return with answers.  
> But deep down…  
> I fear we already sleep beneath the water.

---

## Quick Hooks for Players Leaving This POI
- Journal + map → the well becomes the strongest lead.  
- Crownwatch letter → clears suspicion and narrows focus.  
- Runic page → links to the chapel’s bone fragment.  
- Sleepwalker in cellar → confirms villagers are affected everywhere.


---

## DM Guidance
- Maintain a **rhythm of familiarity and distortion**—every area should *almost* feel normal before twisting.  
- Use **smells and temperature shifts** to cue supernatural changes.  
- Play with **repetition**: repeating sounds, words, movements.  
- End exploration when at least three major clues are uncovered or when night falls naturally, triggering the bleed.
