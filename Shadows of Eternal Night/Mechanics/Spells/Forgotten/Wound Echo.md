**Level**: 1st | **School**: Necromancy  
**Casting Time**: Reaction (when a creature within 60 feet takes damage)  
**Range**: 60 feet  
**Duration**: 1 round  
**Components**: V, S

**Effect**: The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or suffer **1d8 necrotic damage** at the start of its next turn as the wound reopens.

- **At Higher Levels**: When cast using a 2nd-level slot or higher, increase the delayed damage by 1d8 (max 4d8).

**Flavor**: Blood seeps from the same wound again as if struck twice. The flesh pulses once, then splits anew.
