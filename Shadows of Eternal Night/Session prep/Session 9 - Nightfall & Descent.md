*Shadows of Eternal Night — Act I*

## 🎯 Session Goals
- Allow final real-world POI checks (Hunter’s Hut if desired)
- Let dusk
- fall unnaturally quickly → forced Nightmare entry  
- Explore Nightmare Duskwatch **briefly but meaningfully**  
- Deliver the scout’s memory echo (vision of what happened)  
- Solve the **Scrap problem** (how he enters the Nightmare)  
- Follow the dream-path toward the well  
- Reach the **mini-dungeon entrance** (enter now or cliffhanger)

---

# PART I — Final Real-World Checks
Let the party finish anything left in the normal Duskwatch before night falls.
Likely target: **Hunter’s Hut**.

### If they visit the Hunter’s Hut
- Door slightly ajar, inside cold as if abandoned for days  
- A deep bootprint (Salen’s) half-filled with dried mud  
- Carving knife on the floor with dried blood  
- A half-burned letter mentioning “the others are missing”  
- **Loose stone** hides a journal scrap:  
  > “They walk south into the marsh in the night… toward the old ring…”  
- Faint matching mud shows a trail leading toward the marsh/well

### If they skip it  
Drop 1–2 clues elsewhere (inn kitchen, manor basement, tower stairs).

Keep this **fast** — don’t spend more than ~15–20 minutes here.

---

# PART II — Dusk Falls Too Fast
As they finish their investigations:

**Read-aloud (soft, unsettling):**
> The sky dims faster than it should.  
> Clouds drag across the sun like smudged ink.  
> A cold wind slides down the streets, and for a moment…  
> Duskwatch feels hollow again.

Anyone making an Arcana/Nature check instantly realizes:
- time is **compressing**  
- the veil is collapsing again  
- the Dreamer’s influence is strengthening

The Nightmare Realm overlays reality.

---

# PART III — Entering the Nightmare Again

### 📌 How they enter
**Everyone except Scrap** is drawn in immediately when the Nightmare overlays the world.

BUT —

## 🛠️ SCRAP Nightmare Entry (special rule)

Scrap cannot be magically put to sleep.  
So:

### Scrap sees:
- The party suddenly freeze mid-step
- Shadows lengthen unnaturally  
- Villagers begin walking past him through walls  
- A dark film spreads across the ground like oil  

**New mechanic: The Nightmare CONSUMES Scrap physically.**  
He is dragged into the realm the moment he:
- touches one of his companions  
- steps into a pool of shadow  
- or is struck by a Nightmare villager

Pick whichever fits the moment best.

**This solves the Scrap sleep immunity issue** and reinforces that Nightmare entry is not a sleep magic effect — it is planar bleedthrough.

---

# PART IV — Nightmare Duskwatch (Short Exploration)

Keep exploration **tight** (10–15 min).  
The goal is tension + direction, not wandering.

### Key Nightmare Changes
- Villagers walk in **straight lines**, ignoring doors/walls  
- Some head directly toward the **marsh**  
- Others drift toward the **well**  
- Their voices overlap in sync:
  > “He calls… beneath the water…”

### Hostile Behaviors
Use your Hostile NPC system:
- Only attack when approached / startled  
- Thrash in fear  
- Converge slowly if the party lingers too long  

### Light Oddities
Sprinkle 2–3:
- Reflections blink out of sync  
- Chairs rotate to face the party  
- Footsteps echo one extra time  
- Lantern flames point downward

---

# PART V — Scout Memory Vision

Trigger whenever the party investigates ANY corrupted feature (well, marsh path, villager trail).

### Vision Delivery
A flicker of cold light, then:

### **The Vision**
- Scouts following villagers into the southern marsh  
- Stone circle glowing faint blue  
- One scout pulled downward by **tendrils**  
- Another falling straight into the well  
- Third escaping, bleeding, collapsing near the inn (the corpse they found earlier)

Combined:
- explains the disappearances  
- directs them toward the well  
- connects to the runes  
- does NOT reveal void magic directly

---

# PART VI — Nightmare Path to the Well

### Build the pull:
- Villagers now moving in a single direction: **the well**  
- Some tumble in silently  
- Others walk to the edge and lean forward as if sleepwalking  
- One drops a lantern that falls through the surface like passing into ink

### Read-aloud near the well:
> The well water is no longer water.  
> A flat, mirror-black surface reflects none of you —  
> only the sky above, torn and wrong.  
> A whisper rises from below:  
> “Come down…”

This is their path.

---

# PART VII — Entering the Mini-Dungeon

You choose based on time:

---

## **Option A: Cliffhanger Finale**
As they look in:
> The surface cracks like glass.  
> A tendril grabs the stone rim—then another—  
> and gravity tilts violently sideways.

**CUT THE SESSION.**

---

## **Option B: Enter the Dungeon (Stop Before Encounter 1)**
Queek jumps or someone touches the surface →  
They fall through like breaking ice into cold black water, then land on stone.

Describe the dream-fog space and the shifting, warped reality of the dungeon…  
Stop before Encounter 1.

---

# 📌 Notes for You as DM
- Keep the Nightmare exploration **short**, atmospheric, and goal-oriented  
- Use hostile villagers sparingly  
- Deliver the scout vision clearly  
- Use the well as the anchor point of the session  
- Decide mid-session whether to end on a cliffhanger or descend
