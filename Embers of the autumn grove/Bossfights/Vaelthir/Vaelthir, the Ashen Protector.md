### **Vaelthir, the Ashen Protector**

_Huge Fey (Corrupted Guardian), Chaotic Neutral_

Once the **eternal guardian** of the **Autumn Glade**, **Vaelthir** has been **twisted by Emberblight**, his once-vibrant form now wreathed in smoldering flames. His cracked bark glows like embers, and his eyes burn with a flickering rage. Though corruption fuels his wrath, remnants of his former self remain buried beneath the fire.

---

**AC**: 16 (Natural Armor)  
**HP**: 150 (12d12 + 60)  
**Speed**: 30 ft.

---

### **Abilities**

#### **Emberblight Aura (Passive)**

All creatures within **10 feet** of Vaelthir take **2 fire damage** at the start of their turn. This increases to **4 fire damage** in **Phase 2**.

#### **Corrupting Roots (Recharge 5–6)**

Vaelthir strikes the ground, causing **burning roots** to lash out.

- **Range:** 15 feet (all creatures in range)
- **Effect:** Creatures in range must make a **DC 16 Strength saving throw** or be **restrained**.
- **Restrained creatures take 1d4 fire damage at the start of their turn.**
- **Escape:** A creature can retry the save at the end of each of its turns.

#### **Titanic Resilience (Passive)**

Vaelthir has **advantage on saving throws against being knocked prone or pushed**.

---

### **Actions**

#### **Multiattack**

Vaelthir makes two melee attacks.

#### **Cleave (Melee Weapon Attack)**

- **Attack:** +7 to hit
- **Reach:** 10 ft.
- **Hit:** **2d6 + 6 slashing damage** (avg. 13)

#### **Flamebound Strike (Recharge 4–6)**

- **Attack:** +7 to hit
- **Reach:** 10 ft.
- **Hit:** **3d8 fire damage** (avg. 13).
- The target must make a **DC 14 Dexterity saving throw** or be **ignited**, taking **1d6 fire damage per turn** until they use an action to extinguish the flames.

### **Lair Actions (1d2/round, Initiative 20)**

1. **Falling Branches** _(Fire Shockwave)_ **Phase 1 & 2**
    
    - A wave of fire causes **flaming branches** to rain down in three 10-ft. radius areas.
    - **DC 14 Dexterity saving throw** or take **1d8 fire damage**.
2. **Fire Spires** _(Rising Flames)_ **Phase 2**
    
    - Fire erupts from the ground in **two 5-ft. radius areas**.
    - Any creature starting their turn in the fire takes **1d8 fire damage**.
3. **Scorching Roots** _(Blazing Growth)_ **Phase 2**
    
    - A **20-foot line of burning roots** emerges from the ground.
    - **DC 16 Strength saving throw** or take **1d6 fire damage** and be **restrained**.
4. **Corrupted Roots**
    
    - Summons 1 root within 60ft of Vaelthir
