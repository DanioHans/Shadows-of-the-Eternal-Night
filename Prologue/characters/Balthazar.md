## Race: Human

## Class: Wizard (School of Evocation)

## Background: Sage

---

### Appearance

- **Age:** Older, in his late 50s
- **Hair:** Grey, thinning, often covered by a simple hat
- **Eyes:** Blue
- **Clothing:** Simple robes with arcane symbols, slightly worn from travel

---

### Backstory

Balthazar is a scholar of the arcane, traveling to learn and expand his magical knowledge. He often performs small magical feats at festivals to earn his keep.

---

### Skills

- **Arcana**
- **History**
- **Investigation**

---

### Abilities and Features

- **Strength:** 8 (-1)
- **Dexterity:** 14 (+2)
- **Constitution:** 13 (+1)
- **Intelligence:** 16 (+3)
- **Wisdom:** 12 (+1)
- **Charisma:** 10 (0)

#### Wizard Features

- **Spellcasting:** Ability to cast wizard spells using Intelligence.
- **Arcane Recovery:** Once per day when you finish a short rest, you can recover a number of expended spell slots equal to or less than half your wizard level (rounded up).

#### School of Evocation Features

- **Sculpt Spells:** When you cast an evocation spell that affects other creatures you can see, you can choose a number of them equal to 1 + the spell’s level. The chosen creatures automatically succeed on their saving throws against the spell, and they take no damage if they would normally take half damage on a successful save.

#### Racial Traits

- **Variant Human Traits:**
    - **Skill Proficiency:** +1 to two ability scores of your choice (Dexterity and Intelligence).
    - **Skill:** Gain proficiency in one skill (Investigation).
    - **Feat:** Gain one feat of your choice (Magic Initiate - Warlock).
