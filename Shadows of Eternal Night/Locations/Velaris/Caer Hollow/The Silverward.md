# Silverward

**Location**: Western district of [[Caer Hollow]], near the cliffs overlooking the river  
**District Type**: Religious and spiritual center  
**Known For**: Grand temples, shrines, healing sanctuaries, and pilgrimage routes

---

## Overview

The **Silverward** is Caer Hollow’s sacred heart — a district woven with reverence, incense smoke, and echoing bells. Built around the natural curves of the western rise, it gazes down upon the river that glistens at sunset, giving the ward its name. Followers of all pantheons are welcome here, though **three major temples** dominate the skyline.

Silverward is a place of quiet reflection, even amid the bustle. Priests, pilgrims, and spirit-touched wanderers walk in harmony. Guards are respectful here, and even rogues mind their step.

---

## Major Temples

- **Temple of the Hollow Flame** – A large structure devoted to the **state-sanctioned flame**, a spiritual symbol tied to the kingdom’s endurance and legacy. Often treated as a civic temple as well.
- **Moonmaiden’s Rise** – A beautiful crescent-shaped temple to **Selûne**, constructed of pale stone and glass, where **Gawain Brightlance** once trained. Offers nightly prayer services and guidance to the lost.
- **The Verdant Cloister** – A temple of **Chauntea** and other nature deities, surrounded by layered gardens and quiet pools. Maintained by druids and herbalists.

---

## Minor Shrines and Orders

- Shrines to **Ilmater**, **Tymora**, **Oghma**, and **Sarenrae** are scattered in shaded courtyards and alley chapels.
- The **Sanctum of the Bound Word** – A cloistered, reclusive order that believes in divine silence and mental clarity.
- The **Gravetide Lantern** – A shrine to Kelemvor and the honored dead. Offers blessings for the recently departed.

---

## Notable Locations

- **Pilgrim’s Fountain** – A wide marble fountain with steps where travelers rest and leave votive offerings.
- **The Bell Path** – A winding garden path with hanging silver bells that chime as visitors pass through — said to calm the soul.
- **Sanctuary of the Gentle Hand** – A large healing hall maintained by the Temple of the Hollow Flame. Open to the public.

---

## District Character

- Quiet, sacred, respectful. Even during festivals, there’s a sense of calm.
- Strong **divine presence** — lingering magical wards provide comfort, clarity, and warmth.
- Locals and priests are **welcoming**, though faithless wanderers may feel judged.
- Street performers and charlatans are politely escorted out by the **Temple Guard**.

---

## Security and Influence

- **Temple Guard** (sub-branch of City Guard) maintains order, trained in non-lethal takedowns and divine suppression.
- High-ranking clergy hold **civic influence** — a few sit on **advisory councils**.
- Rumors suggest one of the shrines has been visited in secret by someone from the palace.

---

## Campaign Relevance

- [[Merios]] may visit the **Moonmaiden’s Rise**, where Gawain once trained.
- The party may return here for **healing**, **divine research**, or to investigate **strange omens** later in the campaign.
- Silverward will **react differently** to shadow incursions depending on deity — some may resist, others may fall silent.
