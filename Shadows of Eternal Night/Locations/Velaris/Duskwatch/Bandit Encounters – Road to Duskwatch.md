Bandits are displaced villagers/refugees, many from Duskwatch or Ashbarrow. They are desperate rather than purely evil. These encounters highlight unrest in Velaris and the consequences of the Shadowfell bleed.  

---

## Option A: Bandit Ambush (Post-Briarford, Roadside)

**Location**: Forest edge / narrow bend in the road, ~2 hours south of Briarford if the party avoids the ferry.  

**Setup**: A fallen tree blocks the road. 4–6 bandits emerge from cover, armed with spears and bows. Their gear is scavenged, not professional.  

**Dialogue (if allowed)**  
- “Coin or food. We’ve got children starving back home.”  
- “Duskwatch is cursed. We had no choice.”  

**Tactics**: They try to scare rather than kill. If 1–2 are downed, the rest scatter.  

**Outcomes**  
- **Combat** → survivors flee, leaving behind scraps (worth little).  
- **Negotiation** → party can hand over 1–2 days of rations, 5–10 gp, or promise help in Duskwatch. Bandits give rumors in return.  
- **Rumors**:  
  - “The reeds whisper names at night.”  
  - “We saw figures walking beside us in the fog — ourselves.”  

---

## Option B: Bandit Camp (Duskmire Route)

**Location**: 1 day into the Duskmire, off the sinking road. Makeshift huts on raised planks, small watchfire.  

**Setup**: 6–8 bandits (weaker than normal: some injured, some sick from marsh fever). They have captured a **Duskwatch villager** and looted a **scout’s satchel**.  

**Clues**:  
- Satchel bears insignia of [[Missing Scouts – Duskwatch#Salen Kett]]. Contains a partial patrol log.  
- Villager is traumatized, speaks of villagers who “stand in their doorways, eyes empty.”  

**Choices**  
- **Rescue captive** → villagers of Briarford will thank PCs.  
- **Fight bandits** → clear the camp, gain satchel + 20 gp worth of goods.  
- **Negotiate** → give bandits safe passage north; they reveal they saw a “shadow double” of one another in the marsh before fleeing Duskwatch.  

**Combat Flavor**: Bandits fight dirty: mud pits, reed blinds, and crude spears. Their morale is low — they surrender if half are taken out.  

---

## Usage
- **If party skips Briarford ferry** → run Ambush encounter on the road.  
- **If party takes Duskmire route** → run Bandit Camp encounter.  
- Either way, this thread emphasizes desperation and provides additional rumors of the Shadowfell bleed.
