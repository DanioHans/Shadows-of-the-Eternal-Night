
The Hollow Dreamer manipulates fears and memories, twisting reality. The following whispers, visions, and false clues are tools to make players uneasy. Mix them sparingly; don’t confirm what’s true or false. Even after the Dreamer is slain, some of these may linger in their minds.  

---

## General Whispers (ambient, traveling or in town)
- “You should’ve gone to Ironhold.” *(When: travel through Briarford or Duskmire mists)*  
- “The Captain sent you here to die.” *(When: near Crownwatch-related evidence)*  
- “One of you will not leave this town alive.” *(When: someone is injured or exhausted)*  
- “Your parents don’t even know where you are.” *(Directed at Merios, when seeing families in town)*  
- “The Crown will burn in black fire.” *(When: passing Market Square at night)*  
- “Even Verrath cannot save you.” *(When: discussing runes or Arcanum)*  
- “You’re already dead. Haven’t you noticed?” *(When: a PC takes damage but no villager reacts)*  

---

## NPC Slip-Ups (responsive survivors or townsfolk)
- Innkeeper mutters: “The runes bind us all… even you,” then blinks and denies it. *(Inn conversation)*  
- Hunter stares at a PC and whispers: “Your face is different here.” *(At hunter’s hut)*  
- Herbalist says: “I saw you yesterday, but you were already drowned.” *(While handing herbs)*  
- Guard at innfire: “Your orders were to stay away… weren’t they?” *(During downtime talk)*  
- Child survivor: “Mother says Verrath is watching me.” *(Randomly, then claims she never said it)*  

---

## Shadow-Double Taunts (market square/nightmare)
- **Samson**: “The Arcanum laughs behind your back. You’re just their errand boy.”  
- **Ivor**: “Do you even remember which lies you’ve told? Or are you starting to believe them?”  
- **Merios**: “Wings or chains, you’ll always be a demon.”  
- **Queek**: “Your kin already crawl beneath the Crown. Do you hear them scratching?”  
- **Kyron**: “Your magic isn’t yours. It’s wound tight around you like a noose.”  

*(When: at Market Square at night or during Hollow Dreamer battle)*  

---

## False Clues (entity projections)
- Runes briefly resemble the **Crownwatch insignia**. *(When PCs inspect rune trail)*  
- A villager mutters “Alura” before collapsing. *(Hunter’s hut or innkeeper)*  
- A shipment crate appears gnawed by rats, but blinks back intact. *(Warehouse search)*  
- A patrol log scribble: “The Arcanum ordered this.” (illusion only visible on first read). *(Scout station)*  
- A fisher’s shadow stretches to 30 feet and forms Verrath’s silhouette. *(Riverside, dusk)*  

---

## Nightmare Realm Distortions
- Villagers bake bread that bleeds when cut. *(In dream realm inn)*  
- Children play with dolls that look like PCs. *(In dream realm homes)*  
- A PC’s reflection whispers: “You will be the one who fails them.” *(Any pool, mirror, or window)*  
- Glimpse of Caer Hollow’s Market Square engulfed in black fire. *(High DC Wis/Arcana check during dream investigation)*  
- Whispered syllables: “Na…ash…” *(Stone circle, dream realm)*  

---

## Travel-Specific Paranoia
- **Briarford**: Reeve accidentally calls a PC by the name of a dead relative.  
- **Duskmire Path**: Insect swarm shapes itself into Crownwatch heraldry.  
- **Anchor Port**: Ferryman refuses coin, saying: “You already paid me… last week.”  

---

## DM Guidance
- **One per scene**: drop no more than one paranoia effect per significant scene to keep impact strong.  
- **Vary delivery**: whispers (no source), NPC slips, visual distortions, reflections, shadow-doubles.  
- **Never confirm**: after entity is slain, leave players doubting whether some were foreshadowing of future arcs (Verrath, Crownwatch, Blackening).  
- **Escalate**: start subtle (off-hand whispers), grow bolder in dream realm (visions, doubles), and peak during Hollow Dreamer fight (direct taunts).
