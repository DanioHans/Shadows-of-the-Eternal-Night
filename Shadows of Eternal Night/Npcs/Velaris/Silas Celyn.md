**Race:** Human  
**Class:** Sorcerer  
**Affiliation:** Celyn Farmstead  
**Role:** Scholar of Monsters, Log Keeper, Co-guardian of Merios  

## Overview
Silas is the more scientific and reserved of the Celyn twins. While Damon handles the interpersonal and intuitive aspects of monster rehabilitation, Silas is the meticulous documentarian and experimenter. His long-running log entries about Merios' development have become a minor academic curiosity among fringe monster researchers.

He is **highly intelligent but socially stiff**, and often views emotions as puzzles to be solved. Despite this, he has grown fond of Merios in his own way, celebrating her smallest victories with quiet pride.

## Notable Log Entries
- **Entry 17**: Noted Merios’ food preferences and began behavior-based reward training.
- **Entry 31**: Recorded her first voluntary spoken words: “Cook this. Please?”
- **Entry 173**: Observed her participating in daily farm life with signs of joy and mischief.

## Personality
- Methodical, dry-witted, prefers data over dialogue.
- Often critical but not unkind.
- Has a deep love for structure and progress.

## Relationship with Merios
- Acts as a researcher and guardian.
- Sees Merios’ development as proof that even fiends can change.
- Agreed to allow her visits to Caer Hollow under strict conditions.

## Possible Use
- Source of documented information about Merios’ infernal nature.
- Could become suspicious of unusual magical changes if Merios begins to regress.
- Might request the party’s help if Merios disappears or exhibits new signs of corruption.
