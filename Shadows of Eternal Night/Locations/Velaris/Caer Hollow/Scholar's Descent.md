# The Scholar’s Descent

**Location**: [[Velaris]] — Inner Tier, east of the central plateau  
**District Type**: Academic district surrounding the [[Royal Arcanum of Velaris]]  
**Known For**: Magical studies, elite dormitories, artifact vaults, strange phenomena

---

## Overview

The **Scholar’s Descent** earns its name from both its **geography** and its **legacy**. Winding down gently from the High Courts of Velaris, the district houses the prestigious [[Royal Arcanum of Velaris]], flanked by libraries, dormitories, research annexes, and merchant stalls selling rare arcane goods.

The area is cloaked in a **constant low hum of magic** — illusion wards shimmer faintly at dusk, unseen familiars flit between towers, and the occasional planar ripple can be felt when the veil thins. It is **hallowed ground for the learned** — and a territory fiercely guarded by faculty and magical constructs alike.

---

## Vibe and Culture

- **Elitist and self-contained** — the people here view themselves as stewards of knowledge.
- Robed students, arcane apprentices, and stoic researchers fill the paths.
- Many locals feel **watched** — either by the Arcanum’s scrying systems or its professors.
- Public visitors are **tolerated**, but viewed with subtle condescension.

---

## Key Locations

- **[[Royal Arcanum of Velaris]]** – The kingdom’s foremost arcane institution.
- **The Hall of Threads** – A circular observatory for planar research. Always smells faintly of burning salt.
- **Scroll’s End** – A tavern just outside the Arcanum gates. Popular with rebellious students.
- **Lattice Market** – Small but expensive vendor stalls selling spell reagents, magical curios, and elixirs.
- **Glyphspire Annex** – A tall marble building where instructors live or host visiting researchers.
- **The Silent Crossings** – A shrine-like path inscribed with wards of silence — respected by all students as a place to reflect before exams.

---

## Inhabitants

- **Students** – From all over Velaris and beyond. Most are well-off, though a few are scholarship-bound.
- **Instructors** – Powerful mages, often eccentric or reclusive.
- **Constructs** – Silent magical guardians patrol key areas, designed to disable rather than kill.
- **Vendors** – Reagent merchants and bookbinders, often former students or failed applicants.

---

## Security

- The district is watched by **construct sentinels** and **magical scrywards** maintained by the Arcanum.
- Any spellcasting in public is instantly analyzed and flagged by an **automated magical ledger**.
- Guards are rarely seen — **magical enforcement is preferred**.

---

## Secrets and Rumors

- **Secret passageways** under the Arcanum are said to link directly to the old royal crypts.
- The **eastern reflecting pool** occasionally shows visions of other timelines.
- A **disappeared student** is still rumored to whisper from the Silent Crossings.
- **Verrath** is said to have reshaped certain areas of the campus in line with “forgotten principles.”

---

## Use in Session

- Serves as the **starting location** for [[Samson]] and [[Kyron]] in Session 1.
- May house key information, scrolls, or forbidden lore for later acts.
- Certain NPCs such as **Verrath**, Council members, or faculty may be found wandering the area.
- A **contrast to the chaos** of the Market Square — controlled, ordered, but subtly dangerous.
