> A young Clockwork Soul Sorcerer seeking the origins of his nature, carrying emotional instability that may disrupt his magic as the stakes rise.

---

## 🎭 Backstory Summary

- **Kyron** grew up in an **orphanage**, where he was well cared for and formed positive relationships with both caregivers and other orphans.
- Early on, he showed a natural **aptitude for magic**, which led him to the **local library**, where he studied different magical disciplines.
- Visiting wizards occasionally mentored him when they noticed his dedication.
- At **age 18**, Kyron left the orphanage to **seek answers about his nature** — why he is both a **Tiefling** and a **Sorcerer**.
- Kyron remains emotionally connected to the orphanage and will visit whenever nearby.

---

## 🕊️ Personality / RP Notes

- Initially reserved around strangers; will speak when topics involve **magic** or related knowledge.
- Protective and empathetic toward those he grows close to — likely to form **emotional bonds with the party over time**.
- Carries an underlying **guilt** or **self-expectation** to be "good enough" to protect others through magic.

---

## ✨ Clockwork Soul Theme

- Clockwork Soul sorcery symbolizes Kyron’s inner **desire for control and order**, contrasted by the instability of his emotions and his tiefling heritage.
- Visually, his magic may feature **geometric patterns**, **clockwork glyphs**, and **hints of infernal energy** when stressed.

---

## 🧩 Unique Mechanic (Work in Progress)

### Core Concept:
- When **party members are wounded**, Kyron’s guilt causes a **chance of magical instability**.
- The closer his emotional bond to the wounded ally, the stronger the effect.

### Proposed Mechanics (Pending Simplification):
- **Trigger**: When a party member is below a % HP threshold, Kyron rolls when casting a spell.
- If triggered, the spell may **randomly change** into another spell he knows of the same or lower level.
- Both offensive and support spells can be affected.
- The effect should **scale** with:
  - Severity of the ally’s injuries.
  - Kyron’s current emotional state toward that ally.
  - Kyron’s personal confidence (which could improve with character growth).

### Development Notes:
- Current version is somewhat complex; DM + player to **simplify before final integration**.
- Option: Start with *minor visual/auditory distortions* before full spell instability kicks in.
- Option: Use a simple **d100 or d20 trigger roll** rather than complex scaling.

## 🤝 NPC Reactions

- Tiefling nature will draw mixed reactions — wary but not hostile in most circles.
- Certain **mages** and **scholars** may see great potential in him.
- Others may fear the **instability** in his magic, especially if it manifests publicly.

---

## 🎭 Roleplay Hooks

- Emotional instability tied to **ally harm**.
- Possible tension between his **clockwork order** and **inherent chaotic origin**.
- Guilt as a growth path: may learn **self-acceptance** or **magical mastery** to mitigate instability.
- Possible temptation from **Shadowfell corruption** — order and guilt can both be manipulated by dark forces.
