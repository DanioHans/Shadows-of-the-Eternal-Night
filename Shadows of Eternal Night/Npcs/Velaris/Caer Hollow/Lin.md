## Overview
Lin is a sharp-eyed, fast-talking informant operating in the shadows of **Caer Hollow**. Known to criminals and smugglers as a reliable source of information — for a price — she plays both sides of the law when it suits her. Lin is Ivor's alleged contact and the reason he is at the market during the events of Session 1.

## Appearance
- Wiry frame, quick movements
- Short-cropped black hair, mismatched gloves
- Always watching — even when she seems relaxed

## Personality
- Paranoid, always scanning her surroundings
- Speaks in hushed tones, trusts few
- Has a dry wit, often teasing others’ lack of caution
- Values leverage above loyalty

## Role in Session 1
- Set to meet Ivor in the market crowd under the pretense of having “a job” or “new information”
- Once the chaos starts, she slips away without warning — Ivor may choose to pursue or focus on the fight
- This creates a narrative thread for Ivor to investigate later
