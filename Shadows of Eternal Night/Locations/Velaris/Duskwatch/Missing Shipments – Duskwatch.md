All shipments originate in Duskwatch and were meant for Caer Hollow. They disappear along the road or never leave town at all.

---

## 1. Moonshade Moss
- **Contents**: 3 crates of dried moss bundles.  
- **Purpose**: Healing potion reagent.  
- **Route**: Apothecary in Duskwatch → Bastion alchemists.  
- **Last Seen**: Overdue 2 weeks; never reported leaving town.  
- **Personnel**: 1 apothecary apprentice + 1 guard. Both missing.  

---

## 2. Timber
- **Contents**: 2 wagonloads of raw timber beams.  
- **Purpose**: Bastion construction & siege equipment.  
- **Route**: Duskwatch lumberyard → Caer Hollow west gate.  
- **Last Seen**: Day 9, left lumberyard.  
- **Personnel**: 3 dwarves + 2 mules. Wagon found overturned (Day 3 travel encounter).  

---

## 3. Salted Fish & Grain
- **Contents**: 5 barrels of salted river fish + 6 sacks of grain.  
- **Purpose**: Food supply for Bastion kitchens.  
- **Route**: Duskwatch docks → Bastion quartermaster.  
- **Last Seen**: Day 11, loaded at riverside warehouse. Never left.  
- **Personnel**: Fisherman clan of 3 siblings. No word since.  

---

## 4. Silver Ore
- **Contents**: 4 crates of unrefined ore.  
- **Purpose**: Magical reagents & currency backing.  
- **Route**: Duskwatch smeltery → Crownwatch vaults.  
- **Last Seen**: Day 10, just outside Duskwatch.  
- **Personnel**: 1 wagon driver + 2 Bastion scouts (see [[Missing Scouts – Duskwatch#Tobias Hurn]]).  

---

## 5. Lantern Oil Barrels
- **Contents**: 6 barrels of oil.  
- **Purpose**: Fuel for Caer Hollow watchtowers.  
- **Route**: Duskwatch storehouse → Bastion.  
- **Last Seen**: Day 8, at border post half a day north of Duskwatch.  
- **Personnel**: Caravan master + senior scout [[Missing Scouts – Duskwatch#Salen Kett]]. Both missing.
