> An artificial lumberjack given sentience — now exploring the world with wide-eyed wonder and a faithful fox companion.

---

## 🎭 Backstory Summary

- Originally created as a **logging construct** in one of Tharion’s ancient forests.
- Over time, through exposure to **forest life** and **animal companionship**, developed **self-awareness** and empathy.
- Formed a strong bond with a **fox companion** — this represents both a literal friendship and a symbol of his new free will.
- Abandoned his original purpose and now **wanders the world**, seeking understanding.
- Emotionally innocent — simple pleasures and new experiences fascinate him.
- His memory of his creator or original workshop is vague — either forgotten or intentionally erased.

---

## 🕊️ Personality / RP Notes

- Childlike curiosity — things like **markets, temples, books, music, and simple crafts** are all novel experiences.
- Deep bond with **fox companion**; highly protective of it.
- No current sense of **right/wrong** in complex moral terms — learns through observation and party interaction.
- Likely to form **strong attachments** quickly.

---

## 🗺️ Reason to be in Caer Hollow

**Not defined yet** — but very easy to explain:
- Following **merchant caravan** or **woodsman** into the city.
- Pursuing rumors of **other constructs** or ancient magic.
- Accompanying a **forest druid** or hunter who visited the capital.
- Drawn by tales of the **city’s grand library** or scholars (even if he doesn’t fully understand it).

**Player should define this before campaign start** — you can offer these options.

---

## 🧠 Mechanics & Balance

- Ranger 4 — **Beastmaster**.
- Melee primary (Battleaxe), ranged secondary (Longbow).
- Very sturdy frontline with **AC 18** and Constructed Resilience.
- Fox companion: **narrative first**, DM discretion on whether it gains combat utility.
- Excellent tracking, survival, and **anti-undead** capabilities (Undead favored enemy fits *Shadows of Eternal Night* perfectly).

---

## 🤝 NPC Reactions

- Some **fear** or **superstition** from common folk — Warforged are rare in Tharion.
- Scholars and mages may be **fascinated** by him.
- Forest dwellers or **Resistance agents** may see him as a valuable ally against the undead.
- Could attract the interest of **Forgotten Magic users** — constructs and ancient magics interact dangerously.

---

## 🎭 Roleplay Hooks

- Self-discovery arc — he is literally **learning how to be a person**.
- Possible hidden **origin mystery** — why was he able to gain sentience?
- Bond with fox can be an **emotional anchor** in future dark times (great for corruption drama).
- Could be **targeted for reprogramming** or **claimed** by factions aware of his nature.
- Natural **curiosity about the Shadowfell** and undead — his instincts tell him they are wrong, but he lacks deeper understanding.
