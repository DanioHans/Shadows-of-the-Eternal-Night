### **The Fey Gate Puzzle**

#### **Phase 1: Unlocking the Paper**

- **Setup:**  
    The pedestal in front of the Fey Gate is **empty**, with faint runes etched into its surface. A riddle is inscribed in Sylvan:
    
    - _“What is harder to catch the faster you run?”_
- **Solution:**
    
    - The answer is **breath**.
    - Players solve it by **blowing on the pedestal** or saying the answer aloud.
- **Outcome:**  
    The pedestal shimmers, and a **thin sheet of paper** materializes, glowing faintly with Fey magic.
    

---

#### **Phase 2: Folding the Paper**

- **Setup:**  
    The paper shows **two circles** separated by space, with Sylvan script reading:
    
    - _“union creates the way forward.”_
- **Solution:**  
    Players must **physically fold the paper** so the circles overlap. When aligned, hidden runes appear, forming the message:
    
    - _“Balance opens the way.”_
- **Outcome:**  
    The Fey Gate activates, glowing brightly as a shimmering portal opens.
    

---

### **Optional Details**

- **Hints for the Riddle:**
    
    - **Insight or Arcana (DC 12):** Recognize the riddle’s connection to breath.
    - Nyssa might say: _“The answer lies in something fleeting yet essential.”
    - **Arcana (DC 12):** Recognize that the Fey often use layering as part of their puzzles.
    - **Nyssa’s Prompt:** If players hesitate too long, she might softly suggest:
    - _“The Fey love to bring opposites together. Perhaps there is a way to close the distance?”_
- **Mistakes with the Paper:**
    
    - **First Mistake:**
    - A faint laugh echoes in the air, and colorful sparks burst harmlessly around the group.
    - **Second Mistake:**
    - A minor Fey prank occurs:
        - A player’s hair turns a bright color.
        - A harmless illusion manifests, like small woodland animals running across the group’s feet.
        - a voice says "only one more chance"
    - **Third Mistake:**
    -  the **paper vanishes**(_“It seems like the Fey became impatient.”_).
