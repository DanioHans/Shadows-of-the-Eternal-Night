# High Arcanist Verrath

**Role**: Arcane Advisor to King Halvar Thorne  
**Title**: High Arcanist of Velaris  
**Location**: Primarily within the upper chambers of the [[Cradle Tower]] or advising at [[Crownwatch Bastion]]  
**Tone**: Soft-spoken, authoritative, veiled in mystery  

---

## 🧠 Overview

Verrath is the **silent pulse of arcane policy in Velaris**, a mage whose rise to power was marked not by fame, but by *absence*. For over a decade, he advised the Royal Arcanum from the shadows — only stepping into public recognition following the sudden disappearance of his predecessor during the last planar alignment.

He is **trusted by the king**, though not beloved. His knowledge is vast, his speech deliberate, and his presence unnerving. While he holds no formal military or religious command, **none act against him without consequence**. Even the Night Wardens tread lightly.

---

## 🧑‍🎓 Demeanor & Personality

- Always wears layered formal robes of the Royal Arcanum, though rarely adorned with insignia.
- Never raises his voice. **Pauses are weaponized.**
- Known to address individuals by name even if never introduced.
- Has a habit of looking slightly past those he speaks to, as if observing something else.
- Shows mild curiosity rather than frustration or joy — **unreadable yet engaged.**

---

## 🧩 Secrets (Known to DM)

- Verrath has encountered or been influenced by **Nagashian echoes**, whether through corrupted arcana or intentional contact.
- He believes **Velaris must “prepare”**, though his methods are dubious at best.
- He is **not fully loyal** to the king anymore — his allegiance is toward what he calls “preservation beyond collapse.”
- Keeps **private magical records** in a sealed vault beneath the Cradle Tower. These are **inaccessible to all others**, even the king.
