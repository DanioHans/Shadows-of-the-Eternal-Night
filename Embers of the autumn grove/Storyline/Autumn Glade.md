### **Autumn Glade Overview**

#### **Size and Layout**

- **Size:** Roughly **half a mile in radius**, the glade is a medium-sized, self-contained sanctuary within the Feywild.
- **Central Features:**
    - A **sacred clearing** at its heart, where Vaelthir once stood vigil. This clearing is now a scorched and chaotic battleground.
    - Surrounding the clearing are clusters of **ancient trees**, their bark a vibrant mix of gold, orange, and red.
    - Small **streams** wind through the grove, their waters now partially evaporated or tainted with ash.
- **Paths:** Natural trails lead out of the glade, marked by **Fey runes** that once served as wards but now flicker weakly.

---

#### **Inhabitants**

- **Verdant Wardens:**
    
    - The primary guardians of the glade, these Fey-like entities serve as its protectors.
    - Normally blending seamlessly into the environment, they now stand on constant guard due to the corruption.
- **Fey Creatures:**
    
    - Small Fey spirits, such as **sprites**, **pixies**, and **will-o’-wisps**, typically flit about, tending to plants and maintaining the glade's magic.
    - Many have fled or gone into hiding since the Emberblight began spreading.
- **Flora and Fauna:**
    
    - Plants include **autumnal vines**, **golden moss**, and trees with bark that glows faintly at night.
    - Wildlife like **foxes**, **rabbits**, and **songbirds** once thrived here but are now either corrupted or absent.

---

#### **Style and Aesthetic**

- The glade is an **autumnal paradise**, a place of quiet beauty and renewal.
- **Colors:** Dominated by deep reds, fiery oranges, and shimmering golds.
- **Atmosphere:** Before the corruption, the glade exuded warmth, with a crisp, fragrant breeze carrying the scents of fallen leaves and fertile earth.

---

#### **Secrets and Mysteries**

- **Hidden Fey Runes:**
    
    - Scattered across the glade, runes tied to the **Feywild’s magic** may hold clues about Vaelthir’s corruption or offer minor boons (if activated by magic).
- **Ancient Roots (Hidden Beneath the Clearing):**
    
    - Below the central clearing lies an **ancient network of roots**, the lifeblood of the grove. These roots were meant to sustain Vaelthir and the glade but have become tainted by Emberblight.
    - Adventurers investigating these roots may learn that severing their corruption could weaken Vaelthir.
- **Old Relics:**
    
    - Relics of past Fey visitors, such as **enchanted tools** or **forgotten talismans**, might be hidden within the grove, buried by time.

---

#### **Backstory**

- The Autumn Glade was a gift from the **Fey Courts**, created as a place of balance between life and decay. It has stood for **centuries** as a sanctuary where mortals and Fey could seek renewal.
- Vaelthir was appointed as its protector during a time of **great turmoil**, tasked with ensuring no one disrupted the grove’s delicate harmony.

---

### **State of the Grove After the First Encounter**

#### **Environmental Damage**

- The central clearing, where the battle took place, is **scarred and charred**:
    - **Flames** have scorched the ancient trees. Some are **smoldering**, their leaves reduced to ash.
    - The once-soft forest floor is now **blackened and cracked**, littered with debris from fallen branches.

#### **Magic Fading**

- The glade’s **magical wards and Fey runes** flicker weakly, unable to repel the corruption spreading from Vaelthir.

#### **Wildlife and Inhabitants**

- The **Verdant Wardens** are visibly strained, with cracks in their bark-like skin showing where the Emberblight has tried to take hold.
- The few remaining **Fey spirits** hide in the shadows, reluctant to emerge.

#### **How "Wounded" is the Grove?**

- The glade is **not beyond saving**, but it is on the brink of collapse. Without intervention, the Emberblight will spread, turning the entire glade into a smoldering wasteland within days.

---

### **How to Use This in Roleplay**

1. **Describe the Damage Visually:**
    
    - As players explore, highlight the **contrast between the beauty of untouched areas** and the devastation in the central clearing.
2. **Give Players Something to Investigate:**
    
    - Use **Fey runes**, **ancient roots**, or **hidden relics** to reward players for exploration or successful skill checks.
3. **Emphasize the Stakes:**
    
    - Through NPCs like Nyssa or the Verdant Wardens, stress the importance of restoring the glade and stopping the Emberblight before it’s too late.
