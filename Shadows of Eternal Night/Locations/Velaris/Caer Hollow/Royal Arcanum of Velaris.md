# 📌 Royal Arcanum of Velaris

> The prestigious heart of magical scholarship in the kingdom — now quietly slipping toward darker currents.

---

## 🏛️ Overview

The **Royal Arcanum of Velaris** is the foremost — and only truly prestigious — academy of magic in the Kingdom of [[Tharion]].  
Founded centuries ago by royal decree, it serves both as an elite institution of magical learning and as a political arm of the Crown.

Only the most promising candidates are admitted, typically from noble families or through exceptional magical aptitude. The Academy maintains an image of scholarly excellence, though it is increasingly viewed as an **arrogant, insular elite** by the common folk.

Graduates of the Arcanum are respected throughout Tharion — many become court mages, private scholars, or influential arcanists.

---

## 🎓 Philosophy & Curriculum

- The Academy rejects rigid division into traditional **schools of magic**.  
Instead, it teaches an **integrated arcane philosophy**, encouraging students to see all magic as part of a greater weave.
- **Necromancy** is taught openly, as are more subtle magics — but the dangers of certain spells are downplayed or omitted.
- **Forgotten Magic** is subtly embedded in advanced curriculum — *especially in artifact studies and higher necromantic theory*.  
Students are unaware of its origins and true risks.
- Headmaster [[High Arcanist Verrath]] personally influences this creeping corruption.

---

## ⚖️ Political Role

- The Arcanum holds considerable **political weight** in [[Caer Hollow]] and beyond.
- Many nobles view it as both a badge of prestige and a quiet threat.
- The **Crownwatch** respects but monitors the Academy closely.
- Graduates often serve in royal or noble courts as arcane advisors.

---

## 🛡️ Security

- The Academy is heavily **guarded** — both magically and physically.
- The presence of **constructs**, elite mages, and loyal soldiers makes intrusion or public unrest unlikely.
- Its elite status grants it some **legal autonomy** within Caer Hollow.

---

## 🎭 Reputation Among Common Folk

- Widely seen as an **arrogant institution** — aloof from the concerns of ordinary people.
- Respect for its graduates is genuine, but mixed with resentment of its elitism.
- Recent **rumors** of strange lights, odd behavior, and shadowy incidents have begun circulating among lower circles — unnoticed (or ignored) by most of the Academy.

---

## 🧑‍🏫 Known Structure

### Leadership

- **[[High Arcanist Verrath]]** — Official Headmaster.  
Rarely present; resides primarily in the [[Cradle Tower]]. His influence shapes the Academy's slow descent.

### Council of Arcanists

Senior figures, each responsible for broad **subject domains** rather than strict schools:

- **Dean of Arcane Theory** — *Master Feloren Drax*  
Oversees fundamental arcane studies and integration of disciplines.

- **Dean of Transmutation & Artifice** — *Mistress Elira Morn*  
Expert in the physical manipulation of reality and magical crafting.

- **Dean of Enchantment & Illusion** — *Master Kelveth Ravos*  
Subtle manipulator; suspected of aiding some of Verrath's goals.

- **Dean of Life & Death** — *Mistress Naevara Linethil*  
Necromancy expert — *possibly concerned about recent trends*; hard to access.

- **Dean of Divination & Historical Magic** — *Master Ithon Relos*  
Quiet, reserved — may secretly aid students seeking forbidden truths.

### Administration & Public Access

- **Magister Alren Vos** — Academy Administrator / Public Liaison.  
Handles admissions, visitors, and non-student inquiries.  
Kyron will likely have to deal with him to gain access to Academy resources.

---

## 🕵️ Subtle Resistance

- At least one member of the Council — likely **Naevara Linethil** or **Ithon Relos** — harbors private concerns about the Academy’s direction.
- No formal Resistance cell within the Academy, but **concerned individuals** may aid careful students discreetly.

---

## 🎭 Player Interaction Hooks

- **Samson** — openly part of the Academy; his thesis will draw him deeper into forbidden knowledge.
- **Kyron** — outsider; will face political and social barriers if seeking information.
- PCs may learn that the **Council is not unified** — potential for alliances or manipulation.
- The Arcanum's **library and vaults** hold keys to understanding Forgotten Magic and the Blackening.
