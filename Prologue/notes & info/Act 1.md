#### 2. Initial Defense Against the Undead

**Event:**

- As the sun begins to set, a chilling howl pierces the air, and undead creatures burst from the edge of [[Darkwood Forest]], attacking the villagers.

**Enemies:**

- 4 [[Skeleton|Skeleton Swordsman]]
- 2 [[Skeleton|Skeleton Bowman]]
- 1 [[Zombie Brute]]

**Combat:**

- The characters must quickly band together to defend the villagers and repel the undead attackers.
- **Objective:** Protect as many villagers as possible while defeating the undead attackers.
- **Tactics:** The skeletons attack villagers and adventurers alike, aiming to cause chaos. The Zombie Brute targets groups of villagers or adventurers, using its brute strength to smash through defenses.

**Aftermath:**

- Villagers express their gratitude to the adventurers for their bravery.

#### 3. The Village Elder's Request

**Key NPC: [[Elara]], the Village Elder**

- **Appearance:** Elara is a slender woman in her late 60s with long, silver hair tied back in a braid, deep blue eyes, and weathered skin. She wears a dark green cloak and a silver pendant shaped like a leaf.

**Interaction:**

- [[Elara]] approaches the group, thanking them for their help and explaining the dire situation.
- She details the recent disappearances and sightings of undead, emphasizing the urgency of the problem.
- [[Elara]] asks the adventurers to investigate key locations for more clues about the source of the undead threat.
- [[Missing people]]

#### 4. Investigations at Key Locations

**1. The Tavern: "The Weary Traveler"**



- **Description:** A sturdy building with a faded wooden sign. Inside, the tavern is dimly lit, with a low ceiling and a cozy fireplace.
- **NPCs:** Garret, the tavern owner, a burly, bearded man. Various villagers.
- **Information:** Villagers share rumors and personal accounts of the disappearances, mentioning shadowy figures and strange noises at night. Garret can provide information about the missing people and their last known whereabouts.
- **PUZZLE**: In the tavern, a villager mentions an old tale: "They say the ancients revered the owl for wisdom, the serpent for cunning, the stag for strength, and the raven for foresight."

**2. The Elder's House**

- **Description:** A slightly larger, well-maintained house with a small garden.
- **Contents:** The interior is cluttered with books, maps, and trinkets. A large wooden table is covered with documents.
- **Information:** [[Elara]] provides detailed maps of the village and surrounding area, records of previous incidents, and clues about the necromantic symbols found near the graveyard and the missing villager’s home.
- **PUZZLE**: Tapestry with owl, serpent, stag, raven

**3. The Graveyard**

- **Description:** Located on the outskirts, surrounded by a low stone wall. Overgrown with leaning tombstones and disturbed graves.
- **Investigation:** Players find tracks leading into [[Darkwood Forest]] and strange symbols scrawled on some tombstones (Arcana check to identify as necromantic). Freshly disturbed graves with signs of bodies being removed.
- **PUZZLE**: Gravestones with owl, serpent, stag, raven

**4. The Missing Villager's Home**

- **Description:** Small, dilapidated house at the edge of the village. Door ajar, windows dark.
- **Investigation:** Inside, the house is in disarray with overturned furniture and personal belongings scattered. Strange symbols on the walls (Arcana check to recognize as necromantic). Signs of a struggle, suggesting the villager was taken by force.
- **PUZZLE**: owl, serpent, stag, raven scratches into the walls

#### 5. Transition to Act 2

**Guidance:**

- Based on the clues gathered from the investigations, [[Elara]] suggests the adventurers follow the tracks and clues into [[Darkwood Forest]].
- She provides them with supplies and any additional information they might need for their journey.
