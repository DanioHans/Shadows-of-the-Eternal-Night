

_(Post-Duskwatch → The Blackening)_

## Arc Overview

After the events in **Duskwatch**, the party returns to **Caer Hollow** as the only people who have directly survived and resolved a magical anomaly. Their actions grant them a modest reputation and make them a natural choice for handling strange disturbances.

Unknown to them, **High Arcanist Verrath** is secretly constructing a **Weave Accumulation Lattice** beneath the city. This arcane structure slowly siphons ambient magical energy across Caer Hollow in preparation for a catastrophic ritual: **The Blackening**.

The ritual’s goal is not simply magical destruction, but to **tear open a stable breach to the Shadowfell**, allowing a large-scale undead incursion into the city.

The magical energy drawn into the lattice destabilizes the local Weave, producing **increasingly frequent arcane anomalies** throughout the city.

These incidents form the backbone of sessions 12–20.

---

# Hidden Structure (DM Knowledge)

## Verrath’s Goal

Verrath is attempting to create the **Blackening**, a controlled planar rupture that opens a large gate to the **Shadowfell**.

His motivations:

- Gain authority through crisis
    
- Demonstrate the necessity of centralized arcane control
    
- Prove that the realm’s current leadership is too cautious
    

He believes the event can be **contained and weaponized**.

He is wrong.

---

## The Weave Accumulation Lattice

Hidden anchors beneath Caer Hollow siphon small amounts of magical energy from:

- Academy experiments
    
- Enchanted infrastructure
    
- Magical creatures
    
- Spellcasting activity
    
- Ley convergence beneath the city
    

The anchors distribute strain unevenly through the city’s magical environment.

This produces the **arcane anomalies** encountered during the arc.

The lattice remains hidden unless the players discover a pattern in the disturbances.

---

# Role of Archivist Felwin

**Elira Felwin**, keeper of the Deep Index, quietly begins documenting the anomalies.

Her role:

- Observes patterns the authorities ignore
    
- Requests descriptions and samples from the party
    
- Maintains a **private codex of irregular magical incidents**
    

Over time she notices that the disturbances form a **geometric pattern across the city**.

This eventually reveals the presence of the lattice.

Felwin acts as:

- Knowledge gatekeeper
    
- Slow investigative ally
    
- Potential whistleblower regarding changes within the Arcanum
    

---

# Public Situation in Caer Hollow

Following Duskwatch:

### Reputation of the Party

- Known as the group who survived the cursed town
    
- Some citizens admire them
    
- Others remain wary due to unusual races within the group
    

Their reputation allows them access to places that ordinary adventurers would not receive.

---

### Growing Political Tension

Caer Hollow begins to fracture socially.

Common viewpoints emerge:

**Anti-Arcanum factions**

- Claim the academy caused Duskwatch
    
- Protest magical experimentation
    

**Pro-Arcanum scholars**

- Argue the Crown restricts necessary research
    

**Crown loyalists**

- Demand stronger control and security
    

These tensions occasionally erupt into protests, riots, or political confrontations.

---

# Arc Progression Structure

Each session should contain **one primary incident** tied to rising magical instability.

Not every incident must involve combat.

Some may be political, investigative, or social.

The goal is to slowly raise the **Anomaly Intensity** across the city.

---

# Anomaly Intensity Track (DM Tool)

1 — Minor magical oddities  
2 — Localized magical disturbances  
3 — Dangerous arcane instability  
4 — Structural magical breakdown  
5 — The Blackening

Each session should increase this level gradually.
Mix in anomalies of lower intensity to fill out a session

---

# Session Progression Outline

## Session 12

Return to Caer Hollow.

Key elements:

- Debrief with Crownwatch
    
- Deliver moonshade moss to local apothecary
    
- First minor magical disturbance
    

Felwin becomes interested in the anomaly.

---

## Session 13

Arcane disturbance involving enchanted infrastructure.

Possible examples:

- magical streetlights malfunction
    
- animated tools attacking workers
    
- familiar animals behaving violently
    

Felwin begins recording incidents.

---

## Session 14

Social tension increases.

Possible events:

- anti-Arcanum protests
    
- magical accidents during public demonstrations
    
- the party mediates a conflict between factions
    

---

## Session 15

Academy incident.

Example:

- spell surge in lecture hall
    
- unstable magical experiment
    
- minor planar instability
    

Felwin begins suspecting a pattern.

---

## Session 16

Creature incident.

Examples:

- magical beasts agitated
    
- sewer creature encounter
    
- escaped bound elemental
    

Anomaly intensity noticeably rising.

---

## Session 17

Felwin reveals the **pattern**.

She presents a map showing anomaly locations forming a **lattice pattern across Caer Hollow**.

The party begins suspecting deliberate manipulation.

---

## Session 18

Investigation into one anchor location.

Possible discoveries:

- arcane machinery
    
- siphoning runes
    
- signs of recent installation
    

Evidence hints toward the Arcanum.

---

## Session 19

Energy levels become unstable across the city.

Examples:

- magical items failing
    
- spells behaving unpredictably
    
- localized arcane storms
    

The city becomes tense and fearful.

---

## Session 20 — The Blackening

The ritual completes.

A massive planar rupture opens to the **Shadowfell**.

Undead forces begin pouring into Caer Hollow.

The party cannot stop the invasion.

The session focuses on:

- evacuation
    
- saving civilians
    
- surviving the collapse of the city
    

Caer Hollow is permanently lost.

This event marks the transition to **Act II: The Shadowfell Campaign**.

---

# Key Themes of the Arc

- Institutional complacency
    
- Controlled knowledge vs hidden truth
    
- Magical progress vs catastrophic risk
    
- The danger of believing disasters can be contained
    

---

# Campaign Transition

The Blackening forces the party to abandon Caer Hollow.

Survivors flee.

The party’s next objective becomes:

**Entering the Shadowfell to confront the source of the invasion and prevent its spread across the realm.**
