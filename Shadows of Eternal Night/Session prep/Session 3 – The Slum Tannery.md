## 🕳️ Tannery Hideout (Mini-Dungeon)

[[Jespin’s Hideout – The Tannery Dungeon]]
A brief 3-room space below a ruined tannery in Smokestack Row.

- **Entrance Hatch**: Locked (DC 14), or breakable. Trapped (optional acid trap).
- **Storage Room**: Basilisk crate remnants, leather goods, alchemy refuse.
- **Main Room**: Jespin packing supplies. Will **try to talk/bribe first**. Has an escape route (dead end).

🧠 No major puzzle or boss fight unless the party escalates.
💬 Focus is on **interrogation or capture**.

---

## 🗣️ Jespin Wrent – Conversation

[[Jespin Wrent|Jespin]] is slick, fast-talking, and cornered. He confesses partial truth:

> “I didn’t know it was a full basilisk! Got it off a contact passing through from Duskwatch.  
Strange place lately. Folk keep indoors, muttering about ‘shadows in the wheat’ or stars going dark.”

🧅 Teasers (half-truths):
- **“Strange how quiet it gets at night up there.”**
- **“Folk move like they’re not really... there.”**
- **“Saw a child stand still for hours, just humming to herself.”**
- "I'm just a trader, not a killer. Someone paid good coin for exotic beasts."

⏲️ *Keep the scene short but full of hooks and red herrings.*

## 🧭 Hook – Next Mission: **Duskwatch**

Reno or Felwin brings news:

> “A courier from the Arcanum branch at Duskwatch missed their report.  
Locals have gone silent. Some say the place is cursed.  
The Council would like eyes on the situation. Discreetly.”

Jespin’s notes also reference Duskwatch as the origin of his latest “goods.”  
There’s uncertainty if it’s smuggler drama or something deeper.
