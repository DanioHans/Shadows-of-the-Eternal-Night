## Leads to Seed During Travel

- Rumors: “faces in the fog,” “voices calling in dreams.”  
- Evidence of missing scouts (tokens, blood, broken gear).  
- Abandoned shipment crates or ledgers.  
- Optional side hook: fleeing villagers turned bandits.
