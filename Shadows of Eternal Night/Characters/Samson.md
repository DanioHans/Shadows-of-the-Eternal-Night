> A brilliant but socially awkward scholar whose obsession with arcane knowledge may lead him down dangerous paths.

---

## 🎭 Backstory Summary

- **Half-Elf wizard** from a scholarly background.
- Currently a **student at the Caer Hollow Academy**, specializing in **Necromancy**.
- Working on a **thesis about rare and powerful artifacts** — has already encountered **hints of Forgotten Magic**.
- Sees **necromancy** as an academic discipline, not inherently evil.
- Treats the undead as **subjects of study**, not servants or tools (yet).
- Unknown to Samson, his curiosity will soon draw him toward forces far greater than he understands.

---

## 🕊️ Personality / RP Notes

- **Socially awkward**, more comfortable with books than people.
- Views magic through a lens of **intellectual curiosity**, often missing moral implications.
- Not evil, but highly susceptible to corruption if power or knowledge is dangled before him.
- May become increasingly **isolated** if pushed toward darker magic.
- Could develop a **mentor complex** with younger or less experienced party members.

---

## 🗺️ Reason to be in Caer Hollow

- Resident of the **Caer Hollow Academy**.
- Currently conducting **artifact research** in the capital.
- Possible hooks:
  - Has recently come across references to the **Book of Eternity**.
  - In contact with **faculty or hidden circles** discussing Forgotten Magic.
  - Assigned a "field research" project that will draw him into the coming events.

---

## 🧠 Mechanics & Balance

- Wizard (Necromancy), Level 4.
- Strong INT-based skill monkey — **Arcana**, **History**, **Religion**, **Investigation**.
- Solid defensive tools via **Grim Harvest**.
- Combat style: Defensive caster with **Chill Touch** and utility.
- No standout mobility — depends on clever positioning and spell use.

---

## 🤝 NPC Reactions

- Generally respected by **Academy scholars**.
- **Clerics and paladins** will be wary of his studies — necromancy is already a controversial school.
- His curiosity will likely draw **dangerous attention** once the Blackening begins.
- Could be **manipulated by higher forces** if tempted with forbidden knowledge.

---

## 🎭 Roleplay Hooks

- His **thesis** can serve as a natural way to introduce **Book of Eternity lore**.
- Possible **slow descent** toward Forgotten Magic — driven by curiosity, not malice.
- May face inner conflict over **how far to go** for knowledge.
- Could become a **pivotal player in the corruption mechanic** — tipping toward darkness or resisting it.
- Excellent RP potential in **interactions with morally rigid PCs** (paladins, clerics).
