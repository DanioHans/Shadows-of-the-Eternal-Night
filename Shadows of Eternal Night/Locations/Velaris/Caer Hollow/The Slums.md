# The Slums

**Location**: [[Caer Hollow]] — Outer South District  
**District Type**: Deprived residential zone  
**Known For**: Poverty, crime, unlicensed magic, missing persons

---

## Overview

Caer Hollow’s slums lie in the shadow of its grandeur — a **sprawling, broken labyrinth** of collapsed homes, stitched-together shacks, and the burnt remains of older city layers. It is the city’s **most dangerous quarter**, plagued by illness, poverty, and frequent unrest.

Despite their condition, the slums are **not lifeless** — families live here, criminal networks operate here, and secrets often fester where no guards dare to look.

---

## Key Features

### ☠️ Collapsed Quarters  
- Entire streets of **sunken or half-ruined buildings**.
- Some areas built over **buried older architecture**, with **underground access** points.
- Fires and floods are **common hazards**.

### 🏚️ Makeshift Dwellings  
- Homes made of scavenged timber, rusted metal, and tented cloth.
- Little privacy — most sleep in communal shelters or guarded compounds.
- **Wells and food lines** are controlled by local gangs or "volunteer forces."

### 🔥 Open Fire Lanes  
- Large **firepits** for warmth or cooking.
- Many disputes are settled around these in **brutal street justice** or ritualized fights.
- Occasionally used for **illegal monster fights** or blood duels.

---

## Security

- **No official Crownwatch or City Guard presence**.
- Locals follow the rule of whoever has power that week — usually **gangs or enforcer cells**.
- Outsiders are seen with suspicion or as easy prey unless they display strength or local ties.

---

## Known Dangers

- **The Mawrats** – A small gang of thugs who operate a protection racket and sell minor cursed trinkets.
- **Greyspite Cult** – Rumored to worship something “beneath the city.” Graffiti and whispered sermons grow more frequent.
- **Unlicensed Mages** – Sorcerers and warlocks sometimes hide here, experimenting without oversight.

---

## Notable Locations

- **Jespin Wrent’s Retreat** – A decrepit workshop where the [[Beast Smuggler]] lays low. Located behind a crumbling bakery façade.
- **The Severed Mug** – The only drinking house in the area. No questions asked. Run by a mute ex-knight.
- **The Breach** – A collapsed section of the slums where the **old city sewer system is exposed** — used for trafficking and cult activity.
- **The Ash Yard** – A scorched lot where several homes burned down. Nothing grows here. Local children claim they see “figures” at night.

---

## Culture & Vibe

- **Wary, desperate, tight-lipped**.
- Locals do not volunteer information — coin, power, or protection must be offered.
- Rumors fly fast: illness, sightings, people vanishing, strangers with too-clean boots.
- The Slums are **avoided by most of Caer Hollow**, and barely mentioned in palace policy.

---

## Trade & Economy

- Black market alchemical components.
- “Repurposed” goods from other districts.
- Minor smuggled relics or salvaged Verdant Wastes scrap.
- Dirty coin runs clean after a few pockets — many Eastveil stalls buy from here indirectly.

---

## Secrets and Intrigue

- Several **underground tunnels** lead directly toward the High Ring — some believe these were escape paths or servant channels in ancient times.
- Whispers claim **certain nobles fund criminal activity** here to keep parts of the city destabilized.
- A **mysterious healer** known only as “Mother Grey” offers aid for strange prices — memories, promises, or names.
