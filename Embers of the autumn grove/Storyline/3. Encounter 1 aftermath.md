### **Summary of Activities in the Grove**

#### **Tasks for Players**

1. **Extinguishing Fires (Skill Challenge)**
    
    - **Skill Checks:** _Survival, Nature (DC 12)_ or **creative solutions** like water spells.
    - **Result:** Reduces the corruption’s spread and earns the Wardens’ gratitude.
2. **Assisting the Verdant Wardens**
    
    - **Skill Checks:** _Medicine (DC 12)_ to stabilize injured Wardens or attack **weakened corrupted roots** (AC 12, HP 10).
    - **Result:** Wardens regain strength and share helpful information.
3. **Inspecting Fallen Enemies**
    
    - **Skill Checks:** _Arcana, Nature (DC 13)_ or _Medicine, Perception (DC 12)_.
    - **Result:** Learn about Emberblight’s magical nature and its connection to Vaelthir.
4. **Restoring a Small Healing Tree (Optional)**
    
    - **Skill Checks:** _Nature, Arcana (DC 12)_ or use **healing/restorative magic**.
    - **Result:** Revive a **healing tree zone** for minor healing in the next encounter.

---

### **Ally Phrases to Encourage Roleplay**

#### **Nyssa (Guide)**

- _“You fought bravely, but there is still so much to do. Who are you, to stand against such a foe?”_
- _“The corruption runs deep. Every small act of balance you bring matters. What drives you to fight?”_
- _“Vaelthir is not lost entirely... I hope. Do you believe such a protector can be saved?”_

#### **Verdant Wardens**

- _“You fight with skill and heart. Few have stood where you now stand. What brings you to this place?”_
- _“This corruption spreads faster than we can hold it back. If you have strength to give, we will need it.”_
- _“The fire that twists these beings is not natural—it comes from Vaelthir’s core. Be ready for what lies ahead.”_
- _“The battle is not over. What do you need to prepare for the next fight?”_

#### **Skill Checks**

- **Arcana (DC 13):**
    
    - Learn about **Emberblight’s magical nature**, amplifying strength but eroding will.
    - _Hint:_ _“Calm or restorative energies might weaken it.”_
- **Nature (DC 12):**
    
    - Discover Vaelthir’s bond to **autumn and renewal**, and how the **corrupted roots** sustain Emberblight.
    - _Hint:_ _“Breaking his connection to the roots might disrupt the fire.”_
- **Insight (DC 14):**
    
    - Detect Nyssa’s and the Wardens’ conflicting opinions about Vaelthir’s fate.
    - _Hint:_ _“Neither choice may be wholly right—or wrong.”_
- **Religion (DC 12) (Optional):**
    
    - Uncover Emberblight’s origins as a **balance-shifting force**, now uncontrollable.
    - _Hint:_ _“Vaelthir’s suffering fuels the corruption, not his strength.”_
- **Perception (DC 10):**
    
    - Notice the **fallen Revenants’ hesitation** during their corruption, hinting that Vaelthir may still resist.
    - _Hint:_ _“Vaelthir may yet fight the Emberblight within, but his actions could grow more erratic.”_

---

#### **How to Introduce These Checks**

1. **During Post-Battle Exploration**
    
    - When players investigate the battlefield, offer opportunities to make checks:
        - **Inspect Fallen Enemies**: Use _Arcana, Nature, or Perception_ to examine the Revenants or Branchwych for traces of corruption.
        - **Investigate the Grove**: Use _Nature or Religion_ to understand the changes in the environment.
    
    Example Prompt:
    
    - _“The Revenants’ bark is scorched and cracked, but something about the patterns feels unnatural. What do you want to do?”_
2. **NPC Conversations**
    
    - Encourage players to ask Nyssa or the Wardens about Vaelthir or Emberblight.
    - Use their **responses** to hint at the cleansing vs. destruction decision:
        - Nyssa: _“Cycles of renewal are not easy to restore, but they are possible.”_
        - Verdant Wardens: _“Corruption like this leaves no room for doubt. Only its end will save us.”_
3. **Environmental Cues**
    
    - As the players explore the grove, describe subtle signs of Emberblight’s spread, like glowing roots or unnatural heat.
    - Example: _“The roots pulse faintly with fiery embers, as though feeding on the land’s energy. Do you want to investigate?”_
4. **Skill Challenges**
    
    - If players **aren’t proactive**, Nyssa or a Warden could point them toward a specific discovery:
        - Nyssa: _“The fire feeds on something deeper than wood and ash. Perhaps you can uncover its true source.”_
