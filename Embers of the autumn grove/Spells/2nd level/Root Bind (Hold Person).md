#### **Rootbind** _(Custom, Reflavored Hold Person)_

- **Casting Time:** 1 action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Effect:** **Roots burst from the ground**, restraining a humanoid or Fey creature.
- **Target makes a Wisdom saving throw (DC 14).**
- **On Fail:** The target is **restrained** for up to 1 minute.
- **At the end of each turn, the target can repeat the save.**
