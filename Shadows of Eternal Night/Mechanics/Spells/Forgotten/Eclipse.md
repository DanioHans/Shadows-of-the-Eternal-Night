**Level**: Cantrip | **School**: Illusion  
**Casting Time**: 1 Action  
**Range**: 30 feet  
**Duration**: 1 minute  
**Components**: V, S

**Effect**: Choose a point within range. For the duration, **non-magical light sources** (e.g. torches, lanterns) within a 20-foot radius are extinguished or dimmed to a faint glow.

- **Magical light** (2nd level or lower) is reduced to **dim light** for the duration.
- Light sources relight normally after the spell ends.

**Flavor**: Shadows lengthen, and flames sputter as if suffocating. Cold flickers replace warmth. The caster’s fingertips briefly glow black.
